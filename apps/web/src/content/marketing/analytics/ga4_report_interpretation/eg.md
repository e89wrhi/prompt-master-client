# Examples: GA4 Report Interpretation

## 1. Monthly Traffic Acquisition Audit

```text
Act as a Senior Digital Analyst. Review and interpret the following Traffic Acquisition data:

### Exported Data:
Source/Medium, Sessions, Engagement Rate, Key Events, Revenue
google / organic, 5000, 65%, 200, $5000
fb / cpc, 2500, 45%, 50, $1200
(direct) / (none), 1500, 60%, 80, $2000

### Interpretation Requirements:
1. **The Pulse Summary**: Summary of the last 30 days.
2. **Channel Attribution**: Which source brings the highest ROI?
3. **Engagement Analysis**: Why is FB CPC's engagement rate low?
4. **The "Session vs. User" Shift**: Comparison of new vs. returning revenue.
5. **Actionable Recommendations**: Adjust budget between Organic and Paid.

### Output Constraints:
- Precise and Professional tone.
```

## 2. eCommerce Conversion Funnel Analysis

```text
Act as a GA4 Expert. Review the following Event Report data:

### Data:
Event_Name, Count, Total_Users
session_start, 10000, 8000
view_item, 5000, 4000
add_to_cart, 500, 450
begin_checkout, 200, 180
purchase, 50, 45

### Interpretation Requirements:
- Identify the highest abandonment rate page.
- Focus on the drop-off between "view_item" and "add_to_cart."
- Provide 3 tactical changes.

### Output Constraints:
- Headings and bullet points.
```
