# Template: Professional Conversion Rate Optimization (CRO)

Use this template to identify friction points and psychological barriers on your website. It is designed to generate rigorous A/B testing hypotheses based on heuristic analysis, aiming to convert more visitors into leads or customers without increasing traffic spend.

## 📋 Prompt Template

```text
Act as a Senior conversion Rate Optimization (CRO) Specialist. Conduct a heuristic analysis and provide optimization ideas for [PAGE_TYPE/URL].

### Page Context:
[INSERT_DESCRIPTION_OF_USER_JOURNEY_AND_CURRENT_CONVERSION_RATE]

### Analysis Requirements:
1. **Friction Point Identification**: Pinpoint 3 specific elements that are likely causing "Cognitive Load" or [USER_ANXIETY] (e.g., long forms, unclear CTAs).
2. **The "Value Proposition" Audit**: Evaluate if the [PRIMARY_HEADLINE] clearly answers the user's question: "What is in it for me?"
3. **Psychological Triggers**: Propose 2 ways to implement [SOCIAL_PROOF] or [SCARCITY] without being intrusive.
4. **The A/B Test Hypotheses**: Provide 3 prioritized hypotheses in the "If... Then... Because..." format.
5. **Mobile-Specific Fixes**: Identify 2 optimizations specifically for the mobile viewport (e.g., thumb-zone CTAs).

### Output Constraints:
- Present the Hypotheses in a formatted table with "Potential Impact" (High/Med/Low).
- Use an Analytical, Bold, and Objective tone.
- Avoid vague advice; provide specific suggestions for [COLOR_CONTRAST], [COPY_LENGTH], and [PLACEMENT].
```

## 🧩 Variables to Fill Out

- `[PAGE_TYPE/URL]` - e.g., Pricing Page, Checkout, SaaS Landing Page, Newsletter Signup.
- `[INSERT_DESCRIPTION]` - What is the goal of the page? What is the current conversion rate?
- `[USER_ANXIETY]` - e.g., Fear of being spammed, confusion about pricing, credit card requirement.
- `[PRIMARY_HEADLINE]` - The main text the user sees first.
- `[SOCIAL_PROOF]` - e.g., Testimonials, trust badges, user counts.

## 💡 Pro-Tips

- **The Goldilocks Zone of Choice:** Too many options lead to "Analysis Paralysis." Ask the AI to "Audit the page for paradox of choice and remove redundant CTAs."
- **One Page, One Goal:** A landing page should have exactly one desired action. If the page has 5 goals, tell the AI to "Prioritize the primary conversion goal and suggest what to demote."
- **LIFT Model Integration:** For advanced analysis, ask the AI to "Apply the WiderFunnel LIFT Model (Relevance, Clarity, Urgency, etc.) to the page."
