# Examples: UTM Tagging Strategy

## 1. Multi-Channel Product Launch

```text
Act as a Marketing Operations Strategist. Design a UTM Tagging strategy for our "Q4_Holiday_Sale" campaign.

### Campaign Context:
- **Channels**: FB Ads, Email Newsletter, Influencer Bio Links.
- **Goal**: Online Sales.

### Strategy Requirements:
1. **Naming**: Lowercase only, underscores for spaces.
2. **UTM Map**: Generate URLs for:
   - FB Ad 1 (Video).
   - Monthly Newsletter.
   - Influencer 'SarahTech'.
3. **Consistency Audit**: Rules on using 'cpc' vs 'paid_social'.

### Output Constraints:
- Markdown Table format.
- Standardized tone.
```

## 2. Low-Touch Social Media Promotion

```text
Act as a Marketing Operations Strategist. Design a simple UTM system for 5 different Social Media Bio links.

### Campaign Context:
- Channels: Twitter, LinkedIn, Instagram, TikTok.
- Goal: Portfolio visits.

### Strategy Requirements:
- Source: One per platform.
- Medium: 'bio_link'.
- Campaign: 'evergreen_traffic'.

### Output Constraints:
- Precise examples for every link.
```
