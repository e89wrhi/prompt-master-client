# Examples: Campaign Strategy & Ideation

## 1. Sustainable Performance Wear (Brand: "VeloEarth")

```text
Act as a Creative Director and Strategic Campaign Planner. Brainstorm 3 distinct campaign concepts for "VeloEarth," a brand of high-performance cycling shorts made entirely from recycled ocean plastic.

### Campaign Context:
- Product: Lightweight, moisture-wicking bib shorts.
- Mission: To show that "Recycled" doesn't mean "Low Quality."
- Competitors: Rapha, Castelli (High price, traditional materials).

### Ideation Requirements:
1. **The "Big Idea"**: "Armor for the Future" vs "Waste-free Speed."
2. **The Hook Library**: Curiosity, Controversial, and Outcome hooks.
3. **Multi-Channel Manifestation**: Instagram Reels and Email.
4. **Primary Emotional Trigger**: Altruism mixed with Pride.

### Output Constraints:
- Use a Creative and Energetic tone.
- Avoid clichés.
```

## 2. Personal Finance App for Gen Z (App: "PennyWise")

```text
Act as a Strategic Campaign Planner. Brainstorm 3 distinct campaign concepts for "PennyWise," an app that rounds up your change into crypto or gold.

### Campaign Context:
- Target: 18-24 year olds who find investing "boring" or "too complicated."
- Mission: Make wealth-building invisible and automatic.

### Ideation Requirements:
- Concepts: "Investing for People who Hate Investing," "The Invisible Fortune," and "Rich by Accident."
- Hooks: Focus on FOMO and Curiosity.

### Output Constraints:
- Persuasive tone.
```
