# Template: Campaign Strategy & Ideation

Use this template to generate high-concept, multi-channel marketing campaign ideas. it is designed to move beyond "generic promotion" by defining a core "Big Idea" that connects emotional triggers with practical product benefits.

## 📋 Prompt Template

```text
Act as a Creative Director and Strategic Campaign Planner. Brainstorm 3 distinct campaign concepts for [BRAND/PRODUCT] targeting [AUDIENCE].

### Campaign Context:
[INSERT_PRODUCT_SPECS_AND_BRAND_MISSION]

### Ideation Requirements:
1. **The "Big Idea"**: For each concept, provide a 1-sentence "Theme" that anchors the campaign (e.g., "The Rebellion against [PAIN_POINT]").
2. **The Hook Library**: For each concept, draft 3 different types of hooks:
   - **Curiosity Hook**: A question or statement that forces the user to click.
   - **Controversial Hook**: A "hot take" related to the industry.
   - **Outcome Hook**: A vivid description of the post-solution world.
3. **Multi-Channel Manifestation**: Describe how this campaign looks on [CHANNEL_1] (visual) and [CHANNEL_2] (text).
4. **Primary Emotional Trigger**: Identify if the campaign relies on [FEAR/FOMO], [PRIDE], [GREED], or [ALTRUISM].
5. **Key Performance Indicator (KPI)**: Suggest the most important metric to track success for this specific idea.

### Output Constraints:
- Use a Creative, Energetic, and Persuasive tone.
- Present each campaign concept in a structured section with its own header.
- Avoid clichés; focus on "pattern-interrupt" concepts that stand out in a crowded feed.
```

## 🧩 Variables to Fill Out

- `[BRAND/PRODUCT]` - e.g., A sustainable coffee brand, a new personal finance app.
- `[AUDIENCE]` - e.g., Eco-conscious Gen Z, High-net-worth retirees, Remote developers.
- `[INSERT_PRODUCT_SPECS_AND_BRAND_MISSION]` - What makes the product unique?
- `[CHANNEL_1/2]` - e.g., Instagram reels, Google search ads, Email, Physical billboards.
- `[FEAR/FOMO/etc]` - The psychological lever you want to pull.

## 💡 Pro-Tips

- **The Pattern Interrupt:** If you're selling a common product (like coffee), don't show people drinking coffee. Show the *experience* or a common frustration that coffee solves.
- **Micro-Targeting:** The broader the audience, the weaker the campaign. Ask the AI to "Select a hyper-specific sub-niche" for one of the three ideas.
- **The "So What?" Test:** If you read the campaign idea and think "So what?", tell the AI to "Increase the stakes" by making the problem more urgent.
