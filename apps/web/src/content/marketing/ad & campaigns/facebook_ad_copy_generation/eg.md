# Examples: Facebook & Instagram Ad Copy

## 1. Productivity App (B2B SaaS)

```text
Act as a Senior Direct Response Copywriter. Write 3 variations of Facebook Ad copy for "TaskFlow AI," an app that automates project management for creative agencies.

### Ad Context:
- **Target Audience**: Agency owners with 5-20 employees.
- **Core Benefit**: Saves 5 hours of manual status-checking per week.
- **The Offer**: 14-day free trial (no credit card).

### Writing Requirements:
1. **The Scroll-Stopper (Hook)**: One Question, One Unpopular Opinion, One Result.
2. **Body Copy**: One Short & Punchy, One Story-Driven.
3. **Headline**: 5 variations.
4. **CTA**: Benefit-driven.
5. **Creative Direction**: Visual pairing.

### Output Constraints:
- Punchy and Direct tone.
- Strategic emoji use.
```

## 2. High-End Ergonomic Desk Chair (eCommerce)

```text
Act as a Senior Direct Response Copywriter. Write 3 variations for "The Zenith Chair."

### Ad Context:
- **Target Audience**: Remote workers with chronic back pain.
- **Core Benefit**: Zero-gravity suspension system.
- **The Offer**: 20% off for first-time buyers + Free Shipping.

### Writing Requirements:
- Use empathy and storytelling about the "end-of-day back pain."
- Headlines focusing on "Immediate Relief."

### Output Constraints:
- Warm and Empathetic tone.
```
