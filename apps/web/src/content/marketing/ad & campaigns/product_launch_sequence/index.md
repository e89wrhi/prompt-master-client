# Template: Strategic Product Launch Sequence

Use this template to design a high-momentum "orchestrated" launch for a new product, feature, or service. It covers the full arc from the initial "Tease" to the "Launch Day" peak, and finally the "Urgency/Close" phase to maximize initial sales or signups.

## 📋 Prompt Template

```text
Act as a Senior Product Marketing Manager (PMM). Design a [DURATION] Launch Sequence for [PRODUCT_NAME].

### Launch Context:
[INSERT_PRODUCT_DETAILS_AND_UNIQUE_SELLING_POINTS]

### Sequence Requirements:
1. **Phase 1: The Tease (Pre-Launch)**: Design 2 social media posts and 1 email aimed at building curiosity and managing a waitlist.
2. **Phase 2: The Logic (Launch Day)**: Draft the "Official Announcement" content. Focus heavily on [PRIMARY_PROBLEM_SOLVED] and the [LIMITED_TIME_LAUNCH_OFFER].
3. **Phase 3: The Proof (Mid-Launch)**: Write a "Social Proof" sequence (e.g., case studies, founder stories, or user testimonials) to overcome [TYPICAL_OBJECTION].
4. **Phase 4: The Scarcity (Final 48 Hours)**: Increase the tension with a "Doors Closing" or "Bonus Expiring" sequence.
5. **Cross-Channel Distribution**: For each phase, specify the role of [EMIAL], [SOCIAL_MEDIA], and [SMS/PUSH_NOTIFS].

### Output Constraints:
- Present the sequence as a Chronological Timeline (e.g., Day 1, Day 3, Day 7).
- Use an Energetic, Authoritative, and Momentum-Building tone.
- Conclude with a "Launch Checklist" of 5 critical assets that must be ready.
```

## 🧩 Variables to Fill Out

- `[DURATION]` - e.g., 7-day launch, 30-day "Summer of Features."
- `[PRODUCT_NAME]` - The name of the item.
- `[INSERT_PRODUCT_DETAILS]` - Specs, pricing, and why it's better.
- `[PRIMARY_PROBLEM_SOLVED]` - The single biggest pain point.
- `[LIMITED_TIME_LAUNCH_OFFER]` - e.g., 20% off launch week, Early access bonus.
- `[TYPICAL_OBJECTION]` - e.g., "It's too expensive," "I don't have time to learn a new tool."

## 💡 Pro-Tips

- **The "Waitlist" Advantage:** Building hype *before* you can buy is the secret to high launch numbers. Tell the AI to "Emphasize the 'Early Bird' list advantage."
- **Objection Handling is Mandatory:** Every launch has a "Resistance" phase. Ask the AI to "Identify the top 3 reasons people *won't* buy and address them in Phase 3."
- **Stack the Bonuses:** Instead of just a discount, suggest a "Stacked Bonus" (e.g., PDF guide, 1:1 onboarding) to increase the perceived value without lowering the price.
