# Examples: Product Launch Sequence

## 1. New Productivity App ("FocusFlow")

```text
Act as a Senior Product Marketing Manager. Design a 7-day Launch Sequence for "FocusFlow," an AI-native task planner.

### Launch Context:
- USP: Uses bio-rhythm data to tell you *when* to work on specific tasks.
- Problem: People are tired of generic to-do lists.
- Offer: 50% off "Life-Time" license for launch week only.

### Sequence Requirements:
1. **Phase 1: The Tease (Day 1-3)**: Curiosity-based social teasers.
2. **Phase 2: The Logic (Day 4 - Launch Day)**: Official announcement with the outcome video.
3. **Phase 3: The Proof (Day 5-6)**: Overcome the "Another planner?" objection.
4. **Phase 4: The Scarcity (Day 7)**: Final 24-hour countdown.

### Output Constraints:
- Chronological Timeline format.
- Energetic and Momentum-Building tone.
```

## 2. Niche eCommerce Brand (Sustainable Coffee)

```text
Act as a Senior PMM. Design a 14-day Launch Sequence for "RoastRecycle," coffee pods that are 100% compostable and grow wildflowers when buried.

### Launch Context:
- USP: Zero-waste coffee with a positive ecological impact. 
- Offer: Pack of 30 pods + a reusable wooden coffee tin for the first 500 orders.

### Sequence Requirements:
- Phase 1: Focus on the "Ocean Plastic" problem.
- Phase 2: Show the pods blooming into flowers in a time-lapse.
- Phase 4: Show the "Limited Edition Tin" stock remaining.

### Output Constraints:
- Authoritative tone.
```
