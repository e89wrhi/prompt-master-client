# Template: High-CTR Google Ad Headlines

Use this template to generate precise, keyword-optimized headlines for Google Search Ads. It is designed to maximize your Click-Through Rate (CTR) by strictly adhering to character limits while incorporating urgency, social proof, and direct benefits.

## 📋 Prompt Template

```text
Act as a Senior PPC (Pay-Per-Click) Specialist. Generate [AMOUNT] of "Responsive Search Ad" (RSA) Headlines for [PRODUCT/SERVICE].

### Search Context:
- **Primary Keywords**: [KEYWORD_1], [KEYWORD_2], [KEYWORD_3].
- **Main Goal**: [e.g., Lead Form, Direct Purchase, Call Now].
- **Core Benefit**: [THE_BIG_PROMISE].

### Headline Requirements (Strict 30 Character Limit):
Provide headlines categorized by the following strategies:
1. **Keyword-Matched**: Headlines that mirror the [PRIMARY_KEYWORDS] exactly.
2. **Action-Oriented**: Headlines starting with a strong verb (e.g., "Get...", "Buy...", "Save...").
3. **Social Proof & Authority**: Headlines featuring numbers or ratings (e.g., "4.9/5 Rating," "Over 10k Users").
4. **Urgency & Scarcity**: Headlines focusing on time-limited offers (e.g., "Sale Ends Midnight," "Limited Stock").
5. **Hyper-Specific Benefit**: Headlines focusing on a single [CORE_BENEFIT].

### Output Constraints:
- Every headline MUST be **30 characters or less** (including spaces). No exceptions.
- Provide a character count next to each headline for verification.
- Group the headlines by the categories mentioned above for easy A/B testing.
```

## 🧩 Variables to Fill Out

- `[AMOUNT]` - e.g., 15 headlines (standard for one RSA).
- `[PRODUCT/SERVICE]` - What is the specific offering?
- `[KEYWORD_1/2/3]` - The terms users are typing into Google.
- `[THE_BIG_PROMISE]` - The main reason they should click on you vs. the competitor.

## 💡 Pro-Tips

- **Character Limits are Law:** If an ad headline is 31 characters, Google will reject it or truncate it. The AI is usually good at this, but always double-check.
- **Mix the "Pinning" Strategy:** In Google Ads, you can "pin" headlines. Ask the AI to "Designate 2 headlines that *must* appear in Position 1 (Keyword-focused)."
- **Numbers Stand Out:** "50% Off" stands out more than "Half Price." Use digits whenever possible to save space and increase visibility.
