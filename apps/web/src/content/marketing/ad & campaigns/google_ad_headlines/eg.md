# Examples: Google Ad Headlines

## 1. Local Emergency Plumber

```text
Act as a Senior PPC Specialist. Generate 15 Responsive Search Ad Headlines for "RapidFlow Plumbing."

### Search Context:
- **Primary Keywords**: Emergency Plumber, Clogged Drain, Plumber Near Me.
- **Main Goal**: Get a phone call.
- **Core Benefit**: 30-minute response time in the NYC area.

### Headline Requirements (Strict 30 Character Limit):
- Categories: Keyword-Matched, Action-Oriented, Social Proof, Urgency, Benefit-Focused.

### Output Constraints:
- 30 characters or less per headline.
- Character count next to each.
```

## 2. High-Tech CRM Software

```text
Act as a Senior PPC Specialist. Generate 10 headlines for "Nexus CRM."

### Search Context:
- **Primary Keywords**: Best CRM 2024, Sales Automation Tool, CRM for Startups.
- **Main Goal**: Lead Form Signup.
- **Core Benefit**: The only CRM with built-in AI forecasting.

### Headline Requirements (Strict 30 Character Limit):
- Include headlines with social proof (e.g., "Used by 500+ Startups").
- Character count included.
```
