# Examples: Twitter Thread Hooks

## 1. Professional Story: "Scaling to $1M"

```text
Act as a Twitter Growth Ghostwriter. Generate 3 Thread Hooks for: "How I grew my SaaS to $1M ARR alone."

### Thread Context:
- **Achievement**: Reached $1M ARR with 0 employees and $0 in funding.
- **Target**: Solopreneurs and Indie Hackers.

### Hook Requirements:
1. **The Storyteller**: "1 year ago, I was broke. Today, my SaaS does $1M ARR. I have 0 employees and 0 VC funding. Here are the 5 brutal lessons I learned about scaling alone. 🧵"
2. **The List-Maker**: "Scaling a SaaS to $100k/mo doesn't require a team. It requires 7 specific 'Invisible Systems.' I used these to go from $0 -> $1M ARR in 14 months. Let's dive in 👇"
3. **The Contrarian**: "Most people say you need a 'Team' to scale. Most people are wrong. I hit $1M ARR as a solo founder by ignoring the standard advice. Here's exactly how. 🧵"

### Output Constraints:
- Under 280 characters.
- Authoritative and Fast-Paced tone.
```
