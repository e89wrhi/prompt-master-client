# Examples: Engaging Instagram Captions

## 1. Behind-the-Scenes (Small Business Owner)

```text
Act as a Senior Social Media Manager. Write 3 Instagram captions for a "Day in the Life" Reel for a local bakery owner.

### Post Context:
- Visuals: Fast-paced clips of mixing dough, icing cakes, opening doors at 5 AM, and drinking coffee.
- Topic: The early morning hustle.

### Writing Requirements:
1. **Hooks**: (e.g., "5 AM never looked so sweet," "The secret ingredient is... coffee.")
2. **Body**: Short section about the passion behind the bake.
3. **Link in Bio**: Bridge to the "Weekly Special" order link.
4. **Hashtags**: Niche: #nycbestbakery, Broad: #bakinglife.

### Output Constraints:
- Playful and Witty tone.
- 500 character limit.
```

## 2. Product Spotlight (Eco-Friendly Yoga Mat)

```text
Act as a Creative Copywriter. Write captions for a Product Carousel for a "Cork Yoga Mat."

### Post Context:
- Visuals: Slide 1: Close up of the texture. Slide 2: A yogi in a difficult pose (no slipping). Slide 3: Sustainable packaging.

### Writing Requirements:
- Focus on "Grip that gets better with sweat."
- Include engagement prompts: "Natural cork or synthetic poly—which do you prefer?"

### Output Constraints:
- Aesthetic and Minimalist tone.
```
