# Template: Engaging Instagram & Social Media Captions

Use this template to write social captions that stop the scroll and drive engagement. It is designed to balance visual storytelling with a clear text-based "Hook," a values-led "Body," and a friction-free call to action that directs users to your profile or website.

## 📋 Prompt Template

```text
Act as a Senior Social Media Manager and Creative Copywriter. Write [AMOUNT] Instagram captions for a [CONTENT_TYPE] about [TOPIC].

### Post Context:
[DESCRIBE_THE_IMAGE_OR_VIDEO_VISUALS]

### Writing Requirements:
1. **The Scroll-Stopper (The Hook)**: For each caption, provide 3 different hook options:
   - **The Question**: Immediately involving the user.
   - **The "Relatable" Statement**: Building empathy or "I feel seen" moments.
   - **The Outcome**: Highlighting the value of the post.
2. **The "Heart" (Body Copy)**: Draft a [LENGTH] section that tells the story or provides the tip. Use emojis strategically to break up text.
3. **The "Link in Bio" Bridge**: Write a smooth transition that guides the user to the [CALL_TO_ACTION].
4. **Hashtag Strategy**: Provide 10 relevant hashtags categorized by:
   - **Niche** (Low competition, high intent).
   - **Broad** (High visibility).
   - **Branded** (Your specific name/tag).

### Output Constraints:
- Use a [TONE] tone.
- Ensure the formatting is "Clean" (using line breaks for readability).
- Maximum of [CHAR_LIMIT] characters to ensure it doesn't feel like a blog post.
```

## 🧩 Variables to Fill Out

- `[AMOUNT]` - e.g., 3 variations.
- `[CONTENT_TYPE]` - e.g., Behind-the-scenes Reel, Product Carousel, Customer Testimonial.
- `[TOPIC]` - What is the post about?
- `[DESCRIBE_VISUALS]` - Describe the colors, people, or action in the photo/video.
- `[TONE]` - e.g., Witty/Playful, Educational/Helpful, Aesthetic/Minimalist.
- `[CHAR_LIMIT]` - e.g., 2,200 (max) but usually 300-500 is better for engagement.

## 💡 Pro-Tips

- **The "See More" Gap:** On Instagram, people only see the first two lines of your caption before the "See More" button. Your hook MUST finish within those two lines.
- **Engagement is Two-Way:** Always end a caption with a question for the comments (e.g., "Which one is your favorite?"). Ask the AI to "Suggest an engagement prompt tailored to the [TOPIC]."
- **Visual-Text Sync:** If your image is loud and colorful, keep the caption minimal. If the image is simple, the caption can be longer. Ask the AI to "Scale the caption length based on visual complexity."
