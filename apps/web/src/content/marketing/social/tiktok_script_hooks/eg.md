# Examples: Viral TikTok & Reels Hooks

## 1. 3 Productivity Tools for Entrepreneurs

```text
Act as a Viral Video Strategist. Generate 5 hooks for a TikTok about "3 AI tools to save you 10 hours a week."

### Video Context:
- **Target**: Solopreneurs.
- **Value**: High-speed utility.

### Hook Requirements:
1. **On-Screen Text**: "I quit my VA and replaced them with these 3 apps."
2. **Vocal**: "You're working 40 hours a week... but you only need to work 4."
3. **Visual**: Pointing to floating app icons.
4. **Payoff**: Reveal the 3rd tool at the 45-second mark.

### Output Constraints:
- Use Negative Hooks ("Stop wasting time").
- Energetic tone.
```

## 2. Cooking Tutorial: 15-Minute Meal

```text
Act as a Content Creator. Generate 3 "ASMR" hooks for a 15-minute healthy dinner recipe.

### Video Context:
- **Target**: Busy office workers.
- **Visuals**: Slicing vegetables, sizzling pans.

### Hook Requirements:
- Text: "The only 15-min dinner you'll ever need."
- Visual: Extreme close-up of the food hitting the hot pan.

### Output Constraints:
- Fast-Paced tone.
```
