# Template: Viral TikTok & Reels Script Hooks

Use this template to design the first 3 seconds of your short-form videos. In a world of infinite scrolling, the "Hook" is the only thing that prevents the user from swiping away. It is designed to combine visual cues, auditory triggers, and text-based curiosities to maximize watch time.

## 📋 Prompt Template

```text
Act as a Content Creator and Viral Video Strategist specializing in TikTok and Instagram Reels. Generate [AMOUNT] of 3-second "Hooks" for a video about [VIDEO_TOPIC].

### Video Context:
- **Target Audience**: [e.g., Aspiring Side-Hustlers, Busy Moms, Crypto Day-Traders].
- **Core Value**: [e.g., A tutorial, a surprising fact, a relatable comedy sketch].

### Hook Requirements:
For each hook, provide a 3-part breakdown:
1. **On-Screen Text (The Headline)**: A high-curiosity, "Pattern-Interrupt" text overlay (e.g., "Stop doing [X] if you want [Y]").
2. **The Audio/Vocal Hook**: The first sentence spoken in the video or the specific [TRENDING_AUDIO] style to use.
3. **The Visual Action**: Describe the specific movement or [TRANSITION_TYPE] in the first 2 seconds (e.g., "Dropping the camera," "Pointing to the side," "A sudden zoom-in").
4. **The "Wait for It" Payoff**: Briefly explain how you will resolve the curiosity in the middle or end of the video to maintain retention.

### Output Constraints:
- Use an Energetic, Fast-Paced, and Catchy tone.
- Group the hooks by [PSYCHOLOGICAL_TRIGGER] (e.g., Curiosity, Fear of Missing Out, Negative Visualization).
```

## 🧩 Variables to Fill Out

- `[VIDEO_TOPIC]` - What is the video essentially about?
- `[AMOUNT]` - e.g., 5 hook variations.
- `[TRENDING_AUDIO]` - e.g., "Aggressive bass," "ASMR whispers," "Lo-fi beats."
- `[TRANSITION_TYPE]` - e.g., Green screen, Body warp, Jump cut.

## 💡 Pro-Tips

- **Negative Hooks Pull Harder:** "Stop doing [X]" usually gets more views than "Start doing [X]." Ask the AI to "Write 2 'Mistake-Based' hooks."
- **Visual Movement is Mandatory:** A talking head without movement is a swipe-away. Ask the AI to "Suggest 3 camera movements to pair with the vocal hook."
- **The Closed Loop:** A good hook opens a loop in the user's brain ("What is the 3rd tool?"). Ensure the AI specifies *when* in the script that loop is closed.
