# Template: SEO-Optimized Blog Post Outlines

Use this template to architect long-form content that ranks on Google and resonates with readers. It is designed to ensure your content matches exact search intent while incorporating a logical hierarchy of H1-H4 tags, semantic keywords, and internal linking to build topical authority.

## 📋 Prompt Template

```text
Act as a Senior Content Strategist and SEO Copywriter. Generate a detailed Blog Post Outline for the [TARGET_KEYWORD].

### Content Context:
- **Search Intent**: [e.g., Informational, Transactional, Navigational].
- **Target Audience**: [DESCRIBE_READERS].
- **Primary Goal**: [e.g., Drive newsletter signups, Sell a product, Build brand awareness].

### Outline Requirements:
1. **The Title (H1)**: Provide 3 SEO-friendly title options that include the [TARGET_KEYWORD] at the beginning.
2. **Semantic Hierarchy (H2-H4)**: Structure the post with logical headers. For each section, provide:
   - **Key Takeaway**: The main point to be discussed.
   - **LSI/Secondary Keywords**: 3-5 related terms to include in that section.
3. **The "Featured Snippet" Target**: Draft a 2-sentence paragraph (usually under the first H2) that answers the [PRIMARY_QUESTION] directly for Google's "Position Zero."
4. **Internal & External Linking**: Suggest 2 types of pages to link to [INTERNALLY] and 2 authoritative [EXTERNAL_SOURCES] to cite.
5. **Call to Action (CTA)**: Propose a high-conversion CTA to be placed at the end and middle of the post.

### Output Constraints:
- Use a Professional, Expert, and Skimmable tone.
- Format the headers clearly (e.g., # H1, ## H2).
- Ensure the outline accounts for a total word count of approximately [WORD_COUNT] words.
```

## 🧩 Variables to Fill Out

- `[TARGET_KEYWORD]` - The main term you want to rank for.
- `[DESCRIBE_READERS]` - Who is searching for this? (Knowledge level).
- `[PRIMARY_QUESTION]` - The specific question the post answers (for the snippet).
- `[WORD_COUNT]` - e.g., 1,200 words, 2,500 words (pillar post).
- `[INTERNALLY]` - e.g., Product page, related case study.

## 💡 Pro-Tips

- **Match the Intent Above All:** If the keyword is "Best CRM," the user wants a listicle. If it's "What is a CRM," they want a definition. Ask the AI to "Audit the outline to ensure it perfectly matches the search intent."
- **Focus on the "Gap":** Check the top 3 results on Google. Ask the AI: "What information is missing from the existing top-ranking pages that we can include to provide more value?"
- **Topical Authority:** Don't just rank for one keyword. Ask the AI to "Suggest 3 related blog post topics we should write next to build a content cluster."
