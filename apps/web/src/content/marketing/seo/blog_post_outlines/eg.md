# Examples: SEO-Optimized Blog Outlines

## 1. Informational Guide: "How to Choose a Coffee Grinder"

```text
Act as a Senior Content Strategist. Generate a detailed Blog Post Outline for the keyword: "How to choose a coffee grinder."

### Content Context:
- **Search Intent**: Informational / Pre-purchase.
- **Target Audience**: Home coffee enthusiasts.
- **Goal**: Drive affiliate sales or product views.

### Outline Requirements:
1. **Title Options**: (e.g., "How to Choose a Coffee Grinder: A Complete 2024 Guide").
2. **Hierarchy**: H2s for Manual vs Electric, Burr vs Blade, and Price Points.
3. **Snippet Target**: A 2-sentence summary of the "Burr vs Blade" debate.
4. **Internal Linking**: Link to "Our Top 10 Burr Grinder" listicle.

### Output Constraints:
- Expert and Skimmable tone.
- 1,500 word structure.
```

## 2. Comparison Listicle: "Best CRM for Small Teams"

```text
Act as an SEO Copywriter. Generate an outline for "Best CRM for small teams."

### Content Context:
- **Search Intent**: Transactional / Comparative.
- **Goal**: Lead generation for our CRM tool.

### Outline Requirements:
- Focus on the "Features vs Price" comparison for each tool.
- Snippet target: "What is the best CRM for a team of under 10?"
- CTA: "Start our 14-day free trial."

### Output Constraints:
- Professional and Objective tone.
```
