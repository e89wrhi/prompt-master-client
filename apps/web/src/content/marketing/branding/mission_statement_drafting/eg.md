# Examples: Mission & Vision Drafting

## 1. Non-Profit: "TechEd for All"

```text
Act as a Senior CEO Advisor. Draft a Mission and Vision for "TechEd for All."

### Context:
- **What**: Coding bootcamps for low-income students.
- **Who**: Youth in underserved urban areas.
- **Enemy**: The digital divide and generational poverty.
- **Future**: A world where zip code doesn't determine career success.

### Drafting Requirements:
1. **Mission**: To provide world-class technical education to underprivileged youth.
2. **Vision**: A global workforce led by diverse, brilliant minds regardless of background.
3. **Manifesto**: Focus on the idea that "Genius is distributed, but opportunity isn't."

### Output Constraints:
- Inspiring and Authoritative tone.
- Brand Card format.
```

## 2. SaaS: "No-Meeting Project" (NMP)

```text
Act as a Corporate Communications Expert. Draft a Mission and Vision for "NMP."

### Context:
- **What**: Async-first project management software.
- **Enemy**: Meeting bloat and the "Status Update" culture.
- **Future**: Teams spending 80% of their time in Deep Work.

### Drafting Requirements:
- Mission: To eliminate unnecessary meetings through radical async transparency.
- Vision: Reclaiming 1 billion hours of human focus for the work that matters.

### Output Constraints:
- Purpose-Driven tone.
```
