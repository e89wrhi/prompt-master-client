# Examples: Brand Voice Guidelines

## 1. Zero-Jargon Fintech App ("SimpliFi")

```text
Act as a Senior Brand Strategist. Develop a Brand Voice Guideline for "SimpliFi," a finance app for people who find banks intimidating.

### Brand Context:
- **Audience**: Gen Z and Millennials with $0 in savings.
- **Product**: Automated saving and simple investing.
- **Position**: The Friendly Local Helper.

### Guideline Requirements:
1. **Pillars**: Radical Simplicity, Non-Judgmental, and Encouraging.
2. **"Say This, Not That" Examples**: 
   - Neutral: "Your transaction has been processed."
   - SimpliFi: "Boom! Your money is moving where it needs to go."
3. **Voice Scale**: Very Casual, Funny, and Enthusiastic.

### Output Constraints:
- Table format for examples.
- Inspirational tone.
```

## 2. High-Performance Bio-Hacking Brand ("Synapse")

```text
Act as a Verbal Identity Expert. Develop guidelines for "Synapse," selling premium brain-health supplements.

### Brand Context:
- **Audience**: Silicon Valley executives and elite athletes.
- **Product**: Nootropics.
- **Position**: Premium Disruptor/Academic Leader.

### Guideline Requirements:
- Pillars: Precision, Scientific Authority, and Understated Luxury.
- Avoid flowery language; use clinical, direct terms.
- "Say This, Not That": Comparison of a subscription confirmation.

### Output Constraints:
- Professional and Clear tone.
```
