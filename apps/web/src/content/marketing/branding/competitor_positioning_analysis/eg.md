# Examples: Competitor Positioning Analysis

## 1. High-End Remote Work Coffee ("PixelBrew")

```text
Act as a Senior Market Strategist. Perform a Positioning Analysis for "PixelBrew," a subscription coffee service for remote developers.

### Market Context:
- **Our Brand**: Super high caffeine, 24h shipping, includes "focus" music playlists.
- **Competitors**: Starbucks (Convenience), Blue Bottle (Quality/Expensive), Nespresso (Corporate).

### Analysis Requirements:
1. **Perceptual Map**: Axes: "Convenience vs Quality" and "Mass Market vs Niche."
2. **Competitor Teardowns**:
   - Starbucks: Hook is ubiquity. Weakness is average beans.
   - Blue Bottle: Hook is snobbery. Weakness is complexity/wait times.
3. **White Space**: The developer niche who wants quality *at home* without the snobbery.
4. **Blue Ocean Move**: Position as "The Developer's Fuel."
5. **Tagline Test**: 3 options.

### Output Constraints:
- Sharp and Competitive tone.
```

## 2. Budget vs. Premium Fitness App

```text
Act as a Positioning Expert. Analysis for "AuraFit," a premium meditation-led fitness app.

### Market Context:
- **Competitors**: Peloton (Device-heavy), Nike Training Club (Free/Generic), Headspace (Meditation only).

### Analysis Requirements:
- Perceptual Map: "Physical Intensity vs Mental Wellness."
- Identify the gap for people who want intense workouts but struggle with burnout.

### Output Constraints:
- Analytical tone.
```
