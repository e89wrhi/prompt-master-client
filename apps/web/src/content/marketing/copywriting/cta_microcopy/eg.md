# Examples: High-Conversion CTA Microcopy

## 1. SaaS Free Trial Signup

```text
Act as a Microcopy Specialist. Generate 10 variations of CTA microcopy for a SaaS Free Trial button.

### Context:
- **The Page**: Home page hero section.
- **The User Anxiety**: "I don't want to get stuck in a subscription" / "It will take too long to set up."
- **The Core Benefit**: Instant access to the dashboard.

### Writing Requirements:
1. **Benefit-Led**: "Get My Workflow Tool."
2. **Anxiety-Reducer**: "Start Free (No Card Needed)."
3. **First-Person**: "Take Me to the Dashboard."
4. **Curiosity**: "See My Productivity Score."

### Output Constraints:
- Button copy under 5 words.
- Provide psychological rationale for each.
```

## 2. "Book a Demo" for Enterprise Sales

```text
Act as a Conversion Strategist. Generate CTA copy for a "Book a Demo" button on a B2B sales page.

### Context:
- **Anxiety**: "I don't want a pushy sales call" / "This will be a waste of hour."
- **Benefit**: Expert advice on their specific problem.

### Writing Requirements:
- Focus on "Get Expert Advice" or "See it in Action."
- Suggest micro-text for below the button.

### Output Constraints:
- Persuasive tone.
```
