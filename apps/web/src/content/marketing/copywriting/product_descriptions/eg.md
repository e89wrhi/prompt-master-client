# Examples: Product Description Writing

## 1. High-Performance Noise-Canceling Headphones

```text
Act as a Senior eCommerce Copywriter. Write a 200-word description for "SilencePro X1."

### Product Specs:
- 40-hour battery life.
- Active Noise Cancellation (ANC).
- Genuine leather ear cups.
- Bluetooth 5.2.

### Writing Requirements:
1. **Benefit-Head**: Focus on the silence of a busy airplane.
2. **Sensory Storytelling**: The feel of the leather and the "weightless" design.
3. **Feature-to-Benefit**: "40-hour battery" -> "Forget your charger for a week."
4. **Ideal Use Case**: Crowded office or a long flight.

### Output Constraints:
- Luxurious and Elegant tone.
- Optimized for a Shopify product page.
```

## 2. Organic Hemp T-Shirt (Sustainable Brand)

```text
Act as a Product Storyteller. Write a description for the "EarthTee."

### Product Specs:
- 55% Hemp, 45% Organic Cotton.
- Naturally anti-microbial.
- Gets softer with every wash.

### Writing Requirements:
- Emphasize the "Anti-Microbial" benefit (Wear more, wash less).
- Focus on the "Softness" journey.

### Output Constraints:
- Rugged and Durable tone.
- Optimized for Etsy.
```
