# Examples: Direct Response Sales Letter

## 1. High-Ticket Mastermind (For Agency Owners)

```text
Act as a world-class Direct Response Copywriter. Draft a Long-Form Sales Letter for "The $10M Scale Mastermind."

### The Offer:
- **Price**: $15,000/year.
- **Value**: $100k+ in networking and proprietary systems.
- **Target Audience**: Agency owners stuck at $50k-$100k monthly revenue.

### Writing Requirements:
1. **Headline**: Direct Benefit - "How to double your agency revenue in 6 months."
2. **The Lead**: Focus on the "Burnout" of the owner who does everything themselves.
3. **The Bullets**: 10 fascinations about scaling.
4. **The Stack**: 2-day in-person retreat, weekly coaching, SOP library.
5. **Urgency**: Invitation-only, only 5 spots remaining for the Q4 cohort.

### Output Constraints:
- 1,500 words.
- Authoritative and Exclusive tone.
```

## 2. $97 Digital Course (Python for Marketers)

```text
Act as a world-class Direct Response Copywriter. Draft a Short-Form Sales Letter for "Python for Paid Media."

### The Offer:
- **Price**: $97 (One-time).
- **Audience**: Marketers who are tired of manual Excel reporting.

### Writing Requirements:
- Headline: "Automate your GA4 audits in 10 minutes."

- Bonus: 5 pre-made scripts.

### Output Constraints:
- 600 words.
- Urgent and Direct tone.
```
