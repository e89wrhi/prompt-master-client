# Template: Strategic Welcome Series Drip

Use this template to design a multi-email onboarding sequence that turns "New Subscribers" into "Loyal Fans." It is designed to introduce your brand's personality, provide immediate value (the "Quick Win"), and set the stage for your primary product or service offer without being overly aggressive.

## 📋 Prompt Template

```text
Act as a Lifecycle Marketer and Retention Specialist. Design a 5-email Welcome Series for [BRAND/SERVICE].

### Context:
- **The Opt-In**: [DESCRIBE_WHAT_THEY_JUST_SIGNED_UP_FOR] (e.g., A free guide, a newsletter, a waitlist).
- **Primary Goal**: [CONVERSION_GOAL] (e.g., Buy the starter kit, book a demo).

### Sequence Requirements:
1. **Email 1: The Delivery (Immediate)**: Deliver the opt-in. Set expectations for the frequency of emails and provide an immediate "Hidden Treasure" (a small, unexpected value-add).
2. **Email 2: Discovery (Day 1)**: Share the "Story of Why." Explain the problem [BRAND/SERVICE] was created to solve.
3. **Email 3: The Quick Win (Day 2)**: Provide a specific, actionable tip that the user can implement in 5 minutes to see a result related to [THE_OPT-IN_TOPIC].
4. **Email 4: Success Stories (Day 4)**: Share a case study or 3 rapid-fire testimonials that highlight the [PRIMARY_BENEFIT] from a user's perspective.
5. **Email 5: The Invitation (Day 6)**: Make your first direct [OFFER] for the [CONVERSION_GOAL]. Use a "Soft Scarcity" trigger.

### Output Constraints:
- Use a [TONE] tone.
- Include a "One-Sentence Teaser" at the end of every email for the next one.
- Keep the formatting simple and mobile-friendly.
```

## 🧩 Variables to Fill Out

- `[BRAND/SERVICE]` - Your name.
- `[DESCRIBE_WHAT_THEY_SIGNUP_FOR]` - The freebie.
- `[CONVERSION_GOAL]` - The long-term win.
- `[PRIMARY_BENEFIT]` - The #1 reason someone stays with you.
- `[OFFER]` - The specific deal you're pitching in Email 5.
- `[TONE]` - e.g., Warm/Welcoming, Scientific/Analytical, Exclusive/High-End.

## 💡 Pro-Tips

- **The Curiosity Loop:** Always tease the next email. If people know they are getting a "secret tip" in 48 hours, they are more likely to stay subscribed.
- **Micro-Segmentation:** If you have multiple opt-ins, ask the AI to "Adjust the Welcome Series specifically for users who came from the [TOPIC_X] guide."
- **The "Replied" Hack:** In Email 1 or 2, ask the user a simple question ("What's your #1 goal this month?"). When they reply, it sends a positive signal to email providers that your content is high-quality.
