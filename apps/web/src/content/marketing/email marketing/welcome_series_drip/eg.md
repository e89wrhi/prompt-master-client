# Examples: Strategic Welcome Series

## 1. Professional Networking Platform (B2B SaaS)

```text
Act as a Lifecycle Marketer. Design a 5-email Welcome Series for "NetConnect."

### Context:
- **Opt-In**: Signed up for a "10 LinkedIn Lead Gen Templates" PDF.
- **Primary Goal**: Get users to upgrade to the "Pro" plan.

### Sequence Requirements:
1. **Email 1**: Deliver templates + "Hidden Treasure" (Checklist for a perfect profile).
2. **Email 2**: Story of NMP (How our founder was rejected 50 times before automating his networking).
3. **Email 3**: The Quick Win (How to use Template #1 in 5 minutes).
4. **Email 5**: Invitation to the Pro plan with a "First Month Free" trial.

### Output Constraints:
- Professional and Warm tone.
- Teasers included.
```

## 2. Eco-Friendly Cleaning Supplies (DTC eCommerce)

```text
Act as a Retention Specialist. Design a Welcome Series for "GreenScrub."

### Context:
- **Opt-In**: "15% Off Your First Order" popup.
- **Goal**: First Purchase.

### Sequence Requirements:
- Email 2: The "Toxic Chemicals" problem we fight.
- Email 3: How to clean your toughest stain with just our spray and water.

### Output Constraints:
- Helpful and Enthusiastic tone.
```
