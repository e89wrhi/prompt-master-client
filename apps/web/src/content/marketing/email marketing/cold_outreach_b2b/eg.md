# Examples: High-Stakes B2B Cold Outreach

## 1. Selling Recruitment Software to a Fast-Growing Startup

```text
Act as a Senior SDR. Draft a cold email to the Head of Engineering at "FutureCorp."

### Prospect Context:
- **Activity**: They just raised a Series B and announced plans to hire 50 devs.
- **Pain Point**: Engineering leaders spend 50% of their time screening bad candidates.
- **Our Solution**: "ScreenWise," an automated technical assessment tool.

### Email Requirements:
1. **First Line**: "Saw your recent post about the 50-person hiring push at FutureCorp—congrats on the Series B."
2. **Tension Bridge**: "Usually, a push that big means you're about to lose 20 hours a week just on initial screenings."
3. **Social Proof**: "We helped TechScale reduce screening time by 12 hours while improving hire quality."
4. **Soft Ask**: "Open to seeing a 2-minute recording of how we automated their pipeline?"

### Output Constraints:
- Under 100 words.
- Peer-to-Peer tone.
```

## 2. Selling SEO Services to an eCommerce Manager

```text
Act as a Senior SDR. Email to the eCommerce Manager of "ModernHome."

### Prospect Context:
- **Pain Point**: Their organic traffic has dropped 20% since the last Google update.

### Email Requirements:
- Use a "Result-Based" subject line.
- Suggest a "Soft Ask" regarding a one-page audit.

### Output Constraints:
- Helpful and Direct tone.
```
