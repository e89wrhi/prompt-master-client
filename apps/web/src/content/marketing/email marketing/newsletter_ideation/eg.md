# Examples: Professional Newsletter Ideation

## 1. Weekly AI Growth Tips (SaaS Audience)

```text
Act as an Editorial Director. Ideate the next 4 editions for "The Growth Algorithm."

### Newsletter Context:
- **Audience**: SaaS founders and marketing managers.
- **Goal**: Drive signups for our AI-audit tool.

### Ideation Requirements:
1. **Pillars**: 1. The Big Prompt (Actionable), 2. This Week in AI (Curated), 3. Tool Spotlight.
2. **Subject Lines**: For each edition.
3. **VPS**: Focus on "One prompt you can use today."
4. **Call-to-Community**: Ask about their #1 AI frustration.

### Output Constraints:
- Editorial Calendar format.
- Authoritative and Helpful tone.
```

## 2. Personal Productivity & Mindset (Lifestyle)

```text
Act as a Newsletter Strategist. Ideate the next 2 editions for "Focus Saturday."

### Newsletter Context:
- **Audience**: High-performing professionals.
- **Theme**: Mental models and focus techniques.

### Ideation Requirements:
- Pillar 1: The Focus Shift (Mindset).
- Pillar 2: The Gear Guide (Product review).
- Include "Curiosity Gap" subject lines.

### Output Constraints:
- Calm and Encouraging tone.
```
