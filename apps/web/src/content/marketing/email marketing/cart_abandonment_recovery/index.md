# Template: 3-Email Cart Abandonment Sequence

Use this template to win back lost sales by reminding customers what they left behind. It is designed as a psychological progression: starting with a helpful reminder, moving to answering objections/concerns, and concluding with a time-limited incentive to close the gap.

## 📋 Prompt Template

```text
Act as a Senior eCommerce Recovery Specialist. Draft a 3-email Cart Abandonment Sequence for [PRODUCT/BRAND].

### Context:
- **Abandoned Item**: [ITEM_NAME]
- **Average Price**: [PRICE]
- **Typical Friction**: [e.g., Shipping costs, indecision, comparison shopping].

### Email Requirements:
1. **Email 1: The Helpful Reminder (1 Hour Later)**:
   - **Subject Line**: Curiosity-based or helpful (e.g., "Did you forget something?").
   - **Copy**: Focus on "Did life get in the way?" and provide a direct link to their [ITEM_NAME] in the cart.
2. **Email 2: The Social Proof & FAQ (24 Hours Later)**:
   - **Subject Line**: Confidence-driven (e.g., "See what others are saying about [ITEM_NAME]").
   - **Copy**: Address the [TYPICAL_FRICTION]. Include a testimonial snippet and a "Ask us anything" prompt.
3. **Email 3: The Strategic Incentive (48-72 Hours Later)**:
   - **Subject Line**: Urgency-driven (e.g., "Your cart is expiring!").
   - **Copy**: Offer a [DISCOUNT_OR_GIFT] that expires in 24 hours. Emphasize that this is the final reminder.

### Output Constraints:
- Use a [TONE] tone.
- Keep the emails concise and "skimmable" (under 150 words each).
- Include specific placement suggestions for a "Return to Cart" button in every email.
```

## 🧩 Variables to Fill Out

- `[PRODUCT/BRAND]` - Your name.
- `[ITEM_NAME]` - The placeholder for the product.
- `[TYPICAL_FRICTION]` - Why do people leave? (e.g., "I need to talk to my partner," "Is the sizing accurate?").
- `[DISCOUNT_OR_GIFT]` - e.g., 10% Off, Free Shipping, Free Bonus Item.
- `[TONE]` - e.g., Friendly/Approachable, Urgent/Direct, Luxurious/Sophisticated.

## 💡 Pro-Tips

- **Don't Discount too Early:** Email 1 should never have a discount. You don't want to "train" your customers to abandon their cart just to get a coupon.
- **Micro-Copy Matters:** Instead of "Buy Now," use "Take Me Back to My Cart" or "Resume My Order."
- **The "Dynamic" Hero Image:** Remind the AI to "Suggest a dynamic placeholder where the image of the exact [ITEM_NAME] they left behind should appear."
