# Examples: Cart Abandonment Recovery

## 1. High-End Leather Boots ($350)

```text
Act as an eCommerce Recovery Specialist. Draft a 3-email sequence for "Onyx Leather."

### Context:
- **Abandoned Item**: The "Heritage Boot."
- **Price**: $350.
- **Typical Friction**: Sizing concerns and high price point.

### Email Requirements:
1. **Email 1**: "Did life get in the way?"
2. **Email 2**: "The Heritage Boot: Built to last 10 years." (Focus on longevity vs price).
3. **Email 3**: "Free Priority Shipping (Expiring in 24h)."

### Output Constraints:
- Sophisticated and Sophisticated tone.
- Under 150 words per email.
```

## 2. Personalized Pet Food Subscription

```text
Act as an eCommerce Recovery Specialist. Draft a sequence for "PupPlate."

### Context:
- **Friction**: "Will my dog like the taste?"

### Email Requirements:
- Email 2 should focus on the "Taste Guarantee" and include photos of happy dogs.
- Email 3 offers a "1st Bag is on us" (Free Trial) to close the deal.

### Output Constraints:
- Friendly and Approachable tone.
```
