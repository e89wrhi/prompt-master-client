# Style: Neon-Soaked Cyberpunk (Cyberpink)

![Cyberpink](file:///d:/dev/_projects/prompt-library/assets/images/cyberpink.png)

This style captures the "High Tech, Low Life" world of the future. It is defined by vibrant neon lights (Cyan, Magenta, Pink), rain-slicked urban streets, dense technology, volumetric fog, and a sharp contrast between glowing light and deep shadows.

## 📋 Prompt Template

```text
Act as a Sci-Fi World Builder and Concept Artist. Generate a prompt for a [SCENE/CHARACTER] in a Cyberpunk city.

### Aesthetic Profile:
- **Neon Palette**: [e.g., Hot Pink and Electric Blue, Acid Green and Amber].
- **Atmosphere**: [e.g., Heavy rain, thick smog, volumetric neon fog].
- **Tech Detail**: [e.g., Retractable cyber-arms, holographic billboards, flying traffic].

### Visual Requirements:
1. **The Setting**: Describe the [ARCHITECTURE] (e.g., "Deep urban canyons with tangled wires," "Sleek glass skyscrapers with glowing signage").
2. **Surface Reflections**: Include [REFLECTIVE_SURFACES] (e.g., "Puddles on the ground reflecting neon," "Wet asphalt," "Chrome plating").
3. **Lighting**: Define the [NEON_CONTRAST] (e.g., "Hard pink side-lighting," "Glowing cyan halos").

### Output Constraints:
- Vibrant, Gritty, and High-Tech tone.
- Conclude with "Unreal Engine 5 render, ray-tracing, cyberpunk aesthetic, 8k."
```

## 🧩 Variables to Fill Out

- `[ARCHITECTURE]` - The world building detail.
- `[NEON_PALETTE]` - The primary color story.
- `[REFLECTIVE_SURFACES]` - Essential for the cyberpunk look.

## 💡 Pro-Tips

- **Rain is Mandatory:** Cyberpunk looks 2x better when everything is wet. Always include "Rain-slicked streets" or "Falling rain."
- **The "Low Life" Detail:** To make it feel real, add "Tangled power lines," "Discarded electronics," or "Gritty graffiti."
- **Holograms for Depth:** Use "Semi-translucent holographic advertisements" to add layers of light to your scene.
