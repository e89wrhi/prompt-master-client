# Examples: Neon-Soaked Cyberpunk

## 1. Rainy Night Cityscape

```text
Act as a Sci-Fi World Builder. Generate a prompt for a wide cityscape.

### Aesthetic Profile:
- **Palette**: Hot Pink and Electric Blue.
- **Atmosphere**: Heavy rain and volumetric neon fog.
- **Tech**: Flying cars weaving through skyscraper canyons.

### Visual Requirements:
- Deep urban canyons with tangled wires and glowing signs.
- Puddles on the ground reflecting the pink lights.
- Wet asphalt texture.

### Output Constraints:
- Unreal Engine 5 render, ray-tracing, 8k.
```

## 2. Augmented Street Vendor

```text
Act as a Concept Artist. Generate a character portrait of a street food vendor.

### Aesthetic Profile:
- **Tech**: Multiple cybernetic arms cooking noodles.
- **Atmosphere**: Steam rising from the cooking pots.

### Visual Requirements:
- Harsh pink side-lighting from a nearby neon shop sign.
- Gritty graffiti on the wall behind.

### Output Constraints:
- Cyberpunk aesthetic, 8k.
```
