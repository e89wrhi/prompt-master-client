# Examples: Dramatic Monochrome

## 1. Noir Detective Portrait

```text
Act as a Noir Cinematographer. Generate a black and white portrait.

### Monochrome Profile:
- **Contrast**: High-contrast, sharp shadows.
- **Lighting**: Chiaroscuro; light from a single source.

### Visual Requirements:
- A detective in a trench coat, his face half-hidden in shadow.
- Light filtering through blinds across his face.
- Heavy film grain.

### Output Constraints:
- Black and white film aesthetic, dramatic lighting, 8k.
```

## 2. Minimalist Grayscale "Wave"

```text
Act as a Master of B&W Photography. Generate a soft grayscale landscape.

### Monochrome Profile:
- **Contrast**: Soft grayscale, low contrast.
- **Lighting**: Diffused overcast light.

### Visual Requirements:
- A single wave captured in slow motion.
- Smooth digital gradient, shimmering silver highlights.

### Output Constraints:
- Timeless and elegant tone.
```
