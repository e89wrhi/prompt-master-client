# Style: Dramatic Monochrome

![Monochrome](/images/styles/monochrome.png)

This style removes the distraction of color to focus entirely on [LIGHT], [SHADOW], [TEXTURE], and [FORM]. It ranges from high-contrast film noir to soft, minimalist grayscale, emphasizing the emotional weight and dramatic depth of a scene.

## 📋 Prompt Template

```text
Act as a Master of Black & White Photography and Noir Cinematographer. Generate a prompt for a [SCENE/PORTRAIT] in dramatic monochrome.

### Monochrome Profile:
- **Contrast**: [e.g., High-contrast (Hard edges), Soft grayscale (Low contrast), Mid-tone rich].
- **Lighting**: [e.g., Chiaroscuro shadows, single harsh spotlight, diffused ambient glow].
- **Texture**: [e.g., Sharp film grain, smooth digital gradient, rough physical textures].

### Visual Requirements:
1. **The Core Contrast**: Describe the relationship between the [BRIGHTEST_LIGHT] and the [DEEPEST_SHADOW] (e.g., "Light filtering through blinds creating a striped pattern").
2. **The Subject**: Describe [SUBJECT] emphasizing their [FORM_AND_SHAPE] (e.g., "Silhouette of a man," "The intricate curves of a marble statue").
3. **Atmosphere**: Define the "Mood" (e.g., "Lonely and haunting," "Timeless and elegant," "Hard-boiled noir").

### Output Constraints:
- Dramatic, Timeless, and High-Contrast tone.
- Conclude with "Black and white film aesthetic, sharp shadows, dramatic lighting, 8k."
```

## 🧩 Variables to Fill Out

- `[SUBJECT]` - The object of the study.
- `[CONTRAST]` - The "Punch" of the image.
- `[BRIGHTEST_LIGHT]` - The focal point of luminosity.

## 💡 Pro-Tips

- **The "Noir" Blind Trick:** To add instant drama, ask for light filtering through [WINDOW_BLINDS] or [VENETIAN_BLINDS] to create striped shadows across the subject.
- **Silver Highlights:** Ask for "Deep blacks and shimmering silver highlights" to replicate the look of high-quality silver-gelatin prints.
- **Grain for Grit:** If the image looks too "clean," add "Heavy 35mm film grain" to give it a more grounded, physical feel.
