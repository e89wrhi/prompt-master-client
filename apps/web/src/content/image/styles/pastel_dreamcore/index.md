# Style: Ethereal Pastel Dreamcore

![Pastel Dreamcore](/images/styles/pastel_dreamcore.png)

This style focuses on the surreal, hazy, and nostalgic world of "Dreamcore." It emphasizes soft pastel palettes (Cotton Candy, Mint, Lavender), low contrast, hazy atmospheres, [LIMINAL_SPACES], and a sense of peaceful but surreal loneliness.

## 📋 Prompt Template

```text
Act as a Surrealist Video Artist and Dreamcore Architect. Generate a prompt for a [SCENE/OBJECT] in the Dreamcore style.

### Dreamscape Profile:
- **Palette**: [e.g., Soft Mint and Lavender, Peach and Light Blue, Cotton Candy Gradients].
- **Atmosphere**: [e.g., Thick hazy mist, soft-focus glowing air, permanent golden-hour haze].
- **Sub-Genre**: [e.g., Liminal space (Empty malls/pools), Nostalgic childhood room, Surreal floating islands].

### Visual Requirements:
1. **The Core Subject**: Describe [SUBJECT] with focus on [SURREAL_LOGIC] (e.g., "A floating landline phone," "A single door standing in a pink cloud").
2. **Lighting**: Define the [SOFT_GLOW] (e.g., "Ethereal diffused light," "Subtle halo effect around objects").
3. **Texture**: Specify the [HAZINESS] (e.g., "Low-quality vintage VHS feel," "Soft-focus dreaminess," "Clean 3D pastel").

### Output Constraints:
- Ethereal, Surreal, and Peaceful tone.
- Conclude with "Pastel dreamcore aesthetic, hazy atmosphere, soft focus, nostalgic surrealism."
```

## 🧩 Variables to Fill Out

- `[SUBJECT]` - The object of the dream.
- `[PALETTE]` - The soft colors.
- `[SURREAL_LOGIC]` - Something that shouldn't be there.

## 💡 Pro-Tips

- **Liminal Spaces are Key:** Dreamcore often uses [EMPTY_RECOGNIZABLE_SPACES] (e.g., "An empty indoor swimming pool at 3 AM"). This creates a sense of "Anemoia" (nostalgia for a time you never lived).
- **Haze over Detail:** Don't ask for "Sharp 8k detail." Instead, ask for "Soft-focus, diffused glow, and dreamy atmosphere" to capture the feeling of a half-remembered dream.
- **VHS Effect:** To lean into the nostalgic side, add "Subtle VHS tracking lines" or "Low-fidelity aesthetic."
