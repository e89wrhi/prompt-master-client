# Examples: Master-Level Oil Painting

## 1. Rembrandt-Style Portrait

```text
Act as a Master Oil Painter. Generate a portrait of an elderly man.

### Artistic Technique:
- **Style**: Rembrandt, Dutch Golden Age.
- **Brushwork**: Soft sfumato on the skin, thick impasto on the jewelry.
- **Lighting**: Dramatic chiaroscuro.

### Visual Requirements:
- Deep, rich umber and ochre colors.
- Subtle craquelure on the surface.

### Output Constraints:
- Oil on canvas, masterwork.
```

## 2. Van Gogh-Style Starry Night (Modern Concept)

```text
Act as an Art Historian. Generate an oil painting of a futuristic city in the style of Van Gogh.

### Artistic Technique:
- **Brushwork**: Short, thick, rhythmic dabs of pure color (Impasto).
- **Palette**: Cobalt Blue, Cadmium Yellow, and Radiant White.

### Output Constraints:
- Visible brushstrokes, textured paint.
```
