# Style: Master-Level Oil Painting

![Oil Painting](file:///d:/dev/_projects/prompt-library/assets/images/oil_painting.png)

This style replicates the physical properties of traditional oil on canvas. It emphasizes thick [BRUSHSTROCKES] (Impasto), subtle blending (Sfumato), rich pigment saturation, and the distinct [CANVAS_TEXTURE] of historical or modern art masters.

## 📋 Prompt Template

```text
Act as a Master Oil Painter and Art Historian. Generate a prompt for an oil painting of [SUBJECT] in the style of [ARTIST/ERA].

### Artistic Technique:
- **Brushwork**: [e.g., Heavy impasto with thick ridges, soft blended sfumato, short rhythmic dabs].
- **Texture**: [e.g., Coarse linen canvas, cracked varnish over time, smooth wooden panel].
- **Lighting**: [e.g., Dramatic Caravaggio-style chiaroscuro, soft glowing Impressionist light].

### Visual Requirements:
1. **The Composition**: Describe [SUBJECT] with focus on [CLASSICAL_PROPORTIONS].
2. **Color Profile**: Specify the [PIGMENT_QUALITY] (e.g., "Rich umbers and raw ochre," "Vibrant, unblended primaries").
3. **Atmosphere**: Define the "Age" (e.g., "Museum quality antique," "Fresh wet paint on a modern canvas").

### Output Constraints:
- Sophisticated, Precise, and Authoritative tone.
- Conclude with "Oil on canvas, visible brushstrokes, textured paint, masterwork."
```

## 🧩 Variables to Fill Out

- `[ARTIST / ERA]` - e.g., Rembrandt, Monet, Van Gogh, The Renaissance.
- `[SUBJECT]` - The portrait or landscape.
- `[BRUSHWORK]` - The key to the physical feel.

## 💡 Pro-Tips

- **Impasto for Depth:** If you want the paint to look "3D," ask for "Heavy visible impasto where the light hits the canvas."
- **Craquelure for Age:** To make it look like a 300-year-old museum piece, add "Subtle craquelure (fine cracks) across the surface."
- **Glazing for Glow:** For realistic light, ask for "Multiple layers of translucent glazing to create deep luminosity."
