# Examples: Hand-Drawn Pencil Sketch

## 1. Anatomical Study (Da Vinci Style)

```text
Act as a Master Illustrator. Generate a pencil sketch of a human hand in motion.

### Drafting Technique:
- **Medium**: Soft charcoal and HB pencil.
- **Texture**: Visible graphite smudges and faint construction lines.
- **Paper**: Aged, cream-toned vellum paper.

### Visual Requirements:
- Multiple "gestural" sketches of the hand in the same frame.
- Rough, cross-hatched shadows on the knuckles.

### Output Constraints:
- Hand-drawn sketch, pencil texture, detailed shading.
```

## 2. Technical Architectural Sketch

```text
Act as a Sketch Artist. Generate a charcoal sketch of a modern skyscraper.

### Drafting Technique:
- **Medium**: Technical fineliner for edges with soft charcoal for shadows.

### Visual Requirements:
- Loose and gestural lines at the base, becoming ultra-fine and detailed at the top.
- Raw scribbled shadows for the surrounding environment.

### Output Constraints:
- Graphite on paper, artistic tone.
```
