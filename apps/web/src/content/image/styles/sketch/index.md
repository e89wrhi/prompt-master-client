# Style: Hand-Drawn Pencil Sketch

![Sketch](file:///d:/dev/_projects/prompt-library/assets/images/sketch.png)

This style focuses on the raw, tactile beauty of [PENCIL/GRAPHITE] on paper. It emphasizes visible [CROSS-HATCHING], graphite smudges, varying line weights, and the organic texture of [THICK_SKETCHBOOK_PAPER].

## 📋 Prompt Template

```text
Act as a Master Illustrator and Sketch Artist. Generate a prompt for a [PENCIL/CHARCOAL] sketch of [SUBJECT].

### Drafting Technique:
- **Medium**: [e.g., HB Pencil, Soft Charcoal, Technical Fineliner].
- **Texture**: [e.g., Heavy pencil shading, visible graphite smudges, cross-hatched shadows].
- **Paper**: [e.g., Cream-toned cartridge paper, rough wood-pulp paper, vellum].

### Visual Requirements:
1. **The Line Work**: Describe the [LINE_WEIGHT] (e.g., "Loose and gestural lines," "Ultra-fine technical detail," "Thick, dark charcoal strokes").
2. **Shading & Depth**: Specify the [SHADING_METHOD] (e.g., "Careful cross-hatching for depth," "Smooth graphite blending," "Raw scribbled shadows").
3. **The "Draft" Vibe**: Include elements of [PROCESS] (e.g., "Faint construction lines visible," "Eraser marks and lead smudges").

### Output Constraints:
- Raw, Artistic, and Hand-Drawn tone.
- Conclude with "Hand-drawn sketch, pencil texture, graphite on paper, detailed shading."
```

## 🧩 Variables to Fill Out

- `[SUBJECT]` - The character or anatomy.
- `[MEDIUM]` - Graphite, Ink, or Charcoal?
- `[LINE_WEIGHT]` - How bold are the lines?

## 💡 Pro-Tips

- **Construction Lines add Realism:** If the sketch is "too perfect," it looks like a digital filter. Ask for "Visible faint construction lines and circular guides" to make it look like a work-in-progress.
- **Vary the Graphite:** Ask for "A mix of hard HB lines for detail and soft 6B shading for deep blacks."
- **Focus on the "Tooth":** In drawing, "Tooth" refers to the texture of the paper. Ask for "High paper tooth texture" to get those realistic white gaps in the pencil strokes.
