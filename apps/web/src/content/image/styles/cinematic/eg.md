# Examples: Epic Cinematic Photography

## 1. Sci-Fi Desert Arrival

```text
Act as a Film Director. Generate a wide cinematic shot.

### Cinematic Specs:
- **Lighting**: Harsh desert sun with long shadows.
- **Lens**: 35mm Anamorphic.
- **Color Grade**: Warm, desaturated oranges and deep blue sky.

### Visual Requirements:
- A massive spacecraft landing in the middle of a vast salt flat.
- Volumetric dust clouds rising from the ground.
- Low angle tracking shot.

### Output Constraints:
- Shot on Arri Alexa, movie still, 8k.
```

## 2. Noir City Street

```text
Act as a Director of Photography. Generate a close-up shot of a detective.

### Cinematic Specs:
- **Lighting**: Moody noir lighting; light passing through blinds.
- **Color Grade**: Dark, moody, high contrast.

### Visual Requirements:
- Heavy rain falling.
- Silhouette shot.

### Output Constraints:
- Anamorphic lens flare, movie still.
```
