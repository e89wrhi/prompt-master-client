# Style: Epic Cinematic Photography

![Cinematic](/images/styles/cinematic.png)

This style aims for the high-production value of a feature film. It emphasizes dramatic lighting (Chiaroscuro, rim lighting), anamorphic lens flares, shallow depth of field (Bokeh), and professional color grading (Teal & Orange) to tell a large-scale visual story.

## 📋 Prompt Template

```text
Act as a Film Director and Director of Photography. Generate a prompt for a [WIDE/CLOSE-UP] cinematic shot of [SUBJECT].

### Cinematic Specs:
- **Lighting**: [e.g., Dramatic low-key, golden hour backlighting, moody noir shadows].
- **Lens**: [e.g., 35mm Anamorphic, 85mm Prime, Wide-angle].
- **Color Grade**: [e.g., Moody Teal & Orange, Vibrant Technicolor, Desaturated Cyberpunk].

### Visual Requirements:
1. **The Scene**: Describe [SUBJECT] in a high-stakes moment.
2. **Atmospheric Detail**: Include [VOLUMETRIC_FOG], [HEAVY_RAIN], or [SWIRLING_DUST].
3. **Camera Work**: Specify the [SHOT_TYPE] (e.g., "Low angle hero shot," "Over-the-shoulder," "Tracking wide shot").

### Output Constraints:
- Epic and Dramatic tone.
- Conclude with "Shot on Arri Alexa, anamorphic lens flare, movie still, 8k."
```

## 🧩 Variables to Fill Out

- `[SUBJECT]` - The character or setting.
- `[LIGHTING]` - The most important cinematic factor.
- `[COLOR_GRADE]` - The "Look" of the film.

## 💡 Pro-Tips

- **The Rim Light:** To make your subject "pop" from the background, ask for "Harsh rim lighting that outlines the silhouette."
- **Depth of Field:** Use "Soft bokeh" for portraits to focus entirely on the emotion, or "Deep focus" for grand landscapes.
- **Aspect Ratio:** Cinematic shots work best in wide aspect ratios. (Note: Mention `/ar 21:9` for Midjourney users).
