# Examples: Fluid & Translucent Watercolor

## 1. Misty Forest Morning

```text
Act as a Master Watercolor Artist. Generate a prompt for a misty forest landscape.

### Painting Technique:
- **Style**: Loose and splashy with wet-on-wet blurry tree lines.
- **Water Effects**: Beautiful pigment bleeds at the bottom of the frame.
- **Paper**: Heavyweight 300gsm cold-pressed paper.

### Visual Requirements:
- Deep Forest Green and Misted Grey that bleed into one another.
- Significant white space in the sky area.

### Output Constraints:
- Watercolor painting, translucent layers, paper bleed.
```

## 2. Vibrant Botanical Study (Hummingbird)

```text
Act as a Watercolor Painter. Generate a prompt for a hummingbird and a tropical flower.

### Painting Technique:
- **Style**: Detailed hyper-realistic watercolor for the subject with loose splashes in the background.

### Visual Requirements:
- Vibrant Emerald, Fuchsia, and Cobalt Blue accents.
- High granulation in the blue shadows and dry-brush highlights on the feathers.

### Output Constraints:
- Cold-pressed paper texture, vibrant tone.
```
