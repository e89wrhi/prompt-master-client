# Examples: Vibrant Anime & Manga

## 1. Ghibli-Style Countryside

```text
Act as an Anime Concept Artist. Generate a prompt in the Studio Ghibli style.

### Artistic Direction:
- **Style**: Studio Ghibli, hand-painted.
- **Line Work**: Soft and hand-drawn.
- **Shading**: Soft gradients.

### Visual Requirements:
- A small cottage in a field of flowers under a massive, fluffy white cloud.
- Warm, nostalgic summer lighting.

### Output Constraints:
- High-definition anime, gouache background.
```

## 2. Modern Action (Cyberpunk)

```text
Act as a Lead Animator. Generate a prompt in a Modern Shonen style.

### Artistic Direction:
- **Style**: Modern, high-octane.
- **Line Work**: Sharp and clean.

### Visual Requirements:
- A cyborg warrior mid-jump.
- Electric blue speed lines and sparks.

### Output Constraints:
- Cell-shaded, masterwork composition.
```
