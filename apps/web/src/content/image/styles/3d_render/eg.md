# Examples: High-Fidelity 3D Render

## 1. Futuristic Headphones

```text
Act as a 3D Visualization Artist. Generate a render of futuristic over-ear headphones.

### Technical Style:
- **Engine**: Unreal Engine 5 aesthetic.
- **Material**: Matte black carbon-fiber with glowing cyan LED rings.
- **Lighting**: Cinematic side-lighting in a dark void.

### Visual Requirements:
- Focus on the texture of the carbon fiber weave.
- Volumetric fog around the LEDs.

### Output Constraints:
- 8k, photorealistic, cinematic shot.
```

## 2. Minimalist Glass Sculpture

```text
Act as a 3D Artist. Render a floating glass sphere with liquid mercury inside.

### Technical Style:

- **Material**: Refractive glass and liquid metal.

### Output Constraints:

```
