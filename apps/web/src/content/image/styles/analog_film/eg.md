# Examples: Nostalgic Analog Film

## 1. Golden Hour Cityscape

```text
Act as a Film Photographer. Shot on Kodak Portra 400.

### Film Characteristics:
- **Stock**: Kodak Portra 400.
- **Texture**: Fine film grain, soft glow.
- **Optics**: 35mm lens, wide aperture.

### Visual Requirements:
- A street in San Francisco at sunset.
- Warm, orange-toned light hitting the vintage cars.

### Output Constraints:
- Authentic film grain, 35mm aesthetic.
```

## 2. Low-Light Diner (Cinestill Style)

```text
Act as a Cinematographer. Shot on Cinestill 800T.

### Film Characteristics:
- **Stock**: Cinestill 800T.
- **Texture**: Heavy grain, distinct red halation around neon signs.

### Visual Requirements:
- A person sitting in a neon-lit diner at night.
- Teal and Orange color grading.

### Output Constraints:
- Analog imperfections.
```
