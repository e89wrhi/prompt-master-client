# Style: Nostalgic Analog Film

![Analog Film](file:///d:/dev/_projects/prompt-library/assets/images/analog_film.png)

This style captures the warm, organic, and imperfect aesthetic of traditional film photography. It emphasizes high grain, natural color shifts (Halation), soft focus, and the distinct "vintage" feel of stocks like Kodak Portra 400 or Fujifilm Superia.

## 📋 Prompt Template

```text
Act as a Film Photographer and Cinematographer. Generate a prompt for a [STREET/PORTRAIT] shot on [FILM_STOCK].

### Film Characteristics:
- **Film Stock**: [e.g., Kodak Portra 400, Fujifilm Superia, Cinestill 800T].
- **Texture**: [e.g., Heavy film grain, subtle light leaks, dust and scratches].
- **Optics**: [e.g., 35mm f/1.4 lens, soft-focus, slight motion blur].

### Visual Requirements:
1. **The Subject**: Describe [SUBJECT] in a candid, natural moment.
2. **Color Profile**: Specify the [TONAL_WARMTH] (e.g., "Warm golden hour hues," "Cool cyan shadows with orange highlights").
3. **Atmosphere**: Define the "Nostalgia" (e.g., "A sense of a fading memory," "Vibrant retro summer vibe").

### Output Constraints:
- Nostalgic and Organic tone.
- Conclude with "Authentic film grain, analog imperfections, 35mm aesthetic."
```

## 🧩 Variables to Fill Out

- `[FILM_STOCK]` - The soul of the image's colors.
- `[SUBJECT]` - Who or what are we capturing?
- `[TONAL_WARMTH]` - Warm vs. Cold film.

## 💡 Pro-Tips

- **Halation is the Secret:** In film, bright lights often have a red "glow" at the edges. Ask for "Subtle red halation around high-contrast edges."
- **Embrace Imperfection:** Digital is too perfect. Ask for "Slight chromatic aberration" or "Minor light leaks at the bottom edge" for true analog feel.
