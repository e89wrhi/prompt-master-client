# Examples: Modern Pinterest Assets

## 1. "Morning Routine Checklist" (Lifestyle Knolling)

```text
Act as an Information Designer. Generate assets for a "Morning Routine" Pinterest pin.

### Infographic Context:
- **Style**: Realistic top-down knolling photography.
- **Theme**: Productivity and Wellness.

### Visual Requirements:
1. **Header Visual**: A beautiful flat-lay of a ceramic white coffee cup, a leather-bound journal, and a pair of minimalist glasses on an oak table.
2. **Icons**: A stylized 3D water bottle, a small alarm clock, and a green leaf.
3. **Color**: Natural Earth tones (Sage Green, Oak Wood, Bone White).
4. **Composition**: Clean isolation on wood.

### Output Constraints:
- Vibrant and Informative tone.
- Copy-pasteable code block.
```
