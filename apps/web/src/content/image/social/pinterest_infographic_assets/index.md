# Template: Modern Pinterest Infographic Assets

Use this template to generate high-fidelity 3D and 2D assets for Pinterest infographics. It is designed to create [VERITCAL_ASPECT_RATIO] visuals that work within a "Long Scroll" logic—utilizing bold icons, clean 3D objects, and high-contrast sections to convey information at a glance in the Pinterest Feed.

## 📋 Prompt Template

```text
Act as an Information Designer and Pinterest Growth Strategist. Generate a set of high-impact visual assets for an infographic about [TOPIC].

### Infographic Context:
- **Primary Goal**: [e.g., Guide for beginners, Product comparison, Checklist for X].
- **Key Asset Style**: [e.g., 3D "Candy" style, Minimalist line art, Realistic top-down knolling photography].

### Visual Requirements:
1. **The Lead Header Visual**: Describe a [GRAND_CENTERPIECE] that will appear at the top of the 2:3 vertical Pinterest pin (e.g., "A large 3D golden trophy," "A beautifully arranged flat-lay of morning routine items").
2. **Iconography & Symbols**: Generate 5 specific [ICONS_OR_SYMBOLS] that represent the [KEY_STEPS] (e.g., "A glowing checklist icon," "A 3D glass clock," "A stylized minimal plant").
3. **The "Scroll-Stopper" Color**: Use a dominate [PRIMARY_COLOR] that pops against the white/light Pinterest feed.
4. **Composition & Staging**: Specify [CLEAN_ISOLATION] (e.g., "Each asset on a pure white background with soft shadows," "Isometric 3D perspective").
5. **Technical Specs**: Conclude with fidelity cues (e.g., "High-resolution 3D render, Cinema 4D aesthetic, vibrant saturation, clean vector edges, photorealistic textures for flat-lays").

### Output Constraints:
- Use a Results-Oriented, Vibrant, and Informative tone.
- Avoid intricate details that are lost in a small feed preview.
- Provide the final prompt in a copy-pasteable code block.
```

## 🧩 Variables to Fill Out

- `[TOPIC]` - e.g., Beginner's Guide to Crypto, 10 Best Skincare Products, Home Office Setup.
- `[ICONS_OR_SYMBOLS]` - The building blocks of the infographic.
- `[CLEAN_ISOLATION]` - Necessary for easy cropping and layout later.
- `[PRIMARY_COLOR]` - The color that stops the scroll.

## 💡 Pro-Tips

- **Knolling Photography works best on Pinterest:** For lifestyle infographics, ask the AI for a [KNOLLED_FLAT_LAY] (items laid out in a grid from above). It is one of Pinterest's highest-CTR image styles.
- **3D is the new Standard:** Flat 2D icons are becoming dated. Ask the AI for [3D_GLASSMORPHISM_ICONS] or [SOFT_PLASTIC_3D_ICONS] to give your infographic a modern, tactile feel.
- **Title Safety:** The top 20% of your Pinterest pin is for the title. Tell the AI to "Leave the header area uncluttered to allow for a large bold title overlay."
