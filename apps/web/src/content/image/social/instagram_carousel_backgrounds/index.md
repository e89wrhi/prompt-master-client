# Template: High-Engagement Instagram Carousel Backgrounds

Use this template to generate a cohesive set of backgrounds for Instagram carousels. It is designed to ensure a consistent [COLOR_STORY] and [TEXTUREAL_FLOW] across all slides, providing clear areas for text overlay while making the "Swipe" experience feel like a single, continuous journey.

## 📋 Prompt Template

```text
Act as a Senior Social Media Content Strategist and Visual Brand Designer. Generate a set of [NUMBER_OF_SLIDES] background prompts for a carousel about [TOPIC].

### Design Context:
- **Tone**: [e.g., Academic & Professional, Vibrant & High-Energy, Soft & Calming].
- **Texture Profile**: [e.g., Grainy analog paper, sleek glassmorphism, soft 3D clay, minimalist concrete].

### Visual Requirements:
1. **The Lead Slide (Hook)**: Describe a high-impact background for the first slide with a [CENTRAL_FOCAL_POINT] that captures attention (e.g., "A large glowing orb on the right," "A bold geometric shape in the center").
2. **The "Flow" Logic**: Explain how the backgrounds transition (e.g., "The colors shift from dark blue to light cyan as the slides progress," "A continuous gold line moves across the bottom of all slides").
3. **The Text Layout Zones**: Designate [SAFE_AREAS] for heavy text (e.g., "Keep the top 40% of every slide empty of busy patterns," "Use a blurred rectangle on the left side for bullet points").
4. **Color Palette & Lighting**: Specify 3 [KEY_COLORS] and the [LIGHTING_STYLE] (e.g., "Soft diffused top-light," "High-key studio brightness," "Moody neon side-lighting").
5. **Technical Aesthetics**: Conclude with fidelity cues (e.g., "Clean 3D render, minimalist vector style, high resolution, no small distracting details, solid pastel background").

### Output Constraints:
- Use a Cohesive, Modern, and Strategically-Minded tone.
- Ensure the backgrounds are optimized for a 1080x1350 (4:5) aspect ratio.
- Provide a master prompt for the "Style" and then individual variations if needed.
```

## 🧩 Variables to Fill Out

- `[TOPIC]` - e.g., 5 Rules for Investing, My Morning Routine, Why Code is Art.
- `[NUMBER_OF_SLIDES]` - Usually 5-10.
- `[SAFE_AREAS]` - Where the text will live.
- `[KEY_COLORS]` - The brand colors.

## 💡 Pro-Tips

- **The "Continuous Swipe" Secret:** To make the carousel feel premium, tell the AI to "Include an element (like a flowing silk ribbon or a beam of light) that starts on the right side of Slide 1 and continues on the left side of Slide 2."
- **Contrast for Readability:** If your background is dark, the text will be light. Ask the AI to "Maintain a consistent [LUMINOSITY] level across all slides to ensure text doesn't need to change colors halfway through."
- **Minimalism over Art:** A carousel background is a [SUPPORTING_ELEMENT]. If it's too beautiful or chaotic, people will look at the art and forget to read your tips.
