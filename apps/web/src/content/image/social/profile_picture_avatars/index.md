# Template: Professional & Stylized Profile Picture Avatars

Use this template to generate a high-impact "Face of the Brand" avatar. It is designed to maximize visibility in small circle frames, utilizing [SHOULDER-UP_FRAMING], [HIGH-CONTRAST_BACKGROUNDS], and [CLEAN_FACIAL_LIGHTING] to ensure you stand out in comment sections and search results.

## 📋 Prompt Template

```text
Act as a Personal Brand Consultant and Avatar Designer. Generate a prompt for a [STYLIZED_OR_PHOTOREALISTIC] profile picture of a [PERSON_DESCRIPTION].

### Brand Persona:
- **Vibe**: [e.g., Expert Tech Lead, Friendly Wellness Coach, Bold Disruptor, Whimsical Creator].
- **Industry**: [e.g., Software Engineering, High-End Real Estate, Creative Writing].

### Visual Requirements:
1. **Framing & Angle**: Use a [TIGHT_SHOULDER-UP] portrait. Ensure the [EYE_LEVEL_GAZE] is directed straight at the camera to build trust.
2. **Background Context**: Specify a [HIGH-CONTRAST_SOLID_OR_GRADIENT] background (e.g., "A vibrant solid safety-orange," "A soft-focus modern office," "A glowing blue and purple nebula").
3. **Lighting & Clarity**: Define the [FACE_LIGHTING] (e.g., "Soft wrap-around studio lighting," "Dramatic split-lighting with orange and blue highlights," "Bright, clean, shadowless butterfly lighting").
4. **Distinguishing Features**: Describe [CLOTHING_OR_ACCESSORIES] that represent the brand (e.g., "Black turtleneck and modern glasses," "A bright floral shirt," "A sleek robotic headset").
5. **Technical Specifier**: Conclude with style cues (e.g., "3D Pixar-style animation," "Photorealistic 85mm portrait," "Clean vector illustration," "Cinematic Unreal Engine 5 render").

### Output Constraints:
- Use a Bold, Charismatic, and High-Visibility tone.
- Ensure the image works perfectly when cropped into a **Circle**.
- Provide the final prompt in a copy-pasteable code block.
```

## 🧩 Variables to Fill Out

- `[STYLIZED / PHOTOREALISTIC]` - e.g., 2D Illustration, 3D Character, Professional Photo.
- `[PERSON_DESCRIPTION]` - Age, gender, ethnicity, and general expression.
- `[VIBE]` - The feeling your profile should give off.
- `[FACE_LIGHTING]` - Crucial for "popping" off the screen.

## 💡 Pro-Tips

- **The "Pop" Color:** Use a background color that contrasts with the color of the [UI_THEME] on the platform (e.g., if you're on LinkedIn/Blue, use a [YELLOW_OR_ORANGE] background).
- **Simplify the Details:** In a small circle, fine details (like complex jewelry) disappear and look like noise. Tell the AI to "Prioritize large, bold shapes and clean lines."
- **The Catch-Light:** Always include "Sharp catch-lights in the eyes." This adds the "Spark of life" that makes an avatar look professional rather than lifeless.
