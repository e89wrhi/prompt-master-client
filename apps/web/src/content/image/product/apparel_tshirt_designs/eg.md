# Examples: High-Concept Apparel Design

## 1. 90s Vintage Skate Tee ("Retro-Slide")

```text
Act as a Lead Streetwear Designer. Generate a graphic for a heavyweight boxy tee.

### Design Context:
- **Theme**: 90s West-Coast Skate Culture.
- **Subject**: A stylized, pixelated sun setting over a wireframe grid of a half-pipe.

### Visual Requirements:
1. **Artistic Style**: Lo-fi, 8-bit aesthetic with halftone dots.
2. **Ink Texture**: Distressed, crackled ink to look like a 30-year-old vintage find.
3. **Color Story**: Electric Magenta, Sunbeam Yellow, and Charcoal.
4. **Placement**: Large centered chest graphic.
5. **Technical**: Flat design, clean white background, sharp vector-like edges.

### Output Constraints:
- Creative and Brand-Focused tone.
- Copy-pasteable code block.
```

## 2. Minimalist Botanic "Fine-Line" Hoodie

```text
Act as a Graphic Artist. Design for a cream-colored hoodie.

### Design Context:
- **Theme**: Minimalist Botanical / Zen.
- **Subject**: A single stalk of lavender drawn in a continuous line ("Single Line Art").

### Visual Requirements:
- **Artistic Style**: Fine-line vector art.
- **Ink Texture**: Soft-hand water-based ink (looks like part of the fabric).
- **Placement**: Small embroidered-look graphic on the left chest and a large version on the back.
- **Palette**: Deep Forest Green on a Cream background.

### Output Constraints:
- Modern and Elegant tone.
```
