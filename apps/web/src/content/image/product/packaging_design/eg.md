# Examples: Professional Packaging Design

## 1. High-End Single-Origin Chocolate ("Noir Origins")

```text
Act as a Packaging Specialist. Generate an image for "Noir Origins" craft chocolate.

### Packaging Specs:
- **Format**: Sleek, vertical rectangular 3D box.
- **Aesthetic**: Swiss Modernist / Minimalist.

### Visual Requirements:
1. **View**: Perspective shot of the box resting on a pile of raw cocoa nibs.
2. **Typography**: Ultra-clean sans-serif "NOIR" in bold at the top; small geographic coordinates of the farm at the bottom.
3. **Materials**: Matte dark-grey paper with a gold-foil embossed map of Madagascar.
4. **Color**: Charcoal, Gold, and White.
5. **Technical**: 50mm macro shot, soft-box lighting.

### Output Constraints:
- Detail-Oriented and Sharp tone.
- Copy-pasteable code block.
```

## 2. Organic "Field-to-Table" Honey Bag

```text
Act as a Senior Brand Designer. Generate a prompt for "PureWild Honey."

### Packaging Specs:
- **Format**: 3D glass hexagonal jar with a cork lid and a wrap-around paper label.
- **Aesthetic**: Vintage Apothecary / Hand-drawn.

### Visual Requirements:
- **Label**: Textured off-white paper with hand-drawn botanical illustrations of wildflowers.
- **Typography**: Elegant classic serif.
- **Finish**: Spot UV coating on the bee-shaped logo to make it shine.

### Output Constraints:
- Brand-Focused tone.
```
