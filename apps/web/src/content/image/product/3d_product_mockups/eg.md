# Examples: Professional 3D Product Mockups

## 1. Flagship Smartphone ("Nexus X")

```text
Act as a 3D Visualization Artist. Generate a mockup for the Nexus X smartphone.

### Product Specification:
- **Material**: Deep emerald-green titanium frame with a frosted-glass back.
- **Details**: Triple-lens camera array with individual golden sapphire coatings.

### 3D Staging:
1. **Environment**: Floating in a pitch-black void, suspended by invisible wires.
2. **Lighting**: Bright cinematic rim-lighting (white) tracing the edges of the phone; soft emerald under-glow.
3. **Materials**: Highly reflective titanium edges; fingerprint-resistant matte glass.
4. **Cinematography**: Low-angle wide shot, ultra-sharp focus.
5. **Technical**: Octane Render, 8k, ray-tracing.

### Output Constraints:
- Precise and Aesthetic tone.
- Copy-pasteable code block.
```

## 2. High-End Skincare Bottle ("Lumina Serum")

```text
Act as a Senior Industrial Designer. Mockup for a luxury serum.

### Product Specification:
- **Material**: Translucent amber glass with a brushed-gold dropper cap.

### 3D Staging:
- **Environment**: Standing on a wet, dark-grey stone podium.
- **Lighting**: Soft diffused side-lighting reflecting off the liquid inside the bottle.
- **Detail**: Sub-surface scattering to show the viscosity of the amber serum.

### Output Constraints:
- Professional tone.
```
