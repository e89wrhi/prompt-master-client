# 📦 Product Image Prompts

Templates engineered for creating conceptual mockups, packaging designs, and distinct physical objects.

## Available Categories

- [**3D Product Mockups**](./3d_product_mockups/index.md): Generating high-fidelity renders (Octane, Unreal Engine style) of gadgets or bottles.
- [**Apparel & T-Shirt Designs**](./apparel_tshirt_designs/index.md): Vector-style, transparent-background graphics for merch drops.
- [**Isometric Illustrations**](./isometric_illustrations/index.md): Perfect 3D isometric angles of buildings, servers, or rooms.
- [**Packaging Design**](./packaging_design/index.md): Concept art for boxes, labels, and minimalist branding wrappers.
