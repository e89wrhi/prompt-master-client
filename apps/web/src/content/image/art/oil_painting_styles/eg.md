# Examples: Master-Level Oil Painting Styles

## 1. Style: "Dutch Master" Portrait (Rembrandt Inspired)

```text
Act as a Master Painter. Generate a prompt for an oil painting of an elderly scholar in a dark study.

### Artistic Technique:
1. **Brushwork**: Soft sfumato transitions on the face, with thick impasto highlights on the gold chains and white collar.
2. **Canvas**: Aged, heavy linen canvas with subtle craquelure.
3. **Lighting**: Dramatic Tenebrism; a single light source from the upper left illuminates only the scholar's face and hands, leaving the rest of the room in deep, warm black.
4. **Pigments**: Earthy umbers, deep ochre, and rich lead-white.
5. **Era**: 17th-century Dutch Golden Age.

### Output Constraints:
- Authoritative tone.
- Optimized for Midjourney v6.
```

## 2. Style: "French Impressionist" Landscape (Monet Inspired)

```text
Act as an Art Historian. Generate a prompt for a sunrise over a lily pond.

### Artistic Technique:
1. **Brushwork**: Short, rapid, unblended dabs of pure color (En plein air style).
2. **Lighting**: High-key, vibrant luminosity with no pure black in the shadows. 
3. **Pigments**: Ultramarine, Radiant Yellow, and Soft Viridian.
4. **Era**: Late 19th-century French Impressionism.

### Output Constraints:
- Sophisticated and Technically-accurate tone.
```
