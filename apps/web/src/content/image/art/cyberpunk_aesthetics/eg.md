# Examples: High-Fidelity Cyberpunk Aesthetics

## 1. Scene: The Underground Mod-Shop

```text
Act as a Lead Concept Artist. Generate a prompt for an interior shot of a hidden cybernetic mod-shop.

### The Cyberpunk World:
- **Location**: A basement shop underneath a crowded night market.
- **Low Life**: Dirty oil-stained concrete floors and piles of discarded robotic limbs.
- **High Tech**: A sleek, surgical-white laser-assembly arm hovering over a patient.

### Visual Requirements:
1. **Lighting**: Dim room lit only by the blue glow of data monitors and the orange sparks of a soldering iron.
2. **Atmosphere**: Heavy smoke from a cigarette and steam from an overhead pipe.
3. **Materials**: Gritty iron, sterile white plastic, and raw copper wiring.
4. **Cinematography**: Close-up macro shot of the surgical laser touching skin.

### Output Constraints:
- Noir-Inspired tone.
- Optimized for Midjourney v6.
```

## 2. Character: The "Tech-Noir" Detective

```text
Act as a Sci-Fi World Builder. Generate a prompt for a portrait of a cyberpunk detective.

### The Cyberpunk World:
- **Location**: Standing under a flickering neon billboard during a rainstorm.
- **Low Life**: Heavily scarred face, a worn-out synthetic trench coat.
- **High Tech**: A glowing hot-pink cybernetic eye reflecting the city.

### Visual Requirements:
- **Lighting**: Cinematic side-lighting from a hot-pink and cyan neon sign.
- **Surface**: Raindrops on the synthetic leather coat and skin.

### Output Constraints:
- Gritty and Descriptive tone.
```
