# Template: High-Fidelity Cyberpunk Aesthetics

Use this template to generate gritty, neon-soaked, and technologically dense cyberpunk imagery. It is designed to move beyond "neon lights" by incorporating the core philosophy of "High Tech, Low Life"—utilizing volumetric fog, rain-slicked textures, intricate cybernetic details, and a distinct noir atmosphere.

## 📋 Prompt Template

```text
Act as a Lead Concept Artist and World Builder for a Sci-Fi Epic. Generate a high-fidelity image prompt for a [SCENE_TYPE] in a cyberpunk setting.

### The Cyberpunk World:
- **Location**: [e.g., A rainy back-alley in Neo-Tokyo, a high-rise executive's penthouse, an underground mod-shop].
- **The "Low Life" Detail**: Describe one specific element of grit or decay (e.g., [CRACKED_PAVEMENT], [TANGLED_WIRES], [RUSTY_STREET_STALLS]).
- **The "High Tech" Detail**: Describe one advanced technology present (e.g., [GLOWING_HOLOGRAMS], [CUSTOME_CYBERNETICS], [FLYING_DRONES]).

### Visual Requirements:
1. **Lighting Profile**: Define the [NEON_CONTRAST] (e.g., "Cyan and Hot Pink neon reflecting off puddles," "Hard shadows and orange sodium-vapor glows").
2. **Atmospheric Effects**: Specify [VOLUMETRIC_FOG], [HEAVY_RAIN], or [BILLOWING_SMOKE] to create depth.
3. **Materials & Finishes**: Describe the [SURFACE_TEXTURES] (e.g., "Slick wet asphalt," "Brushed titanium," "Dirty synthetic plastic").
4. **Cinematography**: Define the [CAMERA_ANGLE] (e.g., "Low-angle worm's eye view," "Cinematic wide shot," "Slightly tilted Dutch angle for tension").
5. **Technical Specifier**: Conclude with rendering engine cues (e.g., "Octane Render, ray-tracing, photorealistic, 8k, bokeh background").

### Output Constraints:
- Use a Gritty, Descriptive, and Noir-Inspired tone.
- Ensure the prompt highlights the "contrast" between the glowing tech and the dark environment.
- Provide the final prompt in a copy-pasteable code block.
```

## 🧩 Variables to Fill Out

- `[SCENE_TYPE]` - e.g., Portrait of a hacker, Cityscape, Interior shop.
- `[CRACKED_PAVEMENT / TANGLED_WIRES]` - The grit.
- `[SURFACE_TEXTURES]` - How the light interacts with the world.
- `[NEON_CONTRAST]` - The colors that define the mood.
- `[CAMERA_ANGLE]` - The "Director's" view.

## 💡 Pro-Tips

- **Reflections are Everything:** Cyberpunk is defined by light hitting wet surfaces. Always include "Wet asphalt" or "Rain-slicked chrome" to maximize the neon effect.
- **Intricate Modding:** For characters, don't just say "Cyborg." Specify the [TECH_PIECE] (e.g., "Translucent fiber-optic neck cables," "Augmented golden ocular implant").
- **Cultural Fusion:** Cyberpunk often fuses cultures (e.g., Tech-Noir meets Traditional Eastern architecture). Ask the AI to "Infuse a specific cultural architectural style into the tech-city."
