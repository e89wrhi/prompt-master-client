# Examples: Evocative Abstract Art

## 1. Concept: "The Anxiety of a Digital Deadline"

```text
Act as a Visual Philosopher. Generate a prompt for a 3D Abstract Render represented the feeling of a looming digital deadline.

### Artistic Vision:
- **Concept**: The crushing pressure of time in a virtual space, the flicker of a dying monitor, the glitching of reality.
- **Core Palette**: Electric Blue, Vantablack, Neon Warning-Red.

### Composition:
1. **Shapes**: Sharp, jagged digital artifacts and pixels descending upon a fragile, glowing white core.
2. **Texture**: Static noise, glass shards, and chrome.
3. **Lighting**: Flickering fluorescent light, harsh glitches.
4. **Movement**: Chaos, a "falling" sensation.

### Output Constraints:
- Copy-pasteable code block.
- Optimized for Midjourney v6.
```

## 2. Concept: "Nostalgia for a Place Never Visited"

```text
Act as a Modern Art Critic. Generate an abstract oil painting prompt for the concept of "Anemoia" (Nostalgia for a time or place you’ve never known).

### Artistic Vision:
- **Concept**: Hazy, golden-hour memories, blurred edges, the feeling of a faded photograph.
- **Palette**: Sepia, Muted Sage, Burnt Orange.
- **Texture**: Thick impasto brushstrokes mixed with translucent washes.
- **Lighting**: Soft, diffused backlighting, "The Golden Hour."

### Output Constraints:
- Sophisticated tone.
- Optimized for DALL-E 3.
```
