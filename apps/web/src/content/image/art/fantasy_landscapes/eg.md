# Examples: Divine Fantasy Landscapes

## 1. Region: The Citadel of Floating Mirrors

```text
Act as a Master World Architect. Design a cinematic landscape prompt for a kingdom located in a sky filled with floating mirror-like islands.

### World Lore:
- **Civilization**: Celestial High-Mages.
- **The Impossibility**: Thousands of flat, polished obsidian islands floating at different heights, reflecting the clouds.

### Visual Requirements:
1. **Scale**: A wide panoramic shot showing a city with bridges connecting floating islands.
2. **Lighting**: A low sun during a permanent sunset, creating long shadows and intense orange reflections on the islands.
3. **Architecture**: Elegant, slender white marble towers with domed crystal roofs.
4. **Atmosphere**: Soft pink mist floating between the islands.
5. **Palette**: Gold, White, Soft Pink.

### Output Constraints:
- Grand and Epic tone.
- Optimized for Midjourney v6.
```

## 2. Region: The Bioluminescent Swamp (Deep Night)

```text
Act as a Fantasy Concept Artist. Design a prompt for the "Glow-Root Mire."

### World Lore:
- **Civilization**: Primitive Forest Spirits.
- **The Impossibility**: Trees that grow upside down from the clouds, with roots dangling into a glowing purple swamp.

### Visual Requirements:
- **Lighting**: Total night, illuminated only by the neon-purple swamp water and glowing blue mushrooms on the tree roots.
- **Palette**: Deep Purple, Electric Blue, Black.

### Output Constraints:
- Detailed and Magical tone.
```
