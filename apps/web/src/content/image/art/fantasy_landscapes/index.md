# Template: Divine Fantasy Landscape Design

Use this template to design world-class fantasy landscapes that feel grand, mystical, and grounded in unique lore. It is designed to move beyond "mountains and forests" by incorporating impossible geometry, magical lighting (e.g., bioluminescence, stardust), and epic architectural styles that tell a story of the civilizations that live there.

## 📋 Prompt Template

```text
Act as a Master World Architect and Fantasy Concept Artist. Design a cinematic landscape prompt for the [LANDSCAPE_REGION].

### World Lore:
- **Civilization Type**: [e.g., Ancient Elven, Brutalist Dwarf, Celestial Nomads].
- **The "Impossibility" Factor**: Describe one element that defies the laws of physics (e.g., [FLOATING_ISLANDS], [GLOWING_RIVER_OF_STARDUST], [HUGE_VERTICAL_WATERFALL_UPSIDE_DOWN]).

### Visual Requirements:
1. **The Scale**: Define the [MACRO_VIEW] (e.g., "A sprawling valley seen from a mountain peak," "A canyon of infinite depth").
2. **Magical Lighting**: Specify the source of light (e.g., "Two twin moons casting silver and teal light," "Bioluminescent flora glowing in a twilight forest," "A permanent golden hour sunset").
3. **Architectural Style**: Describe the [BUILDING_MATERIAL] and [SHAPE] (e.g., "Sleek obsidian towers with intricate gold filigree," "Ancient stone structures carved into the living roots of a world-tree").
4. **Weather & Atmosphere**: Describe the [AIR_QUALITY] (e.g., "Thick golden mist," "Swirling particles of magical ash," "Perfectly crystal clear with a view of a ringed planet in the sky").
5. **Color Palette**: List 3 [THEMATIC_COLORS] (e.g., Deep Emerald, Gold, Sapphire).

### Output Constraints:
- Use a Grand, Epic, and Detailed tone.
- Ensure the prompt feels like it belongs in a high-budget fantasy film.
- Provide the final prompt in a copy-pasteable code block.
```

## 🧩 Variables to Fill Out

- `[LANDSCAPE_REGION]` - e.g., The Valley of Whispering Mirrors, The Obsidian Wastes.
- `[CIVILIZATION_TYPE]` - Who lives here?
- `[MACRO_VIEW]` - The camera distance and depth.
- `[BUILDING_MATERIAL]` - Stone, wood, light, metal?
- `[THEMATIC_COLORS]` - Colors that bind the image together.

## 💡 Pro-Tips

- **Bioluminescence Adds Magic:** If the scene is too "normal," tell the AI to "Add glowing particles or bioluminescent plants" to instantly shift it into fantasy.
- **Architectural Contrast:** Pair a natural element with a rigid one (e.g., "A flowing river carving through a brutalist stone fortress").
- **Skybox Detail:** Don't forget the sky! Specify if there are [MULTIPLE_PLANETS], [AURORAS], or [SWIRLING_NEBULAS] visible.
