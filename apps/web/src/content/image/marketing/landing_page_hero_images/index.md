# Template: High-Conversion Landing Page Hero Images

Use this template to generate the primary "Hero" visual for websites. It is designed to visualize the "Post-Benefit World" for your users, utilizing high-contrast lighting, clean compositions, and deliberate negative space to ensure that your Headline and Call-to-Action (CTA) are the focus.

## 📋 Prompt Template

```text
Act as a Senior UI/UX Visual Designer and Conversion Artist. Generate a hero image prompt for a [PRODUCT_CATEGORY] landing page.

### Strategic Objective:
- **Core Transformation**: [THE_DREAM_STAY/THE_RESULT] (e.g., A developer achieving total focus, a user feeling financially secure).
- **Target Audience**: [DESCRIBE_AUDIENCE].

### Visual Requirements:
1. **The "Hero" Subject**: Describe the [PERSON_OR_PRODUCT] in their most aspirational state (e.g., "A serene traveler looking out at a mountain peak from a modern cabin," "A team of designers collaborating around a glowing 3D hologram").
2. **The "Overlay" Safety Zone**: Define where the negative space should be (e.g., "[LEFT_THIRD] of the image is a clean, blurred gradient for text," "[CENTER] is minimalist to allow for a centered headline").
3. **Lighting & Clarity**: Specify [HIGH_KEY/BRIGHT] or [LOW_KEY/MOODY] lighting. Ensure [RAZOR_SHARP_FOCUS] on the subject with [SOFT_BOKEH] on the background.
4. **Emotional Color Palette**: Use colors that evoke [SPECIFIC_EMOTION] (e.g., Blue/Teal for Trust, Orange/Yellow for Energy, White/Beige for Luxury).
5. **Technical Aesthetics**: Conclude with professional lens specs (e.g., "Shot on 85mm prime lens, F/1.4, editorial photography, high dynamic range, clean and modern aesthetic").

### Output Constraints:
- Use a Results-Driven, Modern, and Aspirational tone.
- Avoid busy corners or high-contrast edges where readability is needed.
- Provide the final prompt in a copy-pasteable code block.
```

## 🧩 Variables to Fill Out

- `[PRODUCT_CATEGORY]` - e.g., Fintech SaaS, Eco-Resort, AI Writing Assistant.
- `[THE_DREAM_STATE]` - The final goal the product helps achieve.
- `[LEFT_THIRD/CENTER/etc]` - The designated area for your UI elements.
- `[SPECIFIC_EMOTION]` - The psychological goal of the color palette.
- `[HIGH_KEY/LOW_KEY]` - The brightness and contrast levels.

## 💡 Pro-Tips

- **The "Look Direction" Rule:** If you use a person in your hero image, have them [LOOKING_TOWARD_THE_CTA]. The human eye naturally follows the gaze of other humans.
- **Micro-Depth:** To prevent a Hero image from looking like a flat stock photo, ask the AI to "Add subtle foreground elements (e.g., out-of-focus leaf or desk item) for a sense of 3D depth."
- **Focus on the Transformation:** Don't show the tool; show the *result* of the tool. Instead of a "Coding App," show the "Relaxed developer shipping code with a smile."
