# Examples: Landing Page Hero Images

## 1. Fintech App: "The Secure Future"

```text
Act as a Senior UI/UX Visual Designer. Generate a hero image for a Personal Finance SaaS.

### Strategic Objective:
- **Transformation**: A young professional feeling totally at peace with their savings.
- **Audience**: Millennials looking to invest.

### Visual Requirements:
1. **Hero**: A young woman sitting on a light-filled balcony, smiling at her phone, with a blurred cityscape behind her.
2. **Safety Zone**: The left 40% of the image is a minimalist bright-white wall and soft morning light, perfect for black text.
3. **Lighting**: Bright, airy, and high-key "clean" lighting.
4. **Palette**: Soft Blues, Crisp White, Warm Wood.
5. **Technical**: 85mm F/1.4, sharp focus on the person, airy afternoon vibe.

### Output Constraints:
- Aspirational and Modern tone.
- Copy-pasteable code block.
```

## 2. AI Writing Tool: "The Flow State"

```text
Act as a Conversion Artist. Generate a hero image for an AI-powered writing assistant.

### Strategic Objective:
- **Transformation**: A writer having a "lightbulb moment," with ideas flowing out.
- **Audience**: Authors and Copywriters.

### Visual Requirements:
- **Hero**: A person typing on a sleek laptop; glowing golden particles represent "ideas" floating around their head.
- **Safety Zone**: Right-justified person; large negative space on the left for a headline.
- **Palette**: Deep Purple, Navy, Golden Orange.

### Output Constraints:
- Results-Driven tone.
```
