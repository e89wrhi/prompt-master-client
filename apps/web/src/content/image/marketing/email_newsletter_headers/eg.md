# Examples: Engaging Email Newsletter Headers

## 1. Weekly AI & Tech Roundup

```text
Act as a Digital Graphic Designer. Generate a 3:1 aspect ratio header for a "Latest in AI" newsletter.

### Design Context:
- **Theme**: Futuristic tech and neural networks.
- **Brand Vibe**: Clean, Corporate, and Professional.

### Visual Requirements:
1. **Framing**: Wide panoramic. The focal point (a 3D glowing neural network orb) is on the right side.
2. **Text Safe Area**: A gradient sweep to deep charcoal black on the left side, designed for white text overlay.
3. **Subject**: A cluster of fiber-optic cables glowing with soft blue light.
4. **Palette**: Deep Navy, Electric Blue, White.
5. **Lighting**: Cinematic bioluminescence in a dark void.

### Output Constraints:
- Copy-pasteable code block.
- Modern and Functional tone.
```

## 2. Lifestyle & Wellness: "The Mindful Morning"

```text
Act as an E-mail Marketing Expert. Generate a header for a wellness newsletter.

### Design Context:
- **Newsletter Goal**: Minimalist morning rituals.
- **Style**: High-end lifestyle photography.

### Visual Requirements:
- **Focal Point**: A single ceramic cup of steaming tea on a wooden table (Right side).
- **Text Safe Area**: A blurred, soft-focus morning garden background (Left side).
- **Palette**: Sage Green, Teracotta, Off-white.

### Output Constraints:
- Clean and Minimalist tone.
```
