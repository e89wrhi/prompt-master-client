# Template: Professional Ad Campaign Visuals

Use this template to generate high-impact commercial imagery for advertising campaigns. It is designed to balance aesthetic beauty with strategic product placement, utilizing the "Emotional Anchor" technique to connect your product with a specific lifestyle or outcome.

## 📋 Prompt Template

```text
Act as a Commercial Advertising Director and Visual Brand Strategist. Generate a high-production value image prompt for a [CAMPAIGN_THEME] featuring [PRODUCT_NAME].

### Commercial Context:
- **Atmospheric Goal**: [e.g., High-energy athletics, Quiet luxury, Modern minimalism].
- **Emotional Anchor**: [WHAT_FEELING_SHOULD_THIS_EVOKE] (e.g., The thrill of speed, the peace of nature, the confidence of success).

### Visual Requirements:
1. **The Hero Subject**: Describe the [PRODUCT_OR_MODEL] in action (e.g., "A sleek running shoe hitting a rainy pavement at night," "A hand holding a frosty glass of artisanal soda").
2. **Composition & Framing**: Define the [RULE_OF_THIRDS] or [SYMMETRICAL] layout. Leave [NEGATIVE_SPACE] for potential copy/logo overlay.
3. **Lighting & Palette**: Specify the [COLOR_STORY] (e.g., "Golden hour backlighting with lens flare," "Hard blue and cyan studio lighting," "A monochrome palette with a single bright accent").
4. **Surface & Detail**: Describe the [PHYSICAL_PROPERTIES] (e.g., "Slow-motion water splash," "Sharp focus on the leather stitching," "Soft bokeh city lights in the background").
5. **Technical Specifier**: Conclude with professional photography cues (e.g., "Shot on 35mm f/1.8 lens, commercial product photography, hyper-realistic, 8k, color-graded for cinema").

### Output Constraints:
- Use a Sharp, Strategic, and Results-Oriented tone.
- Ensure the prompt highlights the "Premium" nature of the product.
- Provide the final prompt in a copy-pasteable code block.
```

## 🧩 Variables to Fill Out

- `[CAMPAIGN_THEME]` - e.g., "The Urban Explorer," "Summer Refresh," "Executive Power."
- `[PRODUCT_NAME]` - The item being advertised.
- `[WHAT_FEELING]` - The psychological trigger.
- `[NEGATIVE_SPACE]` - Indicate if you need room for text.
- `[COLOR_STORY]` - Harmonious colors that match the brand.

## 💡 Pro-Tips

- **The "Hero" Focus:** Don't clutter the image. If the product is the star, the background should be [BOKEH] (blurred) or [MINIMALIST]. Ask the AI to "Maximize the contrast between the product and the environment."
- **Lifestyle over Specs:** People buy "Cool," not "Thread count." Instead of showing a shirt, show an [ATTRACTIVE_PERSON] wearing the shirt in a [DREAM_LOCATION].
- **Texture conveys Quality:** Use words like "Brushed metal," "Hand-stitched leather," or "Frosted glass" to communicate price and quality without words.
