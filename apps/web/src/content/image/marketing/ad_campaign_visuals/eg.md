# Examples: Professional Ad Campaign Visuals

## 1. High-Performance Athletic Shoe ("Velocity Z")

```text
Act as a Commercial Advertising Director. Generate a prompt for the "Urban Speed" campaign featuring the Velocity Z running shoe.

### Commercial Context:
- **Theme**: Resilience and speed in the concrete jungle.
- **Emotion**: The feeling of unstoppable momentum.

### Visual Requirements:
1. **Hero**: A side-profile ultra-macro shot of the shoe mid-stride, hitting a shallow blue puddle.
2. **Composition**: Rule of Thirds; shoe on the right, large negative space on the left for text.
3. **Lighting**: Dark midnight setting, lit by the electric-blue glow of a nearby neon sign, creating highlights on the water splash.
4. **Detail**: Slow-motion water crowns around the sole.
5. **Technical**: Shot on Phase One XF, 100MP, commercial photorealism.

### Output Constraints:
- Strategic and Results-Oriented tone.
- Copy-pasteable code block.
```

## 2. Luxury Watch ("Aura Classic")

```text
Act as a Visual Brand Strategist. Generate a prompt for the "Heritage of Time" campaign.

### Commercial Context:
- **Theme**: Quiet luxury and legacy.
- **Emotion**: Confidence and timelessness.

### Visual Requirements:
- **Hero**: The watch resting on a mahogany desk next to a crystal glass of whiskey.
- **Lighting**: Soft, warm "Old Money" study library lighting; golden afternoon sun through a window.
- **Surface**: Sharp focus on the gold Roman numerals and the leather grain of the strap.

### Output Constraints:
- Premium and Sharp tone.
```
