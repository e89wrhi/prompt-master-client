# Template: Viral YouTube Thumbnail Visuals

Use this template to generate high-click-through-rate (CTR) thumbnail backgrounds. It is designed to utilize the "Shock and Awe" principles of YouTube—high saturation, clear facial expressions, dramatic lighting, and specific areas for high-contrast text to ensure your video stands out in a crowded feed.

## 📋 Prompt Template

```text
Act as a Viral Growth Strategist and Thumbnail Designer. Generate a high-contrast image prompt for a YouTube Thumbnail about [VIDEO_TOPIC].

### Viral Context:
- **Concept/Hook**: [e.g., I tried X for 30 days, The secret to Y, Why everyone is wrong about Z].
- **Emotional Driver**: [e.g., Shock, Curiosity, Extreme Happiness, Anger].

### Visual Requirements:
1. **The Primary Subject**: Describe a [PERSON/OBJECT] with an exaggerated [FACIAL_EXPRESSION_OR_PRACTICAL_DETAIL] (e.g., "A man with wide eyes looking in shock at a glowing computer screen," "A rusty, broken hammer next to a shiny futuristic one").
2. **Depth & Background**: Specify a [BLURRED_DYNAMIC_BACKGROUND] that adds context but doesn't distract (e.g., "A blurred office on fire," "A beautiful mountain landscape at 200% saturation").
3. **Color & Saturation**: Use [VIBRANT_PRIMARY_COLORS]. Indicate that colors should be "Hyper-saturated and high-contrast."
4. **Lighting**: Define [DRAMATIC_SIDE_LIGHTING] or [RIM_LIGHTING] to make the subject "pop" from the background (e.g., "Electric yellow rim light around the subject").
5. **Text Space Analysis**: Designate a large [UPPER_LEFT_OR_RIGHT] area that is "Clean and uncluttered for large 3D text overlay."

### Output Constraints:
- Use an Energetic, Bold, and Attention-Grabbing tone.
- Avoid small, intricate details; focus on large, bold shapes that are readable at a 120x120px scale.
- Provide the final prompt in a copy-pasteable code block.
```

## 🧩 Variables to Fill Out

- `[VIDEO_TOPIC]` - The title of your video.
- `[FACIAL_EXPRESSION]` - e.g., Jaw-dropped, furrowed brow, confident smirk.
- `[VIBRANT_COLORS]` - e.g., Safety Orange, Electric Blue, Hot Pink.
- `[RIM_LIGHTING]` - The bright light that outlines the subject.
- `[TEXT_SPACE]` - Where the giant letters will go.

## 💡 Pro-Tips

- **The "Blurry Context" Rule:** The background should tell a story but should be [GAUSSIAN_BLURRED]. If it's too sharp, the viewer's eye won't know where to look.
- **Rule of Eyes:** If a person is in the thumbnail, the [EYES] should be slightly larger and more vivid than in reality to capture human attention.
- **Contrast is King:** If your subject is dark, the background must be bright. Always ask the AI to "Maximize the luminosity difference between the foreground and background."
