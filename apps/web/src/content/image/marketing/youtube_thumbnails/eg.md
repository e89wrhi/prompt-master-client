# Examples: Viral YouTube Thumbnails

## 1. Challenge Video: "I Coded an App in 24 Hours"

```text
Act as a Viral Growth Strategist. Generate a thumbnail background for a 24-hour coding challenge.

### Viral Context:
- **Hook**: The exhaustion and the final success.
- **Emotion**: Shock and Relatable Fatigue.

### Visual Requirements:
1. **Subject**: A developer with messy hair and wide eyes, looking in shock at a laptop screen that is emitting a brilliant golden light.
2. **Background**: A messy desk with 5 empty coffee cups and 2 empty pizza boxes, heavily blurred (Gaussian blur).
3. **Color**: Hyper-saturated. Blue light from the screen vs. Orange ambient desk light.
4. **Lighting**: Bright white rim-light around the person's silhouette to separate them from the dark room.
5. **Text Space**: Large clean area on the right side for "24 HOURS" text.

### Output Constraints:
- Bold and Attention-Grabbing tone.
- Copy-pasteable code block.
```

## 2. Mystery/Education: "The Secret Why Everyone Fails at [X]"

```text
Act as a Thumbnail Designer. Generate a background for a mystery-educational video.

### Viral Context:
- **Hook**: The missing piece of the puzzle.
- **Emotion**: High Curiosity.

### Visual Requirements:
- **Subject**: A giant, 3D golden magnifying glass hovering over a complex, messy wiring diagram. 
- **Lighting**: Cinematic spotlight focus on the magnifying glass; dark shadows elsewhere.
- **Color**: Vibrant Gold and Deep Navy.
- **Text Space**: Left side designated for "STOP DOING THIS" text.

### Output Constraints:
- Energetic and Bold tone.
```
