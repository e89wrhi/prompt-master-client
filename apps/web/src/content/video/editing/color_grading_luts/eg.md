# Examples: Cinematic Color Grading & Node Trees

## 1. Golden Hour Blockbuster Look
```text
Act as a Senior Colorist. Provide a step-by-step node tree structure and primary/secondary grading parameters to achieve a Golden Hour Blockbuster look.

### Grading Pipeline:
1. **Color Space Management**: Define the input transform from ARRI LogC3 to Rec.709 Gamma 2.4.
2. **Primary Balance**: Establish the exposure curve and white balance (target 5600K).
3. **Look Development (The Grade)**:
   - Shadows: Push towards deep teal.
   - Midtones: Offset towards warm gold.
   - Highlights: Set towards soft yellow.
4. **Secondary Isolations**: 
   - Define a qualifier for skin tones and explain how to clean up the matte to avoid noise.
5. **Texture & Polish**: 
   - Apply film halation or grain targeting 35mm Kodak Vision3 soft grain.
   - Describe custom curves to ensure a soft rolloff in the highlights.

### Deliverable:
Provide this as a sequential, ordered list of nodes or layers matching standard post-production workflows.
```
