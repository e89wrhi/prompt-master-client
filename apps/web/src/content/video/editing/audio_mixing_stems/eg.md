# Examples: Professional Audio Mixing

## 1. Cinematic Documentary Mix
```text
Act as a Senior Audio Engineer. Format and apply a complete audio vocal chain and mixing workflow for a Cinematic Documentary.

### Audio Processing Requirements:
1. **Noise Reduction**: Apply noise reduction targeting steady-state AC hum, lowering the noise floor by -12dB without artifacting.
2. **Equalization (EQ)**: 
   - Apply a High-Pass filter at 80Hz.
   - Cut at 300Hz to remove muddiness.
   - Boost at 5kHz by +2dB for presence.
3. **Compression**: 
   - Target overall loudness of -23 LUFS.
   - Set attack to 5ms and release to 250ms.
   - Keep True Peak below -3dB.
4. **Ducking & Routing**: Ensure the music bed ducks underneath the primary vocal bus with a fast attack and 600ms release, targeting exactly -15dB reduction floor.

### Output Constraints:
Ensure the mixing breakdown is step-by-step and explicitly provides the exact numerical values or plugin settings needed.
```
