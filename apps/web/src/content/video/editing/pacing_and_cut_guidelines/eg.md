# Examples: Editing Pacing & Audience Retention

## 1. High-Retention YouTube Action Video
```text
Act as a Master Video Editor. Provide structural pacing rules and timeline instructions to edit a High-Retention YouTube Action Video targeting Gen-Z TikTok users.

### Cutting Rules & Pacing:
1. **The Hook**: For the first 15 seconds, ensure cuts occur every 1.5 seconds to maximize viewer retention. 
2. **A-Roll Rhythm**:
   - Trim all dead air and strictly overlap speaker dialogue using J-Cuts.
   - When a loud explosion occurs, let the shot linger for 1.5 seconds to emphasize dramatic tension.
3. **B-Roll Integration**:
   - Cut to B-Roll precisely on the kick-drum beats.
4. **Visual Dynamics**:
   - For static A-roll shots longer than 3 seconds, apply an imperceptible slow digital zoom (e.g., scale from 100% to 105%).

### Output Constraints:
Detail these instructions as an editorial style guide that can be directly mapped to NLE timeline keyframes and audio tracks.
```
