# Examples: Lighting Directives

## 1. Moody Neo-Noir Interview setup
```text
Act as a Master Gaffer and Cinematographer. Describe a Chiaroscuro Low-Key lighting setup for a Detective in a rainy office.

### Lighting Plot:
1. **Key Light**: Set a harsh, slatted light (simulating Venetian blinds) at a 45-degree angle high above the subject.
2. **Fill Light**: Use complete Negative Fill (black floppy flags) on the shadow side to establish a harsh contrast ratio of 8:1.
3. **Rim/Backlight**: Position a cool cyan light over the subject's right shoulder and hat rim to separate them from the dark background.
4. **Practical/Background**: Include a flickering warm amber desk lamp in the background to establish depth and color contrast.

### Deliverable:
Generate a textual lighting diagram and a brief explanation of how this setup emphasizes the grim, secretive mood.
```
