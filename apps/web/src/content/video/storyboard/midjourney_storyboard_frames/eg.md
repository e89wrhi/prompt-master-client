# Examples: Midjourney Storyboards

## 1. Sci-Fi Anamorphic Wide
```text
Create a Midjourney v6 prompt for a cinematic storyboard frame.

### The Scene:
An Extreme Wide Shot (EWS) of a lone astronaut in a battered white spacesuit looking up at a massive alien monolith. 
The setting is a desolate, ash-covered planet surface with two moons in the sky. 

### Cinematic Parameters:
- **Lighting**: Harsh volumetric god rays cutting through the dust. 
- **Lens & Camera**: Shot on Arri Alexa Mini with Panavision 65mm lenses, characterized by horizontal blue anamorphic lens flares. 
- **Color Grading**: Cool cyan shadows with piercing white highlights. 

### Midjourney Suffixes:
Output exactly and only the following string structure, do not explain the prompt:
`/imagine prompt: An Extreme Wide Shot (EWS) of a lone astronaut in a battered white spacesuit looking up at a massive alien monolith. The setting is a desolate, ash-covered planet surface with two moons in the sky. Harsh volumetric god rays cutting through the dust, shot on Arri Alexa Mini with Panavision 65mm lenses, characterized by horizontal blue anamorphic lens flares, cool cyan shadows with piercing white highlights --ar 2.35:1 --style raw --v 6.0`
```
