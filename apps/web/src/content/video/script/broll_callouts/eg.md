# Examples: B-Roll Callouts

## 1. High-Energy Startup Explainer
```text
Act as a Lead Visual Researcher and Video Editor. I will provide a narration script about our new FinTech platform. Identify strong anchor points in the text and generate specific B-Roll callouts.

### Constraints:
1. **Pacing**: Suggest a new B-Roll shot roughly every 3-4 seconds, matching the spoken cadence.
2. **Visual Style**: Recommend minimal UI animations, motion graphics, and Abstract 3D Renders that feel modern and optimistic.
3. **Format**: Please format the output by citing the spoken sentence in bold, followed by a bracketed visual directive.
4. **Keyword Triggers**: Prioritize abstract metaphors for speed and security over literal interpretations of the text.

Here is the script to process:
"Trading used to take days. Now, we're talking milliseconds. Your transactions aren't just fast—they're vault-secure."
```
