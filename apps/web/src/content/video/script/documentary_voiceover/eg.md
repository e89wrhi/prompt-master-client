# Examples: Documentary Voiceover

## 1. True Crime Cold Open
```text
Act as a Senior Documentary Screenwriter. Write an opening voiceover narration detailing the unsolved disappearance of a small town mayor.

### Script Requirements:
1. **Cadence & Tone**: The narration must feel haunting and tense. Use short punchy sentence structures to maintain audience engagement and gravitas.
2. **Perspective**: Write from the perspective of an objective historian narrator.
3. **Thematic Hook**: Start explicitly with a sensory description of the freezing weather to instantly grip the viewer.
4. **Directives**: Include subtle `[BEAT]` or `[LONG PAUSE]` markers where the narrator should let the visuals breathe or allow the music to swell.

### Output Constraints:
Do not use conversational web-friendly filler. Ground the dialogue in cinematic prestige. Output approximately 150 words.
```
