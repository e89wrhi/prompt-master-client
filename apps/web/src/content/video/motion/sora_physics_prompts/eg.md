# Sora & Complex Physics Examples

## 1. Liquid Dynamics & Refraction
**Prompt:**
> "Write a highly detailed text-to-video prompt specifically designed to test complex 3D engine physics.
> - **The Primary Subject:** A massive, completely transparent glass cube resting on black volcanic sand.
> - **Material Physics:** A sudden wave of thick, luminescent pink paint crashes into the cube. The paint wraps around the glass, but the center remains transparent.
> - **Ground Interaction:** The paint splashes onto the porous sand, sinking in and clumping the sand particles together.
> - **Camera Trajectory:** High-speed phantom camera orbiting 180 degrees around the impact point in slow motion.
> - **Lighting & Reflection:** Caustic light refraction twisting through the glass, with subsurface scattering revealing the thickness of the pink paint."

## 2. Zero-Gravity Particle Collision
**Prompt:**
> "Write a highly detailed text-to-video prompt specifically designed to test complex 3D engine physics.
> - **The Primary Subject:** Two floating cups of coffee in a zero-gravity space station module.
> - **Material Physics:** The dark liquid floats out of the cups in perfect spheres. The two liquid spheres collide mid-air, merging seamlessly into a larger, wobbling globus of liquid.
> - **Ground Interaction:** Tiny micro-droplets bounce off the sterile white metal walls of the station, adhering to the surface via surface tension.
> - **Camera Trajectory:** A smooth, continuous handheld push-in floating directly between the colliding droplets.
> - **Lighting & Reflection:** The harsh LED strip lights from the ceiling warp and bend within the reflection of the floating liquid sphere."
