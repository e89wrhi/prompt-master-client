# Runway/Gen-Video Prompt Examples

## 1. FPV Drone Action Shot
**Prompt:**
> "Create a precise prompt for a text-to-video diffusion model.
> - **Camera Movement:** Fast FPV racing drone shot moving forward rapidly.
> - **Subject Action:** Following a neon-green rally car drifting sharply.
> - **Environment Details:** A rain-slicked Tokyo cyberpunk alleyway with glowing puddles. Lighting: Harsh blue and pink neon reflections.
> - **Camera & Lens Setup:** 14mm ultra-wide lens, GoPro Hero 11 style.
> - **Aesthetic:** High-octane, hyper-realistic, heavy motion blur on edges."

## 2. Subtle Emotional Macro
**Prompt:**
> "Create a precise prompt for a text-to-video diffusion model.
> - **Camera Movement:** Extremely slow push-in (dolly in).
> - **Subject Action:** A single tear rolling down the weathered cheek of an elderly fisherman.
> - **Environment Details:** Inside a dark, wooden ship cabin. Lighting: Soft morning light spilling across the face from a window.
> - **Camera & Lens Setup:** 85mm macro lens, shallow depth of field, Arri Alexa.
> - **Aesthetic:** High dynamic range, cinematic, subtle skin texture, Kodak Portra 400."
