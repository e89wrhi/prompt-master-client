# 🎨 Visual Art Prompts

Templates designed to interact with image generation AIs (like Midjourney, DALL-E, and Stable Diffusion) using specific aesthetic parameters.

## Available Categories

- [**DALL-E Concept Art**](./dalle_concept_art/index.md): Narrative-driven environmental and character concept generation.
- [**Logo Design Prompts**](./logo_design_prompts/index.md): Vector-style, minimal, and typography-focused generation.
- [**Midjourney Prompts**](./midjourney_prompts/index.md): Complex aspects ratios, lighting terms, and camera lens setups.
- [**Stable Diffusion Parameters**](./stable_diffusion_parameters/index.md): Negative prompts, CFG scale advice, and sampler tweaks.
- [**Style Transfer**](./style_transfer/index.md): Explaining specific artistic movements or artists to the model.
