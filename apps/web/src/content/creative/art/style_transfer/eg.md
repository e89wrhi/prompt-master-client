# Examples: Artistic Style Transfer

## 1. Modern City (Van Gogh Style)

```text
Act as a Visual Remix Specialist. Apply the style of Van Gogh's "Starry Night" to a modern cityscape of Manhattan.

### The Remix Logic:
1. **Subject**: The Manhattan skyline with the Empire State Building.
2. **Markers**:
   - Short, thick impasto brushstrokes.
   - Swirling glowing yellow starlight.
   - Deep cobalt blue and indigo sky.
3. **Harmonization**: The buildings should appear to be swaying and flowing with the energy of the swirls.

### Output Constraints:
- Style transfer, artistic synthesis.
```

## 2. Cyberpunk Classical Portrait

```text
Act as an Artistic Strategist. Apply a "Cyberpunk Neon" style to a portrait in the style of Johannes Vermeer.

### The Remix Logic:
- **Marker**: Single source side-lighting (Chiaroscuro) but the light source is a neon pink sign.
- **Medium**: Oil on panel but with glowing digital artifacts.

### Output Constraints:
- Masterwork execution.
```
