# Examples: DALL-E Concept Art

## 1. The Ancient Archive (Steampunk Space)

```text
Act as a Lead Concept Artist. Generate a scene of a massive library in space.

### Concept Strategy:
- **Vibe**: Peaceful discovery.
- **Character**: A small robot with a glowing lens shelf-stacking huge, dusty books that are floating in zero gravity.
- **Style**: Painterly digital art with rich textures.

### Visual Requirements:
- A massive scale; millions of books drifting through the void.
- Soft, golden starlight filtering through a distant window.
- Deep Indigo, Gold, and Dusty Brown.

### Output Constraints:
- Narrative and Professional tone.
```

## 2. Cybernetic Jungle Stand-off

```text
Act as a Visual Storyteller. Generate a gritty cinematic concept art scene.

### Concept Strategy:
- **Action**: Two rival tribes facing off in a bioluminescent jungle.
- **Focus**: The lead warriors are both half-machine and half-human.

### Output Constraints:
- Wide aspect ratio, intricate and detailed.
```
