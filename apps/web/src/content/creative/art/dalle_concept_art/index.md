# Template: DALL-E Concept Art (Narrative & Detail)

Use this template to leverage DALL-E's advanced understanding of natural language and complex scene composition. Unlike other models, DALL-E excels at following [SPECIFIC_INSTRUCTIONS] about character relationships, text inclusion, and narrative storytelling, allowing for consistent world-building.

## 📋 Prompt Template

```text
Act as a Lead Concept Artist and Visual Storyteller. Generate a high-detail concept art scene of [SCENE_DESCRIPTION].

### Concept Strategy:
- **Narrative Vibe**: [e.g., A tense moment before a battle, a peaceful discovery of an ancient relic, a crowded neon-market].
- **Character Focus**: [e.g., A lone knight looking up at a dragon, two robots trading a secret].
- **Artistic Style**: [e.g., Painterly digital art, gritty cinematic concept art, vibrant storybook illustration].

### Visual Requirements:
1. **The Primary Action**: Describe exactly [WHAT_IS_HAPPENING] using evocative, natural language.
2. **Atmospheric Lighting**: Define the [MOOD_LIGHT] (e.g., "Deep blue twilight with glowing orange sparks," "Harsh dusty sunlight through a window").
3. **Texture & Scale**: Emphasize the [SIZE_OF_THE_WORLD] (e.g., "The massive scale of the mountain vs the tiny human," "Intricate carvings on the weapon").
4. **Color Story**: Specify 3 [DOMINANT_COLORS].

### Output Constraints:
- Use Descriptive, Narrative, and Professional tone.
- Avoid technical jargon like "8k" or "vray"—DALL-E responds better to [VISUAL_METAPHORS].
- Conclude with "Intricate and detailed concept art, wide aspect ratio [IF_APPLICABLE]."
```

## 🧩 Variables to Fill Out

- `[SCENE_DESCRIPTION]` - The core idea.
- `[WHAT_IS_HAPPENING]` - The action/verb of the scene.
- `[MOOD_LIGHT]` - The emotional atmospheric setting.

## 💡 Pro-Tips

- **DALL-E Loves Characters:** DALL-E is great at placing [SPECIFIC_OBJECTS] in hands. Instead of "A man with a sword," say "A man tightly gripping a rusted broadsword that is glowing with a faint blue light."
- **Story over Tech:** Focus on the [STORY_BEHIND_THE_IMAGE]. Tell the AI *why* the scene is happening. "The knight is exhausted and leaning on his spear" works better than "exhausted knight."
- **Consistent Characters:** To keep characters consistent across prompts, describe their [DISTINCT_FEATURE] in every prompt (e.g., "A bright red scarf").
