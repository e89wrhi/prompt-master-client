# Template: Professional Logo Design Prompts

Use this template to generate clean, scalable, and iconic logo concepts. It is designed to act as a Brand Identity Designer—utilizing [FLAT_DESIGN], [VECTOR_STYLE], and [NEGATIVE_SPACE] to ensure the resulting logo is modern, recognizable, and works in both color and monochrome.

## 📋 Prompt Template

```text
Act as a Senior Brand Identity Designer. Generate a logo design for [COMPANY_NAME], a [INDUSTRY] company.

### Brand Identity:
- **Logo Type**: [e.g., Minimalist pictorial mark, geometric monogram, abstract logo, negative space logo].
- **Core Aesthetic**: [e.g., Sleek and modern, bold and playful, elegant and luxury, technical and high-end].
- **Icon Metaphor**: [e.g., An owl representing wisdom, a rising sun for growth, an abstract interlocking 'N'].

### Visual Requirements:
1. **Geometric Foundations**: Describe the [SYMMETRY_AND_FORM] (e.g., "Constructed using perfect circles," "Sharp 45-degree angles," "Soft flowing curves").
2. **Color Palette**: Specify a [LIMITED_PALETTE] (e.g., "Flat black and white," "Electric blue and charcoal," "Gold and deep green").
3. **Presentation**: Place the logo on a [SOLID_BACKGROUND] (Usually "Solid white background" or "Solid black background") for maximum clarity.
4. **Style Keywords**: Include [FLAT_VECTOR], [MINIMALIST], [SYMMETRICAL], [HIGH_CONTRAST].

### Output Constraints:
- Minimalist, Iconic, and Professional tone.
- Avoid 3D effects, shadows, or complex textures—focus on [SCALABLE_SILHOUETTES].
- Provide a clean version without any mockups or text unless requested.
```

## 🧩 Variables to Fill Out

- `[COMPANY_NAME]` - The brand.
- `[ICON_METAPHOR]` - What are we representing?
- `[FLAT_VECTOR]` - The secret to a "Real" logo look in AI.
- `[SOLID_BACKGROUND]` - Crucial for background removal later.

## 💡 Pro-Tips

- **The "No Mockups" Rule:** AI models love putting logos on "Realistic T-shirts" or "Storefronts." If you want just the logo, add the phrase: "Vector style, isolated on white background, no mockups, no 3D effects."
- **Focus on the Silhouette:** A great logo must be recognizable as a solid black shape. Ask for "High contrast, bold silhouette."
- **Negative Space Magic:** If you want a "Hidden" logo (like Fed-Ex), ask for a "Negative space logo incorporating [ELEMENT_1] and [ELEMENT_2]."
