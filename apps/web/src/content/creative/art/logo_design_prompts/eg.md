# Examples: Professional Logo Design

## 1. Tech Startup: "FinFlow" (Minimalist Wave)

```text
Act as a Senior Brand Identity Designer. Generate a logo for "FinFlow," a modern fintech app.

### Brand Identity:
- **Logo Type**: Minimalist pictorial mark.
- **Icon Metaphor**: An abstract "F" that looks like a flowing stream or wave.
- **Aesthetic**: Sleek and modern.

### Visual Requirements:
- Constructed using perfect circles and golden ratio proportions.
- Flat black and electric blue.
- Solid white background.
- Flat vector, symmetrical, no 3D effects.

### Output Constraints:
- Iconic and Professional tone.
```

## 2. Coffee Shop: "The Green Leaf" (Negative Space)

```text
Act as a Logo Designer. Generate a negative space logo.

### Brand Identity:
- **Icon Metaphor**: A coffee bean that hides a small leaf inside it using negative space.
- **Palette**: Dark espresso brown and sage green.

### Output Constraints:
- Isolated on white background, no mockups.
```
