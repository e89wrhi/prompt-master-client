# Template: Emotional Chord Progression Ideation

Use this template to architect the harmonic foundation of a song. It is designed to act as a Music Theorist and Composer—mapping specific [EMOTIONAL_STATES] to [CHORD_VOICINGS] and [HARMONIC_STRUCTURES], utilizing the Nashville Number System and advanced concepts like "Borrowed Chords" to create progressions that feel fresh and evocative.

## 📋 Prompt Template

```text
Act as a Music Theorist and Professional Composer. Design a chord progression for a [GENRE] track.

### Composition Context:
- **Desired Emotion**: [e.g., Melancholic hope, sudden tension, euphoric release, nostalgic warmth].
- **Key Signature**: [e.g., C Minor, A Major, E Phrygian].
- **Track Vibe**: [e.g., Lofi chill, epic orchestral, aggressive synthwave].

### Harmonic Framework:
1. **The Progression (Nashville Numbers)**: Provide the core [SEQUENCE] (e.g., i - VI - III - VII).
2. **The Voicing Detail**: Suggest specific [EXTENSIONS] (e.g., "Use a Major 7th for the IV chord," "Add an 11th to the ii chord for a jazzy textures").
3. **The "Surprise" Chord**: Introduce one [BORROWED_CHORD] or [SECONDARY_DOMINANT] to break the repetitive cycle and add intellectual depth.
4. **Rhythmic Suggestions**: Describe the [STRUMMING/PLUCKING_PATTERN] (e.g., "Synchronized syncopation," "Long sustained whole notes with a slow attack").
5. **The Bridge Variation**: How does the progression change to create [CONTRAST] in the second half?

### Output Constraints:
- Use a Technical, Imaginative, and Precise tone.
- Present the chords in a clear horizontal list (e.g., Am7 -> Dm9 -> G13).
- Conclude with "Theory Tip"—why this specific progression triggers the [DESIRED_EMOTION].
```

## 🧩 Variables to Fill Out

- `[EMOTIONAL_STATES]` - The primary "Feel" of the track.
- `[CHORD_VOICINGS]` - The "Vertical" structure of the chord.
- `[NASHVILLE_NUMBERS]` - The Roman Numeral representation.
- `[BORROWED_CHORD]` - Taking a chord from a parallel scale.

## 💡 Pro-Tips

- **Major vs. Minor is only the Start:** For true emotion, focus on [MODES]. Ask the AI: "Utilize the [DORIAN_MODE] for a 'Soulful/Cool' feeling or the [LYDIAN_MODE] for a 'Dreamy/Magical' feeling."
- **Voice Leading is King:** The best progressions feel like a [CONVERSATION]. Ask the AI to: "Ensure smooth voice leading where each note in the chord only moves a small distance to the next."
- **Inversions add Elegance:** Don't just play root position. Ask for "[FIRST_INVERSION]" or "[SECOND_INVERSION]" to create a more fluid bassline.
