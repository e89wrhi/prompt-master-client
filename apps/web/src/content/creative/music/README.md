# 🎵 Music & Audio Generation

Prompts focused on audio generation AI (Suno, Udio) or generating structured musical theory and lyrics.

## Available Categories

- [**Chord Progression Ideation**](./chord_progression_ideation/index.md): Generating jazz, pop, or unconventional chord structures.
- [**Genre Blending**](./genre_blending/index.md): Instructions for combining two distinct styles (e.g., Synthwave x Bluegrass).
- [**Lyric Writing**](./lyric_writing/index.md): Structure rules (AABA, Verse/Chorus) and rhyme schemes.
- [**Suno Song Generation**](./suno_song_generation/index.md): Optimizing metatags and generic prompts for Suno.ai.
