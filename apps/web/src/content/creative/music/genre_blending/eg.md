# Examples: Experimental Genre Blending

## 1. "Cyber-Cello" (Cyberpunk + Classical)

```text
Act as a Music Producer. Architect a blend between "Synthwave/Cyberpunk" and "Neo-Classical Cello."

### Genre DNA:
- **Cyberpunk**: Aggressive distorted sub-bass, 100bpm mechanical pulse, bit-crushed leads.
- **Classical**: Staccato cello plucking, emotional vibrato, wide orchestral room reverb.

### Blending Strategy:
- **Rhythms**: Mechanical steampunk clockwork clicks acting as the percussion.
- **Melody**: A solo cello that has been run through a heavy distortion pedal.
- **Secret Sound**: A digitized female opera voice that "glitches" into static.

### Output Constraints:
- Experimental and Production-Focused tone.
- Conclude with Track Title and "First 30 Seconds" description.
```

## 2. "Reggae-Industrial" (Dub + Metal)

```text
Act as a Sound Designer. Blend "Deep Roots Reggae" with "Industrial Metal."

### Framework:
- Reggae: One-drop drum beat, off-beat skank guitar.
- Industrial: Industrial metal clanging, high-gain guitars.
- Bridge: The "Skank" guitar is replaced by a high-gain industrial saw synth.

### Output Constraints:
- Sharp tone.
```
