# Examples: AI Music Architecture

## 1. 80s Synthpop (High Energy)

```text
Act as an AI Music Architect for Suno.

### Style Prompt:
80s Synthpop, Retro-wave, Euphoric, 120bpm, Analog Synths, Linn-Drum machine, Pristine production.

### Structural Arrangement:
[Intro] Filtered synth pulsating.
[Verse 1] Male baritone, robotic but emotional.
[Chorus] Huge wall of sound, bright FM synths, heavy gated reverb on drums.
[Outro] Long synth fade.

### Output Constraints:
- Copy-pasteable into Suno.
- Technical and High-Energy tone.
```
