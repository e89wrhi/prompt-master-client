# Examples: Emotional Lyric Writing

## 1. Country Ballad: "Dust on the Dashboard"

```text
Act as a Senior Songwriter. Draft a country ballad about leaving a small town.

### Song Context:
- **Emotion**: Nostalgic sadness.
- **Metaphor**: The dust settling on a dashboard as a representation of time.
- **Influence**: Chris Stapleton / Kacey Musgraves.

### Lyric Structure:
- Verse 1: "The screen door's still swinging..." (Focus on the house).
- Chorus: Simple ABAB rhyme. Syllable count: 8-6-8-6.

### Output Constraints:
- Poetic and Raw tone.
- Songwriter's Note: "Deliver with a slight gravelly crack in the voice."
```

## 2. Rap Verse: "Silicon Valley Noir"

```text
Act as a Lyricist. Draft a complex rap verse about the dark side of tech.

### Framework:
- Metaphor: A computer motherboard as a maze.
- Rhythm: Fast triplet flow, heavy internal rhyme.

### Output Constraints:
- Use [INTERNAL_RHYMES].
- Sharp and Cynical tone.
```
