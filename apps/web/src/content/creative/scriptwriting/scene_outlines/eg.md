# Examples: High-Tension Scene Outlines

## 1. The Rooftop Escape (Action/Thriller)

```text
Act as a Story Producer. Outline a beat-by-beat escape scene.

### Scene Context:
- **Location**: A rain-slicked Gotham-style rooftop at midnight.
- **Goal**: To reach the helicopter before the police swarm the roof.
- **Obstacle**: A jammed door and a slippery ledge.

### Beats:
1. **Hook**: Character slams against the rooftop door; it won't budge.
2. **Escalation**: 
   - Police sirens get louder.
   - They try to jump to the next ledge, but the rain makes it like ice.
   - They lose their bag over the side.
3. **Turn**: They find a maintenance cable instead of jumping.
4. **Resolution**: They swing across, but the cable starts to pull from the wall.

### Output Constraints:
- Value Shift: Started [+] (On the roof), Ended [-] (Dangling).
```

## 2. The Awkward Dinner (Cringe Comedy)

```text
Act as a Screenwriter. 

### Context:
- **Location**: A 5-star restaurant.
- **Goal**: Propose without the ex-girlfriend seeing.
- **Obstacle**: The ex-girlfriend is the waitress.

### Output Constraints:
- Suspenseful (in a comedy way) and Action-Oriented.
```
