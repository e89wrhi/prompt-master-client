# Examples: Hard-Rule World Building

## 1. World: "Neon-Oasis" (Sci-fi)

```text
Act as a World Architect. Build a framework for "Neon-Oasis," a city inside a giant biodome on a frozen planet.

### The Rules:
1. **Constraint**: Oxygen is a currency. You have to "Earn" your breaths through labor.
2. **Power**: Cybernetic implants exist but they require "Oxygen Packs" to run, making combat very expensive.
3. **Hierarchy**: The "Oxygen Barons" live at the top of the dome.
4. **Geography**: The "Perma-Frost" outside the dome kills in seconds.

### Output Constraints:
- Systematic tone.
- The Iceberg Tip: A description of an Oxygen credit card.
```

## 2. World: "The Echo-Lands" (Fantasy)

```text
Act as a Narrative Systems Designer. Build a world where magic is sound-based.

### Rules:
- Magic cost: Using magic causes permanent hearing loss.
- Taboo: Whistling in a public place.

### Output Constraints:
- Imaginative and Objective tone.
```
