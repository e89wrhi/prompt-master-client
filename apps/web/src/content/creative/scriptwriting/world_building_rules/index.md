# Template: Hard-Rule World Building (Consistency & Depth)

Use this template to architect a cohesive world for your story. It is designed to act as a World Architect and Systems Designer—moving beyond "Vague Settings" and into [INTERNAL_LOGIC], [ECONOMIC_SISTEMS], and [HARD_CONSTRAINTS] that ensure your world feels lived-in, believable, and full of conflict-generating limitations.

## 📋 Prompt Template

```text
Act as a World Architect and Narrative Systems Designer. Build a foundational framework for a [GENRE] world called [WORLD_NAME].

### The "Rules of the Reality":
1. **The Core Constraint**: Identify the one [LIMITATION] that defines life in this world (e.g., "No electricity exists," "Water is more valuable than gold," "Everyone can hear everyone else's thoughts").
2. **The Magic/Power System (Hard Rules)**: Define the [COST_OF_POWER]. If someone uses magic/tech, what do they lose? (e.g., "Energy," "Sanity," "Lifespan").
3. **The Socio-Economic Hierarchy**: Who has the [POWER] and why? (e.g., "The Technomancers who control the battery mines").
4. **Geography as Conflict**: Describe one [GEOGRAPHICAL_BARRIER] that prevents people from moving or communicating (e.g., "The Poisoned Sea," "The Vertical Mountains").
5. **The Taboo**: Identify the one thing that is [STRICTLY_FORBIDDEN] on pain of death.

### Output Constraints:
- Use an Imaginative, Systematic, and Objective tone.
- Conclude with "The Iceberg Tip"—a description of a common [EVERYDAY_OBJECT] and how it is different in this world.
- Present the framework in an easy-to-read [SYSTEM_GUIDE] format.
```

## 🧩 Variables to Fill Out

- `[WORLD_NAME]` - The name of your setting.
- `[LIMITATION]` - The soul of the conflict.
- `[COST_OF_POWER]` - Preventing "invincible" characters.
- `[TABOO]` - The cultural boundaries.

## 💡 Pro-Tips

- **The "Cost" is More Important than the "Power":** A magic system is only interesting if it has [LIMITS]. Ask the AI to: "Identify 3 ways the power system can [FAIL_OR_BACKFIRE]."
- **Build from the Bottom Up:** Don't just build the Kings and Queens. Ask for: "A description of a [STREET_LEVEL_MEAL] in this world." It makes the world feel grounded.
- **The "Why" of Technology:** If there are flying cars, why aren't there [FLYING_DELIVERY_PIZZAS]? Ensure the tech has a logical [REASON_FOR_EXISTENCE] in the economy.
