# Examples: Minimalist Haiku Generation

## 1. Moment: "First Snow of Winter"

```text
Act as a Zen Master. Generate 3 Haikus about the first snowfall.

### Haiku Logic:
1. **5-7-5**: Strictly enforced.
2. **Kigo**: Frost or frozen ground.
3. **Kireji**: Contrast the silence of the snow with a sudden sound (a branch snapping).

### Output Constraints:
- Calm and Observational tone.
- Syllable count verified.
```

## 2. Image: "Late Night City Traffic"

```text
Act as a Minimalist Poet.

### Logic:
- Kigo: Heat (Summer night).
- Focus: The red trail of taillights.
- Constraint: No metaphors, just literal truth.

### Output Constraints:
- Profund tone.
```
