# Examples: Classical Sonnet Structuring

## 1. Theme: "The Cruelty of Time"

```text
Act as a Renaissance Poet. Compose a Sonnet about how time destroys beauty.

### Poetic Architecture:
- Meter: Iambic Pentameter.
- Rhyme: ABAB CDCD EFEF GG.
- Volta: In the final couplet, argue that poetry makes beauty immortal.
- Imagery: Summer fading into winter, the scythe of the reaper.

### Output Constraints:
- Sophisticated and Romantic tone.
- Mark the stress pattern for the last line.
- Conclude with "The Argument."
```

## 2. Theme: "The Broken Smartphone" (Modern Twist)

```text
Act as a Master of Meter. Write a sonnet about a shattered screen in Renaissance language.

### Architecture:
- Metaphor: The screen as a "Spider's silver web" or "Glassy heaven cracked."
- Volta: The device is dead, but the world goes on.

### Output Constraints:
- Authoritative tone.
```
