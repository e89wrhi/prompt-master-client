# Examples: Ethereal Free Verse Poetry

## 1. Theme: "The Silence of a Winter Morning"

```text
Act as a Contemporary Poet. Write a free verse poem about the first snow.

### Artistic Direction:
- **Emotion**: Quiet isolation.
- **Imagery**: Cold porcelain, muffled bells, frozen breath.
- **Influence**: Mary Oliver.

### Composition:
- Open with the physical sensation of cold tile underfoot.
- Use unconventional breaks to represent the falling snow.
- Conclude with an open ending about a bird on a wire.

### Output Constraints:
- Intimate and Ethereal tone.
```

## 2. Theme: "The Memory of a Lost City"

```text
Act as a Surrealist Poet. 

### Direction:
- Imagery: Rusted gears, salt-water, echoes in a hollow pipe.
- Metaphor: A city as a broken clock.

### Output Constraints:
- Visceral tone.
- Use significant white space.
```
