# Template: High-Impact Flash Fiction (The 500-Word Challenge)

Use this template to write tight, emotionally resonant short stories. It is designed to act as a Short Story Author and Minimalist—utilizing the "In Media Res" (starting in the middle) technique, focusing on a single [PIVOTAL_MOMENT], and delivering a [HIGH-IMPACT_ENDING] that changes the reader's perception of the entire story.

## 📋 Prompt Template

```text
Act as a Master of Flash Fiction and Minimalist Storytelling. Write a [STORY_GENRE] flash fiction story entitled "[STORY_TITLE]."

### Story Constraints:
- **Maximum Length**: 500 words.
- **The Hook**: Start "In Media Res"—no long introductions. The first sentence must contain [IMMEDIATE_TENSION].
- **The Focal Point**: The entire story should revolve around a single [OBJECT/MOMENT/CONVERSATION].

### Narrative Requirements:
1. **Sensory Anchoring**: Use 3 specific [SENSORY_DETAILS] (e.g., "The smell of burnt toast," "The cold vibration of the ship's floor").
2. **The "Unspoken"**: Include a detail that hints at a larger [BACKSTORY] without explaining it.
3. **The Turn**: In the middle of the story, shift the [EMOTIONAL_TONE] (e.g., from hope to dread).
4. **The Final Sentence**: End on a [STUNNING_IMAGE_OR_REVEAL] that makes the reader re-examine everything they just read.

### Output Constraints:
- Use a Sharp, Evocative, and Concise tone.
- Do NOT use adverbs or filler descriptions.
- Conclude with "The After-Image"—a 1-sentence description of the feeling the story should leave behind.
```

## 🧩 Variables to Fill Out

- `[STORY_TITLE]` - The name of the piece.
- `[STORY_GENRE]` - e.g., Sci-fi, Gothic Horror, Slice of Life.
- `[PIVOTAL_MOMENT]` - The core of the story.
- `[SENSORY_DETAILS]` - Grounding the reader.

## 💡 Pro-Tips

- **Kill Your Darlings:** Every word must earn its place. If a sentence doesn't [DRIVE_THE_PLOT_OR_REVEAL_CHARACTER], delete it. Ask the AI to: "Identify and remove the 20% most 'flowery' sentences."
- **Focus on the "Small":** A flash fiction story about "The end of the world" is too big. A story about "[A_HALF-EATEN_MEAL_DURING_THE_END_OF_THE_WORLD]" is perfect.
- **The Emotional Arc:** Even in 500 words, the protagonist must [INTERNAL_SHIFT]. Ask for: "A change in the character's internal conviction by the last paragraph."
