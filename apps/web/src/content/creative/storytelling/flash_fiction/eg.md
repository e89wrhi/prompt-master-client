# Examples: High-Impact Flash Fiction

## 1. Sci-Fi: "The Last Message from Mars"

```text
Act as a Master of Flash Fiction. Write a 400-word sci-fi story.

### Story Constraints:
- **Title**: The Last Message from Mars.
- **Hook**: "The red dust was finally winning, clogging the filters that had kept us alive for a decade."
- **Focal Point**: An old radio transmitter that is losing power.

### Requirements:
- Sensory: The taste of metallic recycled air.
- The Turn: Changing from fear of death to acceptance of a new beginning.
- Ending: A reveal about who is actually listening on Earth.

### Output Constraints:
- Sharp and Evocative tone.
- Conclude with "The After-Image."
```

## 2. Gothic Horror: "The Mirror on the Third Floor"

```text
Act as a Minimalist Storyteller. 

### Constraints:
- Genre: Gothic Horror.
- Hint at backstory: A missing sister.
- Ending: The reflection moves a second before the person does.

### Output Constraints:
- Concise tone.
```
