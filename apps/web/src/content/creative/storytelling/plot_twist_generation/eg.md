# Examples: Strategic Plot Twist Generation

## 1. Murder Mystery: "The Killer's Ghost"

```text
Act as a Mystery Author. Generate 3 plot twists for a story about a detective hunting a serial killer who has been dead for 20 years.

### Twist Architecture:
1. **Assumed Truth**: The detective is the only one who can see the "ghost."
2. **Reveal**: The "detective" is actually the killer's son who has inherited his memories through a secret lab procedure.
3. **Foreshadowing**: He has a scar that matches the killer's; he knows details about the victims that weren't in the files.

### Output Constraints:
- Shock Value: 9/10.
- Calculating and Suspenseful tone.
```

## 2. Sci-Fi: "The Generation Ship"

```text
Act as a Thriller Specialist. The premise: 1000 people on a ship to Alpha Centauri.

### Twists:
- Environmental: The "Windows" are just screens, and the ship never actually left Earth. They are in an underground bunker for a social experiment.
- Motivation: The "Captain" is a robot designed to test human endurance.

### Output Constraints:
- Logically consistent with the premise.
```
