# Template: Deep Mythos & Lore Creation

Use this template to build the history, religion, and culture of a fictional world. It is designed to act as an Archivist and Historian—utilizing [AGES_OF_HISTORY], [FACTION_MYTHS], and [CULTURAL_PROVERBS] to create a sense of depth and ancient mystery that makes your world feel far larger than the story itself.

## 📋 Prompt Template

```text
Act as a Master Historian and Fictional Archivist. Develop the Lore for [FACTION/PLACE/OBJECT] in the world of [WORLD_NAME].

### Lore Framework:
1. **The Foundation Myth**: Describe the [ORIGIN_STORY] (e.g., "The God of Stone gave his heart to build the first city").
2. **The 3 Ages of History**: Provide a brief summary of the [TIMELINE]:
   - **The Age of Order**: A time of zenith.
   - **The Age of Discord**: A great war or disaster.
   - **The Current Age**: The "Silent Now."
3. **Artifact of Power**: Describe one [OBJECT] associated with this lore and its [CURRENT_STATUS] (e.g., "The Broken Crown, lost in the Southern Sea").
4. **Cultural Proverbs**: Provide 3 "Sayings" that common people use (e.g., "Never trust the wind when the sky is red").
5. **The Secret Truth**: Reveal one [FACT] that the people of this world have forgotten but is historically true.

### Output Constraints:
- Use a Formal, Mythic, and Detailed tone.
- Format the history in a [CHRONOLOGICAL_TIMELINE].
- Conclude with "A Relic from the Past"—a description of a single physical item found by the protagonist that proves this lore is real.
```

## 🧩 Variables to Fill Out

- `[FACTION / PLACE / OBJECT]` - The focus of the lore.
- `[ORIGIN_STORY]` - The religious or historical start.
- `[THE_SILENT_NOW]` - The current state of the world.
- `[CULTURAL_PROVERBS]` - Bringing the lore to life for everyday people.

## 💡 Pro-Tips

- **History is Written by the Victors:** Ask the AI to: "Provide the [OFFICIAL_VERSION] of the history and then the [HIDDEN_TRUE_VERSION] known only to the outcasts."
- **Use the "Rule of Three":** People remember things in threes. 3 Gods, 3 Wars, 3 Artifacts. Ask the AI to: "Structure the [RELIGION] around a trinity of forces."
- **Lore should impact Gameplay/Plot:** Lore isn't just flavor. Ask for: "One way this lore [CREATES_A_CONFLICT] for the protagonist in the current time."
