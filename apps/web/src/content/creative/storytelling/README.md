# 📖 Storytelling & Prose

Templates for novelists, short story writers, and interactive fiction creators.

## Available Categories

- [**Flash Fiction**](./flash_fiction/index.md): Generating high-impact stories under 500 words.
- [**Interactive Fiction**](./interactive_fiction/index.md): Setting up CYOA (Choose Your Own Adventure) and text adventure games.
- [**Lore Creation**](./lore_creation/index.md): Generating histories, mythos, and item descriptions.
- [**Plot Twist Generation**](./plot_twist_generation/index.md): Generating unforeseen consequences or red herrings.
