# 🧠 Brainstorming & Ideation

Templates explicitly designed to make the AI act as a creative sparring partner, bouncing unconventional ideas without being purely derivative.

## Available Categories

- [**Lateral Thinking Exercises**](./lateral_thinking_exercises/index.md): Prompts to force the AI out of standard thought patterns.
- [**Mind Mapping Prompts**](./mind_mapping_prompts/index.md): Generating branching concepts or Markdown-compatible mind maps.
- [**Name Generation**](./name_generation/index.md): Generating SaaS names, fantasy character names, or book titles with specific rules.
- [**Product Ideation**](./product_ideation/index.md): Feature brainstorming using SCAMPER and other frameworks.
