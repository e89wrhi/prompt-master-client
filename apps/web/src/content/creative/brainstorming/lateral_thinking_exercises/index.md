# Template: Lateral Thinking & Creative Problem Solving

Use this template to break through mental blocks and find unconventional solutions to stubborn problems. It is designed to utilize Edward de Bono's "Lateral Thinking" techniques—challenging established [ASSUMPTIONS], using [RANDOM_STIMULI], and moving "sideways" to find paths that logical, linear thinking often misses.

## 📋 Prompt Template

```text
Act as a Creative Strategist and Expert in Lateral Thinking. Help me find an unconventional solution for the following [PROBLEM/CHALLENGE].

### Thinking Frameworks:
1. **The Assumption Shaker**: Identify 3 "Hard Rules" we usually follow for [CHALLENGE] and explain what happens if we [BREAK_THEM].
2. **The "Random Word" Association**: Pick a random, unrelated noun (e.g., "Bicycle," "Cloud," "Anchor") and force a connection to the [PROBLEM]. How does this new perspective change our approach?
3. **The Stepping Stone**: Suggest one [ABSURD_OR_IMPOSSIBLE_IDEA] and use it as a bridge to find a practical, innovative one.
4. **The "Reverse-Logic" Path**: How would we intentionally make this [PROBLEM] worse? Now, reverse those steps to find a unique improvement.

### Output Constraints:
- Use an Imaginative, Provocative, and Strategic tone.
- Avoid obvious or "cliché" business advice.
- Conclude with "The Wildcard"—one final idea that is 80% crazy and 20% brilliant.
```

## 🧩 Variables to Fill Out

- `[PROBLEM / CHALLENGE]` - The stubborn wall you've hit.
- `[BREAK_THEM]` - e.g., "What if a restaurant had no waiters?", "What if an app had no buttons?".
- `[RANDOM_STIMULI]` - Words that have nothing to do with the domain.

## 💡 Pro-Tips

- **Challenge the "Undisputed":** The best ideas come from questioning things everyone takes for granted. Ask the AI: "What is the [MOST_OBVIOUS_FACT] about this industry? Now, design a solution that ignores it."
- **Quantity over Quality (Initially):** In lateral thinking, the first 10 ideas are usually bad. Ask the AI to "Generate 20 [RAPID-FIRE_CONCEPTS] before we pick the best one."
- **The "Six Thinking Hats":** If you need a more structured debate, ask the AI to "Analyze this problem using the [WHITE,_RED,_BLACK,_YELLOW,_GREEN,_AND_BLUE_HATS] method."
