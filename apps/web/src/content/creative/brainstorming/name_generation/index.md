# Template: Strategic Name Generation (Naming & Branding)

Use this template to find the perfect name for a company, product, or character. It is designed to act as a Naming Specialist and Linguist—utilizing [PHONETIC_FLOW], [ETYMOLOGICAL_ROOTS], and [BRAND_PSYCHOLOGY] to ensure names are memorable, evocative, and available for trademarking.

## 📋 Prompt Template

```text
Act as a Senior Naming Specialist and Brand Consultant. Generate [AMOUNT] of name ideas for [THE_ENTITY].

### Context & Personality:
- **Target Audience**: [e.g., Gen Z Techies, Luxury Watch Collectors, Sustainable Parents].
- **Core Values**: [e.g., Speed, Security, Transparency, Playfulness].
- **Current Category**: [e.g., Fintech app, Vegan bakery, Sci-fi RPG].

### Naming Frameworks:
1. **The Modern Portmanteau**: Combine two relevant words (e.g., Fin + Flow = FinFlow).
2. **The Latin/Greek Root**: Use [ANCIENT_ROOTS] to convey authority or history (e.g., "Solar" from Sol, "Vitas" from Life).
3. **The Abstract Evocative**: Use a [METAPHORICAL_WORD] that captures a feeling (e.g., "Sparrow," "Forge," "Apex").
4. **The "Misspelled" Modern**: Strategically drop a vowel or swap a letter for a [STARTUP_VIBE] (e.g., "Lyft," "Flickr").
5. **The Short & Punchy**: 1 or 2 syllable names that are easy to remember.

### Output Constraints:
- Provide a [RATIONALE] for every name (Why does it work?).
- Include a "Trademark Check" warning—a 1-sentence note on the potential [DOMAIN_AVAILABILITY].
- Use a Creative, Linguistic, and Analytical tone.
```

## 🧩 Variables to Fill Out

- `[THE_ENTITY]` - Brand, Product, or Character.
- `[AMOUNT]` - e.g., 20 names.
- `[ANCIENT_ROOTS]` - e.g., Aqua, Terra, Omni, Nova.
- `[RATIONALE]` - The "Sales Pitch" for the name.

## 💡 Pro-Tips

- **The "Radio Test":** If you say the name on the radio, can people spell it? Ask the AI to "Rate each name from 1-5 on the [RADIO_SPELLING_TEST]."
- **Phonetics and Mouthfeel:** Some words are hard to say. Ask for "Names that start with [PLOSIVE_CONSONANTS] (P, T, K, B, D, G) for high energy and impact."
- **Cultural Sensitivity:** If the brand is global, ask to "Check if the name has [NEGATIVE_CONNOTATIONS] in major world languages (Spanish, Mandarin, French)."
