# Template: Innovation-Led Product Ideation (SCAMPER)

Use this template to design new products or improve existing ones. It is designed to act as a Product Lead and Innovation Consultant—utilizing the [SCAMPER] method and [USER_PAIN_POINTS] to find "Blue Ocean" opportunities where your product solves a problem in a way no one else has.

## 📋 Prompt Template

```text
Act as a Product Lead and Innovation Consultant. I want to ideate a new product/feature for: [THE_MARKET_OR_EXISTING_PRODUCT].

### Ideation Framework:
1. **The SCAMPER Audit**: Use the 7-step innovation method:
   - **Substitute**: What materials or processes can we swap?
   - **Combine**: What unrelated features can we merge?
   - **Adapt**: How can we use a solution from a different industry?
   - **Modify/Magnify**: What can we make 10x larger or 10x smaller?
   - **Put to Another Use**: Who else could use this?
   - **Eliminate**: What feature can we remove to make it better?
   - **Reverse**: What happens if we do the opposite of the industry standard?
2. **User Pain Point Bridge**: Identify 3 [FRUSTRATIONS] the current user faces and how the new idea solves them.
3. **The "Blue Ocean" Check**: Explain why this idea is [DIFFERENT] from the current competition.
4. **Feasibility Breakdown**: Briefly rate the [TECHNICAL_DIFFICULTY] vs [USER_VALUE] on a scale of 1-10.

### Output Constraints:
- Use an Innovative, Analytical, and User-Centric tone.
- Present ideas in a detailed Table format.
- Conclude with "The Prototype Blueprint"—a 1-paragraph description of the MVP (Minimum Viable Product).
```

## 🧩 Variables to Fill Out

- `[THE_MARKET / PRODUCT]` - e.g., The travel luggage industry, A fitness tracking app.
- `[FRUSTRATIONS]` - e.g., Losing keys, long battery charge times, complex UI.
- `[SCAMPER]` - The core engine of the prompt.
- `[MVP]` - The simplest version to build.

## 💡 Pro-Tips

- **Solve the "Job to be Done":** People don't buy a drill; they buy [A_HOLE_IN_THE_WALL]. Ask the AI to "Identify the [CORE_JOB_TO_BE_DONE] and ideate 3 alternative ways to achieve it."
- **Focus on Elimination:** Innovation is often about what you [TAKE_AWAY]. Ask the AI to "Design the 'Minimalist' version of this product that has zero [FRU-FRU_FEATURES]."
- **The "Adjacent Possible":** Ask for "Ideas that are just 1 step away from [CONVENTIONAL_LOGIC]" for realistic, buildable products.
