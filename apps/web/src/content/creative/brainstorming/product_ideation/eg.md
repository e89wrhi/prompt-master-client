# Examples: Innovation-Led Product Ideation

## 1. Product: "The Smart Water Bottle"

```text
Act as an Innovation Consultant. Ideate a new "Smart Water Bottle" for high-performance athletes.

### Ideation Framework:
1. **SCAMPER**:
   - Combine: Merge a water bottle with a biometric sweat sensor.
   - Eliminate: Remove the physical cap; use a magnetic pressure seal.
   - Put to Another Use: It’s not just a bottle; it’s a high-accuracy hydration data tracker.
2. **Pain Points**: Forgetting to drink, over-hydration, and cleaning difficulties.
3. **Blue Ocean**: Unlike competitors (which just track 'Amount Ounces'), ours tracks 'Salt/Nutrient Needs'.

### Output Constraints:
- Innovative and Analytical tone.
- MVP description included.
```

## 2. Market: "The Commuter Experience" (Public Transit)

```text
Act as a Product Lead. Ideate 3 software features for a city transit app.

### Framework:
- Identify frustrations: UNPREDICTABLE_TIMES, SAFETY, SOCIAL_ISOLATION.
- Use the "Reverse" SCAMPER: What if the app didn't show the route, but showed the "Vibe" of the bus?

### Output Constraints:
- User-Centric tone.
```
