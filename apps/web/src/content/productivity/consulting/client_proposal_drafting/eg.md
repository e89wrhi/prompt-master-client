# Examples: Client Proposal Drafting

## 1. Custom Software Development (SaaS MVP)

```text
Act as a Senior Partner at a Top-Tier Management Consulting Firm. Draft a comprehensive project proposal for "HealthTrack AI" regarding the development of their mobile MVP.

### Project Inputs:
- Client: A Seed-funded health tech startup.
- Pain Point: They have raw algorithms but no user-facing interface. They need to show a working product to VCs in 3 months.
- Budget: ~$50k - $80k.

### Proposal Requirements:
1. **Executive Summary**: A high-impact opening that mirrors the client's need for speed-to-market and investor readiness.
2. **Detailed Scope of Work**: Break down the project into 3 phases: UI/UX Design, Backend Integration, and Beta Testing.
3. **Unique Value Proposition (UVP)**: Focus on our experience with HIPPA compliance and rapid prototyping.
4. **Timeline & Milestones**: Provide a 12-week timeline with bi-weekly demos.
5. **Pricing Tiers**: 
   - Basic ($50k): Core mobile app only.
   - Recommended ($65k): App + Web Dashboard.
   - Premium ($85k): App + Dashboard + 6 months of post-launch support.
6. **Risk Mitigation**: Scalability of the algorithm and data security.

### Output Constraints:
- Use a Persuasive and Professional tone.
- Format pricing and timeline in tables.
```

## 2. Strategic Branding & Marketing Refresh

```text
Act as a Senior Partner at a Top-Tier Management Consulting Firm. Draft a comprehensive project proposal for "OldGuard Logistics" regarding a modern brand refresh.

### Project Inputs:
- Client: A 30-year-old logistics firm.
- Pain Point: They are losing market share to younger, "tech-first" competitors. Their logo and website look like they are from 2005.
- Desire: To look modern, reliable, and technologically advanced.

### Proposal Requirements:
1. **Executive Summary**: Target the "Old vs New" narrative and the cost of inaction.
2. **Detailed Scope of Work**: Logo redesign, New Website, and 3-month LinkedIn social strategy.
3. **Unique Value Proposition (UVP)**: Our ability to bridge tradition with modern tech aesthetics.
4. **Timeline & Milestones**: 6-week brand sprint.
5. **Pricing Tiers**: 
   - Essential ($10k): Logo & Brand Guidelines.
   - Performance ($25k): Logo + Website + SEO.
   - Market Leader ($40k): Full refresh + Content Strategy + Video Hero Header.

### Output Constraints:
- Use a Sharp and Authoritative tone.
```
