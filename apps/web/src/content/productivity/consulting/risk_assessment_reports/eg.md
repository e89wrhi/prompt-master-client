# Examples: Risk Assessment Reports

## 1. Cloud-to-Cloud Database Migration

```text
Act as a Senior Risk Manager and Compliance Officer. Conduct a comprehensive Risk Assessment for a database migration from Azure to Google Cloud (GCP).

### Context & Scope:
- Project: Migrating 10TB of user PII (Personally Identifiable Information).
- Goal: Reduce latency and hosting costs by 15%.
- Timeline: 48-hour cutover window.

### Analysis Requirements:
1. **The Risk Register**: Identify 5 risks across Technical (data loss), Legal (GDPR), and Operational (downtime).
2. **Impact vs. Likelihood Matrix**: Score each from 1-5.
3. **Primary Mitigation Plans**: Detailed strategies for "Data Corruption during Transit" and "Exceeding the 48-hour Window."
4. **Contingency "Plan B"**: Protocol for a total migration failure requiring a rollback to Azure.
5. **Stakeholder Communication**: Draft a summary for the CTO.

### Output Constraints:
- Present the Risk Register in a Markdown Table.
- Categorize using a RED/AMBER/GREEN status.
- Use a Formal and Cautious tone.
```

## 2. Supply Chain Diversification (Manufacturing)

```text
Act as a Senior Risk Manager and Compliance Officer. Conduct a comprehensive Risk Assessment for moving 30% of our manufacturing from China to Vietnam.

### Context & Scope:
- Reason: Tariffs and geopolitical instability.
- Goal: Improve supply chain resilience.
- Challenge: Quality control and longer shipping lead times.

### Analysis Requirements:
1. **The Risk Register**: Identify 5 risks across Financial (tariff math), Quality Control, and Logistics.
2. **Impact vs. Likelihood Matrix**: Score each from 1-5.
3. **Primary Mitigation Plans**: Strategies for "Initial Quality Variance" and "Logistics Delays at Vietnam Ports."
4. **Contingency "Plan B"**: Sudden port closure or trade policy shift in Vietnam.
5. **Stakeholder Communication**: Draft a summary for the COO.

### Output Constraints:
- Present the Risk Register in a table.
- Categorize outcomes using status dots (🔴/🟡/🟢).
```
