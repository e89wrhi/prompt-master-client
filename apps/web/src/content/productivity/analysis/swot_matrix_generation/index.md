# Template: Professional SWOT Analysis

Use this template to evaluate a project, business venture, or product concept. It organizes internal factors (Strengths/Weaknesses) and external factors (Opportunities/Threats) into a 2x2 matrix to inform strategic decision-making.

## 📋 Prompt Template

```text
Act as a Senior Business Strategist. Conduct a comprehensive SWOT analysis for [SUBJECT].

### Subject Context:
[INSERT_DESCRIPTION_OF_PRODUCT_OR_BUSINESS]

### Analysis Requirements:
1. **Strengths (S)**: Identify internal advantages like [STRENGTH_FACTOR_1] and [STRENGTH_FACTOR_2].
2. **Weaknesses (W)**: Identify internal limitations such as [WEAKNESS_FACTOR_1] or performance gaps.
3. **Opportunities (O)**: Identify external growth areas like [MARKET_TREND] or [COMPETITOR_GAP].
4. **Threats (T)**: Identify external risks like [REGULATORY_CHANGE] or [EMERGING_COMPETITOR].
5. **Strategic Synthesis**: Choose the most promising "Opportunity" and explain how to leverage a "Strength" to capture it.

### Output Constraints:
- Format the SWOT items in a Markdown Table or a 2x2 grid layout.
- Use Sharp, Actionable language.
- Conclude with a "Critical Priorities" section containing 3 immediate next steps.
```

## 🧩 Variables to Fill Out

- `[SUBJECT]` - The business, product, or individual being analyzed.
- `[INSERT_DESCRIPTION_OF_PRODUCT_OR_BUSINESS]` - Detailed context, mission, and current market position.
- `[STRENGTH_FACTOR_1/2]` - e.g., Proprietary tech, Strong brand loyalty, High cash reserves.
- `[WEAKNESS_FACTOR_1]` - e.g., High churn, Legacy code, Lack of international presence.
- `[MARKET_TREND / COMPETITOR_GAP]` - e.g., Rise of AI, untapped APAC market, competitor's recent price hike.
- `[REGULATORY_CHANGE / EMERGING_COMPETITOR]` - e.g., GDPR updates, new low-cost startups.

## 💡 Pro-Tips

- **Be Honest on Weaknesses:** A SWOT is only useful if it’s realistic. If the AI is being too positive, prompt it: "Critique this as if you were a pessimistic venture capitalist."
- **Internal vs. External:** Remember, S/W are what you control. O/T are what happens in the world around you.
- **TOWS Matrix:** For advanced users, ask the AI to "Convert this SWOT into a TOWS matrix to find actionable strategies (SO, ST, WO, WT)."
