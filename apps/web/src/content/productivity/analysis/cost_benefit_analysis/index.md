# Template: Professional Cost-Benefit Analysis (CBA)

Use this template to evaluate the financial and operational viability of a project, purchase, or strategic shift. It focuses on identifying both quantitative (hard) and qualitative (soft) factors to calculate a projected Return on Investment (ROI).

## 📋 Prompt Template

```text
Act as a Senior Financial Analyst and Strategic Consultant. Conduct a rigorous Cost-Benefit Analysis for the following [PROPOSAL]:

### Financial Data & Context:
[INSERT_CONTEXT_OR_DATA]

### Analysis Requirements:
1. **Hard Costs (Direct)**: List all immediate financial outlays including [COST_CATEGORY_1], [COST_CATEGORY_2], and [COST_CATEGORY_3].
2. **Soft Costs (Indirect)**: Identify hidden costs such as [SOFT_COST_TYPE] (e.g., training time, productivity dips during transition).
3. **Hard Benefits**: Quantify direct gains like [REVENUE_INCREASE] or [EXPENSE_REDUCTION] over a [TIME_PERIOD] timeframe.
4. **Soft Benefits**: Evaluate intangible advantages like [INTANGIBLE_BENEFIT] (e.g., employee morale, brand reputation).
5. **ROI Calculation**: Provide a projected ROI percentage and a "Break-Even Point" analysis.

### Output Constraints:
- Present the final comparison in a clearly formatted table.
- Conclude with a "Go/No-Go" recommendation based on the data.
- Use a Professional and Objective tone.
```

## 🧩 Variables to Fill Out

- `[PROPOSAL]` - The specific project or purchase being evaluated.
- `[INSERT_CONTEXT_OR_DATA]` - Raw numbers, quotes, or descriptions of the current state vs. the proposed state.
- `[COST_CATEGORY_1/2/3]` - e.g., Licensing fees, Hardware costs, Implementation labor.
- `[SOFT_COST_TYPE]` - e.g., Opportunity cost, Learning curve, Talent attrition risk.
- `[REVENUE_INCREASE/EXPENSE_REDUCTION]` - Specific financial goals.
- `[TIME_PERIOD]` - e.g., 12 months, 3 years, 5 years.
- `[INTANGIBLE_BENEFIT]` - e.g., User experience, Market positioning, Compliance security.

## 💡 Pro-Tips

- **The Value of Time:** Don't forget to price human labor. If a team of 5 spends 10 hours on a transition, that's a significant hard cost.
- **Sensitivity Analysis:** If you're unsure of a number, ask the AI to run "Best Case" and "Worst Case" scenarios.
- **Maintenance Matters:** Always include Year 2 and Year 3 maintenance costs, not just the initial purchase price.
