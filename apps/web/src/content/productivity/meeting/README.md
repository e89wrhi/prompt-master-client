# 🗣️ Meeting Assistance

Templates to automate the painful parts of asynchronous and synchronous meetings.

## Available Categories

- [**Action Items Extraction**](./action_items_extraction/index.md): Identifying who explicitly promised to do what from a transcript.
- [**Agenda Generation**](./agenda_generation/index.md): Structuring a productive meeting before it happens.
- [**Meeting Minutes Summarization**](./meeting_minutes_summarization/index.md): Turning a raw Otter.ai transcript into a clean summary.
- [**Post-Mortem Facilitation**](./post_mortem_facilitation/index.md): Structuring a blameless post-mortem report from incidents.
