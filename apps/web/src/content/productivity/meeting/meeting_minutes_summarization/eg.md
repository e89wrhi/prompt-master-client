# Examples: Meeting Minutes Summarization

## 1. Annual Board of Directors Meeting

```text
Act as a Senior Executive Scribe and Corporate Secretary. Summarize the following board meeting transcript into a formal "Minutes of Meeting" (MoM) document.

### Source Content:
[User pastes 30-page board transcript discussing CEO's performance, a potential merger with Company X, and a $2M Q4 budget.]

### Summarization Requirements:
1. **The Executive Snapshot**: 3-sentence summary of the meeting's atmosphere.
2. **The Decision Log**: Decisions regarding the CEO's bonus and the Merger negotiation.
3. **Key Discussion Points**: Group by: Governance, Growth, and Finance.
4. **Dissenting Opinions**: Note the CFO's concerns regarding the merger's debt implications.
5. **Next Steps & Accountability**: Identify owners for the merger due diligence.

### Output Constraints:
- Use Formal and Precise language.
- Format with Date, Attendees, and Decisions.
```

## 2. Technical Design Review (New API)

```text
Act as a Senior Executive Scribe. Summarize the following technical design review into a "Minutes of Meeting" (MoM).

### Source Content:
[User pastes transcript of 5 developers arguing about REST vs GraphQL for the new mobile app.]

### Summarization Requirements:
1. **The Executive Snapshot**: Summary of the consensus.
2. **The Decision Log**: Final choice of protocol and the rationale behind it.
3. **Key Discussion Points**: Categorize by: Performance, Developer Experience, and Scalability.
4. **Dissenting Opinions**: Note the concerns about GraphQL caching complexity.
5. **Next Steps**: Action items for the lead architect.

### Output Constraints:
- Neutral and Professional language.
- Concise structure.
```
