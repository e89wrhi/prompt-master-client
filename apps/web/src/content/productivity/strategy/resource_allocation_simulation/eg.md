# Examples: Resource Allocation Simulation

## 1. Product Team: Feature Growth vs. Technical Debt

```text
Act as a Senior Operations Lead. Perform a Resource Allocation Simulation for the following WORKSTREAM: The Q4 Product Roadmap.

### Input Parameters:
- **Total Resource**: 1,200 dev-hours per month.
- **Current Headcount**: 8 Developers.
- **Competing Priorities**: New "AI Insights" Feature, Legacy Code Refactor, and Customer Bug Fixes.

### Simulation Requirements:
1. **Scenario A: The Aggressive Growth Model**: 70% to AI Insights. 
2. **Scenario B: The Balanced Hybrid Model**: 40% AI, 30% Refactor, 30% Bugs.
3. **Scenario C: The Conservative Maintenance Model**: 60% Refactor.
4. **The "Efficiency Discovery"**: Identify the point of diminishing returns.
5. **Final Recommendation**: Select the most "Resilient" model for reaching $5M ARR.

### Output Constraints:
- Side-by-side comparison table.
- Analytical and Data-Driven tone.
```

## 2. Marketing Spend Allocation (Early Stage)

```text
Act as a Resource Planner. Perform a Resource Allocation Simulation for our $20k Monthly Marketing Budget.

### Input Parameters:
- **Total Budget**: $20k/month.
- **Competing Priorities**: Facebook Ads (Direct Sales), Content Marketing (Long-term SEO), and Influencer Partnerships (Brand Awareness).

### Simulation Requirements:
1. **Scenario A: High Performance**: 80% to Facebook Ads.
2. **Scenario B: Brand Building**: 50% to Influencers.
3. **Scenario C: Diversified**: 33% each.
4. **The "Efficiency Discovery"**: At what spend level does Facebook CPC usually spike for our niche?
5. **Final Recommendation**: Best model for a new DTC brand.

### Output Constraints:
- Precise and Data-Driven tone.
```
