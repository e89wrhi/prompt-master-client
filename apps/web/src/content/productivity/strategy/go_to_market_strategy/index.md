# Template: Professional Go-To-Market (GTM) Strategy

Use this template to launch a new product, enter a new market, or reposition an existing brand. It is designed to bridge the gap between "product development" and "revenue generation" by defining your Ideal Customer Profile (ICP), acquisition channels, and the competitive "moat."

## 📋 Prompt Template

```text
Act as a VP of Marketing and Growth Strategy. Draft a comprehensive Go-To-Market (GTM) Strategy for [PRODUCT/SERVICE].

### Business Context:
[INSERT_PRODUCT_DESCRIPTION_AND_TARGET_MARKET]

### Strategy Requirements:
1. **The Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)**: Define the top 2 personas that will derive the most value from [PRODUCT/SERVICE]. Include their specific [PAIN_POINTS] and [BUYING_TRIGGERS].
2. **Value Proposition & Core Message**: Craft a "unique selling point" (USP) that differentiates us from [PRIMARY_COMPETITOR].
3. **Multi-Channel Acquisition Plan**: Propose 3 acquisition channels (e.g., [CHANNEL_1], [CHANNEL_2], [CHANNEL_3]). For each, define a high-level tactic.
4. **The Sales Funnel Projection**: Outline the path from "First Touch" to "Conversion." What does the customer journey look like?
5. **Pricing & Packaging Strategy**: Suggest a pricing model that balances market entry with long-term [LTV_GOAL].
6. **Timeline & Milestones**: Provide a [TIME_PERIOD] launch roadmap with specific 30-60-90 day goals.

### Output Constraints:
- Use a Strategic, Ambitious, and Data-Driven tone.
- Format the roadmap and channeled plans in Markdown Tables.
- Ensure all recommendations focus on "Speed to Traction" and "Scalability."
```

## 🧩 Variables to Fill Out

- `[PRODUCT/SERVICE]` - The actual thing you are selling.
- `[INSERT_PRODUCT_DESCRIPTION_AND_TARGET_MARKET]` - Details on what it does and who you think it's for.
- `[PAIN_POINTS]` - The "What hurts?" factor for the customer.
- `[BUYING_TRIGGERS]` - Events that make a customer realize they need a solution *now*.
- `[PRIMARY_COMPETITOR]` - Your biggest threat.
- `[CHANNEL_1/2/3]` - e.g., Paid Search, Content/SEO, Cold Outreaching, Influencer Marketing.
- `[LTV_GOAL]` - Lifetime Value targets.
- `[TIME_PERIOD]` - e.g., 6 months, 1 year.

## 💡 Pro-Tips

- **The Moat:** A GTM strategy without a "Moat" is just a marketing plan. Ensure the AI identifies why a competitor can't just copy your strategy tomorrow.
- **Fail Fast Metrics:** Ask the AI to "Define 3 'Kill Switches'—metrics that, if not met by Day 60, mean we should pivot the strategy."
- **Distribution is Queen:** If the product is good but the distribution is weak, the strategy will fail. Ensure the AI focuses heavily on the `[CHANNELS]` section.
