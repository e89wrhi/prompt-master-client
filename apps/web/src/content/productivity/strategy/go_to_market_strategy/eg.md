# Examples: Go-To-Market (GTM) Strategy

## 1. AI-Powered Writing Assistant for Legal Teams

```text
Act as a VP of Marketing and Growth Strategy. Draft a comprehensive GTM Strategy for "LexiWrite," an AI tool that automates the first draft of legal briefs.

### Business Context:
- Product: LexiWrite leverages LLMs but is fine-tuned on case law to ensure 98% accuracy.
- Target: Small-to-mid-sized law firms (5-50 lawyers).
- Goal: Secure 100 paying firms in 6 months.

### Strategy Requirements:
1. **ICP**: Focus on "The Overwhelmed Associate" and "The Cost-Conscious Partner."
2. **Value Prep**: "Cut 8 hours of drafting into 15 minutes without sacrificing security."
3. **Multi-Channel Acquisition**: LinkedIn Account-Based Marketing (ABM), Partnerships with Legal Tech conferences, and SEO targeting "automated legal brief drafting."
4. **Sales Funnel**: High-touch demo -> 7-day free trial -> Annual contract.
5. **Pricing**: $200/lawyer/month. 
6. **Launch Roadmap**: 30-60-90 day milestones focusing on Beta tests and then full launch.

### Output Constraints:
- Strategic and Data-Driven tone.
- Format the roadmap in a table.
```

## 2. Direct-to-Consumer (DTC) Ergonomic Desk Chair

```text
Act as a VP of Marketing. Draft a GTM Strategy for "AeroSit," a $300 ergonomic chair that ships in a box half the size of competitors.

### Business Context:
- Target: Remote tech workers in the USA.
- Competitive Advantage: Patent-pending folding mechanism that reduces shipping costs by 50%.
- Goal: Reach $1M in revenue in Year 1.

### Strategy Requirements:
1. **ICP**: The "Home Office Optimizer" (ages 25-40).
2. **Core Message**: "The best support your back ever had, delivered in minutes." 
3. **Acquisition**: Influencer reviews (YouTube/TikTok), Podcast ads (Tech-focused), and Meta Retargeting.
4. **Funnel**: Viral ad -> Landing page with "Real Posture" quiz -> Purchase.
5. **Pricing**: $299 (Premium Disruptor).

### Output Constraints:
- Ambitious tone.
```
