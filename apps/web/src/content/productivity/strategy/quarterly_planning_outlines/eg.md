# Examples: Quarterly Planning Outlines

## 1. Early-Stage Startup (Scale Mode)

```text
Act as a Senior Strategy Consultant. Develop a Quarterly Planning Outline for "Streamify" (SaaS) for Q3.

### Source Inputs:
- OKR 1: Increase user base by 25%.
- OKR 2: Lower churn from 8% to 5%.
- Challenge: Manual onboarding is a huge bottleneck.

### Planning Requirements:
1. **The Quarterly Theme**: "Scale with Automation."
2. **The 3 "Big Rocks"**: 
   - Feature: Automated User Onboarding Flow. 
   - Marketing: "Free Tier to Pro" email sequence overhaul.
   - Ops: Implement automated billing and dunning.
3. **Monthly Roadmap**: Break down into Month 1, 2, and 3.
4. **Resource Check**: Team size = 6 (3 devs, 1 designer, 1 marketer, 1 founder).
5. **Dependency Map**: Reliance on Legal for the new Terms of Service.

### Output Constraints:
- Gantt-style Markdown Table for the roadmap.
- Realistic and High-Velocity tone.
```

## 2. HR Department (Culture & Recruitment)

```text
Act as a Program Manager. Develop a Q4 Planning Outline for the HR Team.

### Source Inputs:
- Objective: Hire 10 senior engineers.
- Objective: Improve internal employee satisfaction scores.

### Planning Requirements:
1. **The Quarterly Theme**: "People First."
2. **The 3 "Big Rocks"**: 
   - Program: Engineering Referral Bonus relaunch.
   - Event: Annual Company Retreat planning.
   - Process: Introduction of bi-weekly 1:1 standardized feedback.
3. **Resource Check**: Team size = 3.

### Output Constraints:
- Use a Direct tone.
```
