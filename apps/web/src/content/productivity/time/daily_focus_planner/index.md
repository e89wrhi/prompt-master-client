# Template: Professional Daily Focus Planner

Use this template to transition from a "Passive To-Do List" to an "Active Success Plan." It is designed to prioritize your highest-leverage work using the "Eat the Frog" method while accounting for your natural energy cycles throughout the day.

## 📋 Prompt Template

```text
Act as a High-Performance Architect. design a 24-hour Daily Focus Plan for [ROLE] based on the following [LIST_OF_TASKS_AND_APPOINTMENTS]:

### The Input:
[INSERT_YOUR_TASKS_AND_CALENDAR_ITEMS]

### Planning Requirements:
1. **The "Frog" (MIT)**: Identify the single Most Important Task (MIT) that must be done first. Why is this high-leverage?
2. **Energy Matching**: Sort the tasks into [PEAK_FOCUS_PERIODS] and [LOW_ENERGY_VALLEYS]. (e.g., Deep Work when I'm sharpest, admin when I'm tired).
3. **The "Non-Negotiable" Block**: Create a protected 90-minute block for [DEEP_WORK_PROJECT].
4. **The Buffer Protocol**: Include two 15-minute "Reaction Buffers" to handle unexpected Slack/Emails without derailing the day.
5. **The Shutdown Ritual**: Design a 10-minute closing sequence to clear your mind for the evening.

### Output Constraints:
- Present the final plan as a Chronological Schedule (e.g., 08:00 - 09:00).
- Use an Encouraging, Disciplined, and Action-Oriented tone.
- If the workload is impossible for one day, explicitly state what should be [DEFERRED] to tomorrow.
```

## 🧩 Variables to Fill Out

- `[ROLE]` - e.g., Freelance Graphic Designer, Account Executive, PhD Student.
- `[LIST_OF_TASKS_AND_APPOINTMENTS]` - List everything on your plate.
- `[PEAK_FOCUS_PERIODS]` - When are you naturally most alert? (e.g., 8am-11am).
- `[LOW_ENERGY_VALLEYS]` - When do you usually crash? (e.g., 2pm-4pm).
- `[DEEP_WORK_PROJECT]` - The "big needle mover" you've been procrastinating on.

## 💡 Pro-Tips

- **Decentralize Your List:** If your task list is too big, tell the AI: "Pick the 3 tasks that will move my career forward the most and ignore the rest."
- **Respect Biological Rhythms:** Don't schedule a complex creative task right after lunch if you know you'll be sleepy.
- **The "Tomorrow" Hook:** Always have the AI end the day by identifying the "First Step" for tomorrow morning. This prevents morning friction.
