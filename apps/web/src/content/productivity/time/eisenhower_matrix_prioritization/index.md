# Template: Professional Eisenhower Matrix Prioritization

Use this template to escape the "Urgency Trap" by categorizing tasks into four quadrants based on their Importance and Urgency. It is designed to help you stop "firefighting" and start focusing on the high-value activities that drive long-term success.

## 📋 Prompt Template

```text
Act as a Senior Systems Thinker and Management Consultant. Prioritize the following [LIST_OF_TASKS] using the Eisenhower Matrix framework:

### The Backlog:
[INSERT_YOUR_UNSTRUCTURED_TASK_LIST]

### Prioritization Requirements:
1. **Quadrant 1 (Do First)**: Identify tasks that are both Urgent and Important (e.g., [CRITICAL_DEADLINE]).
2. **Quadrant 2 (Schedule)**: Identify tasks that are Important but NOT Urgent (e.g., [STRATEGIC_GROWTH]). This is where your focus should be.
3. **Quadrant 3 (Delegate)**: Identify tasks that are Urgent but NOT Important (e.g., [INTERRUPTIONS]). Propose [WHO] could handle these instead.
4. **Quadrant 4 (Eliminate)**: Identify tasks that are neither Urgent nor Important (e.g., [BUSY_WORK]). Provide a 1-sentence "Rationale for Deletion."
5. **The Strategic Pivot**: Suggest how to move 2 tasks from Quadrant 1 down into Quadrant 2 through better planning.

### Output Constraints:
- Format the result as a 2x2 Markdown Matrix or a categorized list.
- Use an Objective, Unsentimental, and Efficient tone.
- Conclude with "The Action Plan": The top 3 tasks to start on *immediately*.
```

## 🧩 Variables to Fill Out

- `[LIST_OF_TASKS]` - Everything you are currently tracking.
- `[CRITICAL_DEADLINE]` - Things that will cause immediate failure if missed.
- `[STRATEGIC_GROWTH]` - Things that will move your career or business forward in 6 months.
- `[INTERRUPTIONS]` - Meetings, Slack pings, or requests from others that serve *their* goals.
- `[BUSY_WORK]` - Low-value tasks that make you feel "productive" but yield no results.
- `[WHO]` - Teammates, virtual assistants, or automated tools.

## 💡 Pro-Tips

- **Important != Urgent:** Most people spend 80% of their time in Q1 and Q3. The goal of this prompt is to ruthlessly protect your Q2 time.
- **The Delegation Hack:** If you have no one to delegate to, ask the AI to "Suggest an automation or software tool that could handle these Q3 tasks."
- **Be Brutal on Q4:** If you can't delete a Q4 task, it's actually an "Important" task in disguise—re-evaluate its value.
