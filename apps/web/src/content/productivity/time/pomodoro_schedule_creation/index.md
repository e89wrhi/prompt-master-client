# Template: Professional Pomodoro Schedule Creation

Use this template to break through procrastination and maintain high-intensity focus without burning out. It is designed to group related tasks into "Deep Concentration" sprints (25 or 50 mins) and "Restorative" breaks (5 or 10 mins).

## 📋 Prompt Template

```text
Act as a Focus Specialist and Cognitive Scientist. Create a structured Pomodoro Schedule for a [SESSION_DURATION] deep work session targeting [PRIMARY_GOAL].

### Tasks to Accomplish:
[INSERT_TASK_LIST_AND_ESTIMATED_TIME]

### Schedule Requirements:
1. **The Interval Strategy**: Recommend a [25/5_OR_50/10] interval split based on the complexity of [PRIMARY_GOAL].
2. **The Batching Protocol**: Group the following [SHALLOW_TASKS] into a single Pomodoro block.
3. **Restorative Breaks**: For each break, propose a specific activity to [RESTORE_COGNITIVE_RESOURCES] (e.g., movement, hydration, eye-rest). No screens allowed.
4. **The "Flow State" On-Ramp**: Design a 2-minute "Trigger Ritual" to start the first block (e.g., specific music, desk clearing).
5. **The Mid-Point Review**: Suggest a 2-minute check-in activity at the end of the 2nd Pomodoro to adjust the plan if progress is slow.

### Output Constraints:
- Present the schedule in a Block-by-Block format (e.g., Block 1: 09:00 - 09:50).
- Use a Calm, Encouraging, and High-Focus tone.
- Conclude with "The One Thing": The most important rule for this specific session.
```

## 🧩 Variables to Fill Out

- `[SESSION_DURATION]` - e.g., 2 hours, 4 hours, all morning.
- `[PRIMARY_GOAL]` - e.g., Writing 2,000 words, debugging the auth flow, clearing the backlog.
- `[INSERT_TASK_LIST_AND_ESTIMATED_TIME]` - The things you need to do and how long you think they take.
- `[25/5_OR_50/10]` - 25 mins work/5 mins break (Good for shallow work) or 50 mins work/10 mins break (Better for deep, creative flow).
- `[RESTORE_COGNITIVE_RESOURCES]` - Activities that involve zero input (no phone, no email).

## 💡 Pro-Tips

- **The Goldilocks Zone:** If a task is too big (e.g., "Build the whole app"), the AI needs to "Atomize" it into smaller sub-tasks that fit into a single block.
- **Manage the Distraction Log:** Tell the AI to include a "Distraction Catch-All" section where you jot down random thoughts to process *after* the session.
- **The Screen-Free Rule:** A break where you check Slack is not a break. Ensure the AI gives you "Analog" break suggestions (stretching, water, looking out a window).
