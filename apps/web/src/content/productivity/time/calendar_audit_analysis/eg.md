# Examples: Calendar Audit Analysis

## 1. Startup Founder (Post-Funding Chaos)

```text
Act as a world-class Productivity Coach. Perform a detailed Calendar Audit for a Startup Founder based on the following weekly logs:

### Calendar Data:
- Mon: 9-11 Investor sync, 11-12 Team Standup, 13-14 Hiring interview, 14-17 Back-to-back 30m coffee chats.
- Tue: 9-10 Product Review, 11-15 Board Prep (Fragmented by Slack), 16-18 Strategic Planning.
- Wed: "No Meeting Wednesday" (But ended up in 4 emergency calls).

### Analysis Requirements:
1. **The Energy Tax**: Categorize info Deep/Shallow/Admin.
2. **The Fragmentation Index**: Highlight the Monday afternoon "Swiss Cheese" chaos.
3. **Meeting ROI**: Evaluate the 4 hours of coffee chats.
4. **The "Clean Slate" Recommendation**: Suggest 3 bold moves for next week.

### Output Constraints:
- Use a Bold and High-Performance tone.
- Format time categorization in a table.
```

## 2. Senior Developer (Meeting Bloat)

```text
Act as a Performance Architect. Perform a detailed Calendar Audit for a Senior Software Engineer.

### Calendar Data:
- Daily: 10-10:30 Standup.
- Tue/Thu: 14-15 Design Review.
- Wed: 11-12 Architecture Sync, 13-14 Sprint Planning, 15-16 Retro.
- Mon/Fri: 13-14 Deployment check-in.

### Analysis Requirements:
1. **The Energy Tax**: How much time is left for "Coding" (Deep Work)?
2. **The Fragmentation Index**: Focus on the Wednesday "Action/Interruption" cycle.
3. **Ideal State Comparison**: Compare to a "Maker's Schedule" (4-hour morning blocks).
4. **The "Clean Slate" Recommendation**: Reclaim 20% of the week.

### Output Constraints:
- Direct and Analytical tone.
```
