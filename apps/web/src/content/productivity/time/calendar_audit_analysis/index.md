# Template: Professional Calendar Audit & Analysis

Use this template to reclaim your time by identifying "meeting bloat," fragmented work blocks, and misalignment between your schedule and your goals. It is designed to move beyond "organizing" and into "optimizing" your energy and output.

## 📋 Prompt Template

```text
Act as a world-class Productivity Coach and Performance Architect. Perform a detailed Calendar Audit for [PERSONA/ROLE] based on the following [TIME_FRAME] logs:

### Calendar Data:
[INSERT_CALENDAR_ITEMS_OR_CSV_LIST]

### Analysis Requirements:
1. **The Energy Tax**: Categorize every entry into [DEEP_WORK], [SHALLOW_WORK], or [ADMIN/MAINTENANCE]. Calculate the percentage of time spent in each.
2. **The Fragmentation Index**: Identify "Swiss Cheese" blocks (gaps between meetings that are <45 minutes). Explain why these are draining productivity.
3. **Meeting ROI**: Flag any meetings that appear [RECURRING_WITHOUT_VALUE] or involve more than 10 people without a clear decision goal.
4. **Ideal State Comparison**: Compare the current calendar to a "Perfect Week" model. Where are the 3 biggest misalignments?
5. **The "Clean Slate" Recommendation**: Suggest 3 bold moves to reclaim at least 20% of the [TIME_FRAME] (e.g., "Deleting all Wednesday calls," "Batching admin to Fridays").

### Output Constraints:
- Present the time categorization in a Markdown Table.
- Use a Bold, Direct, and High-Performance tone.
- Ensure the recommendations focus on "Deep Work" protection.
```

## 🧩 Variables to Fill Out

- `[PERSONA/ROLE]` - e.g., Senior Software Engineer, Startup Founder, Creative Director.
- `[TIME_FRAME]` - e.g., Last Week, This Month.
- `[INSERT_CALENDAR_ITEMS_OR_CSV_LIST]` - Your actual meeting list, times, and descriptions.
- `[DEEP_WORK]` - Tasks that require zero distraction and high cognitive load.
- `[SHALLOW_WORK]` - Logistical tasks, status updates, or low-stakes emails.
- `[RECURRING_WITHOUT_VALUE]` - Meetings that "could have been an email."

## 💡 Pro-Tips

- **Be Brutal with Recurrence:** If a meeting has been on your calendar for 6+ months, ask the AI to "Determine if this meeting should be deleted or converted to a monthly async update."
- **Contextualize with Goals:** If you paste your Q3 goals alongside your calendar, the AI can say things like "You spend 0% of your time on Goal #1."
- **Focus on Transitions:** It takes 20 minutes to re-focus after a distraction. Tell the AI to "Account for Context Switching costs in the Fragmentation Index."
