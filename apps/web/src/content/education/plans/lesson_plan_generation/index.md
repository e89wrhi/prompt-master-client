# Template: High-Impact Lesson Plan Generation

Use this template to design engaging, high-retention lessons for any subject or age group. It is designed to move beyond "Lecturing" by incorporating the "Gradual Release of Responsibility" model (I Do, We Do, You Do), specific checks for understanding, and time-boxed activities to keep energy high.

## 📋 Prompt Template

```text
Act as a Senior Educational Consultant and Instructional Designer. Generate a [DURATION] Lesson Plan for the topic: [LESSON_TOPIC].

### Lesson Context:
- **Grade Level/Audience**: [e.g., 4th Grade, Undergraduate, Corporate Onboarding].
- **Prerequisite Knowledge**: [WHAT_THEY_ALREADY_KNOW].
- **Learning Objective**: By the end of this lesson, students will be able to [SPECIFIC_MEASURABLE_ACTION].

### Lesson Plan Requirements:
1. **The Hook (Introduction)**: A 5-minute activity to grab attention and activate prior knowledge.
2. **"I Do" (Direct Instruction)**: Describe exactly what the teacher will model or explain. List the [RESOURCES] needed (e.g., slides, whiteboard).
3. **"We Do" (Guided Practice)**: A collaborative activity where students work with the teacher or in pairs to master the concept.
4. **"You Do" (Independent Practice)**: A task students complete on their own to demonstrate mastery.
5. **Check for Understanding (CFU)**: Provide 3 specific questions to ask throughout the lesson to catch misconceptions early.
6. **The Exit Ticket (Assessment)**: A 2-minute task at the end to prove they met the [LEARNING_OBJECTIVE].

### Output Constraints:
- Present the plan as a Chronological Timeline with [TIME_STAMPS].
- Use an Instructional, Encouraging, and Organized tone.
- Conclude with "Teacher Tips"—3 pieces of advice for handling [COMMON_STUDENT_PAIN_POINTS].
```

## 🧩 Variables to Fill Out

- `[LESSON_TOPIC]` - e.g., Solving Quadratic Equations, The French Revolution, Using the 'useState' hook in React.
- `[DURATION]` - e.g., 45 minutes, 90-minute workshop.
- `[SPECIFIC_MEASURABLE_ACTION]` - Use verbs like "Solve," "List," "Design," "Compare."
- `[RESOURCES]` - Physical or digital tools.
- `[TIME_STAMPS]` - e.g., 0-10: Hook, 10-25: Direct, etc.

## 💡 Pro-Tips

- **The "Rule of 10":** Adult learners lose focus after 10-15 minutes of passive listening. Ask the AI to "Insert a 2-minute active engagement break every 15 minutes."
- **Multiple Intelligences:** Not everyone learns by listening. Ask the AI to "Suggest a visual or kinesthetic alternative for the 'We Do' phase."
- **The "Struggle" Point:** Identify the hardest part of the topic. Tell the AI to "Design the 'Guided Practice' specifically around the [TALLEST_BARRIER] of the lesson."
