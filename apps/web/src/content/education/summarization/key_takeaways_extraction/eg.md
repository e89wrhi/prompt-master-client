# Examples: Key Takeaways Extraction

## 1. Podcast: "The Science of Sleep" (Huberman style)

```text
Act as an Intellectual Editorial Director. Extract takeaways from this 2-hour podcast transcript on "Circadian Rhythms."

### Extraction Framework:
1. **Summary**: The light exposure you get in the first 30 minutes of waking determines your focus all day.
2. **Takeaways**:
   - **View sunlight**. Evidence: Review of the 2008 study on melanopsin cells. Action: Use a 10,000 lux lamp if the sun isn't out.
3. **Contrarian**: Coffee at 8 AM actually hinders sleep at 10 PM.
4. **Terms**: Adenosine, Cortisol Spike, SCN.

### Output Constraints:
- Sharp and Insightful tone.
- Utility Score: 10/10.
```

Act as an Information Curator. Extract takeaways from our Q4 Strategy sync.
