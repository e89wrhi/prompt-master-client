# 🧑‍🏫 Tutoring & Personas

Transforming the AI into an active participant rather than just a Q&A engine.

## Available Categories

- [**Debate Opponent**](./debate_opponent/index.md): Simulating a devil's advocate to strengthen your arguments.
- [**Language Partner Simulation**](./language_partner_simulation/index.md): Acting as a native speaker who corrects your grammar organically.
- [**Socratic Questioning**](./socratic_questioning/index.md): Guiding the user to the answer *without* giving it directly.
- [**Step-by-Step Math Solving**](./step_by_step_math_solving/index.md): Breaking down calculus or algebra without skipping logic.
