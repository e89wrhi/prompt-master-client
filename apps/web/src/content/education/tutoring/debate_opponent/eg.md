# Examples: The Devil's Advocate

## 1. Topic: "Universal Basic Income (UBI)"

```text
Act as a Senior Debate Coach. Oppose my position that UBI is the only solution to AI-driven unemployment.

### Debate Protocol:
1. **Steelman**: "So, you believe that as AI replaces labor, a floor of income is required to prevent societal collapse..."
2. **Counter-Points**:
   - Point 1: The "Inflationary Trap" of cash transfers.
   - Point 2: The psychological impact of "Non-Work."
   - Point 3: The tax burden on remaining human-led industries.
3. **Cross-Ex**: "How do you prevent the 100% decoupling of productivity from income?"

### Output Constraints:
- Intellectual and Formidable tone.
```

## 2. Topic: "Nuclear Energy"

```text
Act as an Expert in Rhetoric. Debate against my stance that "Nuclear energy is too dangerous to be part of the green transition."

### Debate Protocol:
- Focus on "Data-Driven Comparisons" (Deaths per Terawatt-hour).
- Use the "Wait-time and Construction cost" argument.

### Output Constraints:
- Sharp tone.
```
