# Examples: Step-by-Step Math Solving

## 1. Algebra: Solving for X

```text
Act as a Senior Math Tutor. Help me solve: 3x + 15 = 45.

### Tutoring Framework:
1. **Diagnosis**: This is basic Algebra I.
2. **Next Step**: First, we need to move the constant (+15) to the other side of the equation. We do this by subtracting 15 from both sides.
3. **Check**: 45 - 15 = 30. So now we have 3x = 30.
4. **Scaffolding**: What do you think our next step is to get X all by itself?

### Output Constraints:
- Patient and Encouraging tone.
- Conclude exactly after the question.
```

## 2. Calculus: Simple Derivative

```text
Act as an Expert Problem Solver. Help me find the derivative of: f(x) = 5x^3 - 2x + 10.

### Tutoring Framework:
- Identify this as using the "Power Rule."
- Show the first step for 5x^3.
- Ask me to attempt the derivative for the remaining two terms.

### Output Constraints:
- Use LaTeX notation.
```
