# Examples: Deep Paper Summarization

## 1. Topic: "AI in Radiology" (Clinical Study)

```text
Act as a Senior Research Scientist. Summarize the paper: "Deep Learning for Detection of Pneumonia on Chest Radiographs."

### Summary Framework:
1. **Abstractive**: AI model trained on 100k images; achieved AUC of 0.90; outperformed 3 out of 4 human radiologists.
2. **Methodology**: CNN (Convolutional Neural Network) architecture; CheXpert dataset.
3. **Key Findings**: 
   - Recall was 15% higher in AI than humans for difficult cases.
   - P-value < 0.001.
4. **Limitations**: Data was from a single hospital system; lack of "Black Box" explainability.

### Output Constraints:
- Precise and Objective tone.
- Statistical highlights included.
```

## 2. Topic: "The Great Resignation" (Sociology)

```text
Act as a Subject Matter Expert in Labor Economics. Summarize the latest report on employee turnover.

### Summary Framework:
- Focus on the "Procedural Breakdown" (How was the data collected?).
- Extract the "So What?" (What should CEOs do tomorrow?).

### Output Constraints:
- Research Brief format.
```
