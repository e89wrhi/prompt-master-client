# Examples: Professional Citation Formatting

## 1. Journal Article (APA 7th Edition)

```text
Act as a Citation Specialist. Format the following into APA 7th Edition.

### Source Details:
- Title: The impact of social media on teenage sleep patterns.
- Authors: Sarah J. Miller, Robert D. Smith.
- Journal: Journal of Adolescent Health.
- Vol: 45. Issue: 2. Pages: 110-125.
- Date: 2021.
- DOI: 10.1016/j.jadohealth.2021.05.002.

### Formatting Requirements:
- Full bibliography entry.
- In-text citation for a direct quote.

### Output Constraints:
- Copy-pasteable code block.
```

## 2. YouTube Video (BibTeX for LaTeX)

```text
Act as an Academic Librarian. Format this YouTube video into BibTeX format.

### Source Details:
- Video Title: Why AI is taking over coding.
- Channel: The Tech Reviewer.
- Upload Date: Oct 12, 2023.
- URL: https://youtube.com/watch?v=12345.

### Output Constraints:
- Include the 'note' field for the URL.
```
