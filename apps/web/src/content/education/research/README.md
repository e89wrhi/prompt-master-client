# 🔍 Research & Academia

Prompts for graduate students and researchers to accelerate literature reviews and hypothesis formulation.

## Available Categories

- [**Citation Formatting**](./citation_formatting/index.md): Fixing or generating APA/MLA/Chicago formatted bibliographies.
- [**Hypothesis Refinement**](./hypothesis_refinement/index.md): Taking broad ideas and turning them into testable statements.
- [**Literature Review Outlines**](./literature_review_outlines/index.md): Structuring themes from a collection of imported abstracts.
- [**Paper Summarization**](./paper_summarization/index.md): Extracting methodology, p-values, and conclusions from academic papers.
