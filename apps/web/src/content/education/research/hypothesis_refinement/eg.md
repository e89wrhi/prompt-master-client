# Examples: Scientific Hypothesis Refinement

## 1. Psychology: Sleep and Academic Performance

```text
Act as a Senior Research Scientist. Refine this topic: "Sleep affects how well students do in school."

### Research Context:
- **Observation**: Students who pull all-nighters seem to fail their midterms more often.
- **Population**: Undergraduate engineering students.

### Refinement Requirements:
1. **Hypothesis 1**: If a student sleeps less than 6 hours the night before an exam, their score will be 15% lower than their average.
2. **Variables**: IV: Hours of sleep; DV: Exam score.
3. **Directionality**: Negative (Less sleep = lower score).
4. **Measurement**: Electronic sleep trackers and GPA data.

### Output Constraints:
- Precise and Logical tone.
- Conclude with advice on confounding variables like "Caffeine consumption."
```

## 2. Business: Remote Work and Employee Retention

```text
Act as a Methodology Expert. Refine: "Work-from-home makes people stay at their jobs longer."

### Research Context:
- **Population**: Tech workers in the Bay Area.
- **Observation**: Resignations dropped when we went remote.

### Refinement Requirements:
- Operationalization: "Years of tenure" vs "Ability to work remotely (Y/N)".
- Falsifiability: If tenure is the same for in-office workers, the hypothesis is disproven.

### Output Constraints:
- Scientific tone.
```
