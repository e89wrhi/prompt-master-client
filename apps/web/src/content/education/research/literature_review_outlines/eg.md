# Examples: Literature Review Outlines

## 1. Topic: "The Impact of AI on Entry-Level Coding"

```text
Act as a Senior Researcher. Design a Literature Review Outline for: "AI Adaptation in Junior Software Engineering Roles."

### Research Context:
- **Question**: Are junior roles disappearing or evolving?
- **Logic**: Thematic (Economic, Pedagogical, Technical).

### Outline Requirements:
1. **The Great Debate**: "Displacement vs. Augmentation." Group papers that predict mass layoffs vs papers that predict '10x developers'.
2. **Synthesis Blocks**:
   - Theme 1: Changes in hiring requirements (2022-2024).
   - Theme 2: Instructional changes in Bootcamps.
3. **Knowledge Gap**: Lack of long-term (2+ year) longitudinal studies on junior dev retention in the AI era.

### Output Constraints:
- Formal and Analytical tone.
- Include a Synthesis Matrix suggestion.
```

## 2. Topic: "Mental Health in Remote Work" (Methodological)

```text
Act as an Academic Editor. Outline a review focused on [METHODOLOGY].

### Research Context:
- **Focus**: Comparing Qualitative vs Quantitative findings on WFH burnout.
- **Logic**: Methodological.

### Outline Requirements:
- Group studies that used "Self-Report Surveys" vs those that used "Biometric Data."
- Highlight the gap: Very few studies use "Mixed-Method" approaches for this topic.

### Output Constraints:
- Objective tone.
```
